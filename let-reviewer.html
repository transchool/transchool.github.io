DEFINITION OF HUMAN BODY SYSTEMS

A body system is a group of organs that work together to perform a specific function. The human body has 11 body systems. The systems studied in elementary school are usually the circulatory system, respiratory system, muscular system, digestive system, and nervous system. The other systems are just as important but more complex, so they are studied at higher grade levels. They are the endocrine system, immune system, lymphatic system, reproductive system, skeletal system, and urinary system.

1. Which of the following statements has a very limited definition of educational technology?

a. It is a profession composed of various job categories.

a. Design is the production stage while development is the planning stage.

c. Evaluation is synonymous with implementation.

Answer: D

26. Let Us Review What is tempo in music?​


Answer:

Tempo is the speed of rhythm in music. It is the Italian word for time.

• Composers and musicians have developed a system of verbal descriptions or tempo markings.

• This allows them to quickly and effectively recognize the rate of speed to be followed in a musical piece.

ITALIAN TERMS & MEANINGS

Vivace - Fast and lively
Allegro - Quick and lively
Allegretto - Fairly quick and lively
Adagio - Slow but not as slow as largo
Andante - Walking pace
Andantino - Slightly faster than andante but slower than moderato.
A Tempo - To resume to its original speed. This marking is used after a change of tempo.

Why is tempo important?

- It is important because it helps to create the mood or feeling in music.
- The way we sing, is sometimes based in our mood.
