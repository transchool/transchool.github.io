4. At the parking lot of Signal Village National High School, the 300 meter path to the canteen and the 400 meter path to the New building meet at a right angle. Carlo walks straight from the parking lot to the covered court. How far is Carlo from the canteen?​


Answer: Carlo is 180 meters away from the canteen.

Step-by-step explanation:

Use the Pythagorean Theorem to find the length of the hypotenuse of ΔCAB. Let x be AB.

[tex]300^{2} + 400^{2} = x^{2} \\90,000 + 160,000 = x^{2} \\x^{2} = 250,000\\x= 500[/tex]

The length of AB is 500 meters. Use Corollary 2 of Geometric Mean Theorem to find the length of AD.

[tex]\frac{AB}{CA} = \frac{CA}{AD}[/tex]

Let x be AD.

[tex]\frac{500}{300} = \frac{300}{x} \\\\500x = 90,000\\x = 180[/tex]

The length of AD is 180 meters. Therefore, the distance between the Canteen and Carlo's spot is 180 meters.

30. On a map, the distance between village A and village B measures 6 centimeters and the distance between village A and village C measures 9 centimeters. The actual distance from village A to village B is 20 kilometers. If the distance between two points on a map is directly proportional to the actual distance between the locations, how far is it from village A and village C in kilometers?


Using the idea of ratio and proportion you'll have the distance from A to C as 
[tex] \frac{AC}{9cm} = \frac{20km}{6cm} [/tex]
[tex]AC = \frac{(20km)(9cm)}{6cm} [/tex]
cm will cancel out
AC = 30km
