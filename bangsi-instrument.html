Answer: Aerophone

Bangsi is bamboo flute instrument which means it is a wood wind instrument/ Aerophone instruments

For more definition:

The Hornbostel sachs system divides all musical instruments into five categories: idiophones, membranophones, chordophones, aerophones, and electrophones.
Idiophones: Instruments which produce sound by vibrating themselves.
Membranophones: Instruments which produce sound by a vibrating membrane.
Chordophones: Instruments which produce sound by vibrating strings.
Aerophone: Is any musical instrument that produces sound primarily by causing a body of air to vibrate.
Electrophones: A class of musical instruments in which the initial sound either is produced by electronic means or is conventionally produced as by a vibrating string and electronically amplified.
