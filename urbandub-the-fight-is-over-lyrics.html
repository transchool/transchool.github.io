1/2 x + 5/6 = (3/6)x + 5/6 = (1/2)x + 5/6

(2/3)x - 1/6 = (1/2)x + 5/6

(2/3)x - (1/2)x - 1/6 = 5/6

(4/6)x - (3/6)x - 1/6 = 5/6

(1/6)x - 1/6 = 5/6
