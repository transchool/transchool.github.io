2. describe the location of mount ragang​


Answer:

Ragang is an active stratovolcano 40 km SE of Marawi city, Mindanao Island, Philippines. It is Mindanao's most active volcanoes and belongs to the Central Mindanao Arc. Most of its historic eruptions were small explosive eruptions from the summit crater.

30. use online resources to find a location and classification of the following volcanoes volcano mount pinatubo mount hood kilanea paricutin mount cotopaxi mount saint helens mount ranier mauna loa mount fuji mount shasta mount etna location feature type of volcano​


Answer:

1. Mount Pinatubo - Location: Luzon, Philippines. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

2. Mount Hood - Location: Oregon, United States. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

3. Kilauea - Location: Hawaii, United States. Feature type: Shield volcano.

4. Paricutin - Location: Michoacán, Mexico. Feature type: Cinder cone volcano.

5. Cotopaxi - Location: Andes, Ecuador. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

6. Mount St. Helens - Location: Washington, United States. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

7. Mount Rainier - Location: Washington, United States. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

8. Mauna Loa - Location: Hawaii, United States. Feature type: Shield volcano.

9. Mount Fuji - Location: Honshu, Japan. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

10. Mount Shasta - Location: California, United States. Feature type: Stratovolcano.

11. Mount Etna - Location: Sicily, Italy. Feature type: Stratovolcano.
