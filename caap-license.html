17. ano po yung CAAP ? thanks po


Civil Aviation Authority of the Philippines

Ang CAAP ay responsable sa pagpapatupad ng mga patakaran sa aviation ng sibil upang matiyak ang ligtas, pangkabuhayan, at mahusay na paglalakbay sa himpapawid. Bilang isang independiyenteng regulatory body na may quasi-judicial at quasi-legislative powers, ang CAAP ay inatasan na magtakda ng komprehensibo, malinaw at walang kinikilingan na mga patakaran at regulasyon para sa industriya ng aviation ng Pilipinas.

Pinuno: Col. Ramon Gutierrez (OIC)

Professional Misconduct

Professional misconduct refers to behaviors taken by those with a license that show a lack of capacity to uphold professional standards and competently carry out their responsibilities as licensed professionals. A few examples of professional misconduct are as follows: committing multiple instances of negligence or incompetence, as well as acts of egregious ineptitude on one occasion. Enabling or assisting someone who isn't licensed to carry out tasks that call for a license. The following behaviors could qualify as professional misconduct:

Abhorrent overchargingCompeting interestsActing against directionsDishonest or deceptive behavior inside or outside of courtStealing from trust funds

Professional misconduct can also refer to behavior that occurs outside the realm of the practice of law, such as being found guilty of a serious crime, a tax offense, or a dishonesty-related offense, or being barred from managing or participating in the management of a corporation under the Corporations Act.
