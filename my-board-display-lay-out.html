9. Differentiate Formal Dining Table Lay-out from Informal Dining table Lay-out?​


Answer:

An informal setting has minimal tableware and is used for up to a three-course meal while a meal of six courses calls for a formal setting. In an informal setting, all the flatwear is placed on the table at the same time while in a formal setting, some pieces may be cleared and others added.

Answer:

There will be fewer tools and serving dishes on the table for informal table settings. In most cases, the principle or manager will establish the casual table settings. 17. make a re.search about power lay-out,ligthing lay-out​


Answer:

Power Layout:

Power layout is the design of electrical wiring systems in a building, which includes the placement of power outlets, switches, and other components. Power layout is an important component of a building’s electrical system as it ensures that the wiring is done correctly and safely. The layout should be designed with the intent of minimizing risk of electric shock, fire, and other hazards. It should also take into account the aesthetic and ergonomic needs of the building owners and users.

Lighting Layout:

Lighting layout is the design of a building's lighting system, which includes the placement of lights, fixtures, and other components. Lighting layout is an important component of a building’s lighting system as it ensures that the lighting is done correctly and safely while also providing the necessary illumination and atmosphere. The layout should be designed with the intent of maximizing energy efficiency, reducing light pollution, and providing the desired lighting effects. It should also be designed with the aesthetic and ergonomic needs of the building owners and users in mind.
