1. 1. Ano ang layunin ng Special Program for Employment of Students o SPES?​


Spes

Ang Spes o Special Program for Employment of Students ay taunang programa ng DOLE na ang layunin ay bigyan ng pansamantalang trabaho ang mga kabataan na mahihirap, out of school youth, at mga na-displaced sa trabaho upang makapagtrabaho sila sa panahon ng summer o Christmas vacation o kahit kailan dahil layunin nitong makatulong na kumita naman sila at magkaroon ng benepisyo na makatutulong sa kanilang pamilya lalo na sa kanilang pag-aaral.

Ang Spes ay mandated sa ilalim ng Republic Act No. 9547, kung saan ang kalihim ng labor and employment ang gumaganap bilang Direktor nito.

Maganda ang programa ito dahil bukod sa perang kikitain ay nahihikayat din ang mga mag-aaral na gawing produktibo ang kanilang bakasyon sa pamamagitan ng pagtatrabaho tuwing summer.

Ang programang ito na pinangunahan ng Department of Labor and Employment (DOLE), katuwang ang Department of Education (DepEd) ay ipatutupad sa DepEd Central Office lamang.

Mas mainam din na ang mga aplikante ay may basic knowledge sa paggamit ng kompyuter at mga software na tulad ng Microsoft word at Microsoft excel. Madalas kasi ng trabaho na ibinibigay ay encoding, pag-aayos ng files, pagsagot ng tawag, at ibang pang trabahong pang-computer at office.

Mga kwalipikasyon:

dapat ay hindi bababa sa anyos at hindi lalagpas sa 30 anyos ang edad ng out of school youth

dapat ay pasado ang nakuha na mag-aaral general weighted average (GWA) ng mag-aaral

ang kanya pati ang combined net income ng magulang ng mag-aaral ay hindi lalagpas sa poverty threshold sa kanilang rehiyon

ang out of school youth ay dapat na may certification mula sa local Social Welfare and Development Office (SWDO) na sinasabing siya ay OSY o out of school youth

Ang mga kwalipikadong aplikante ay makapagtatrabaho sa loob ng hindi bababa sa 20 hanggang sa maximum na 52 araw. Ang matatanggap nilang sahod ay minimum wage.

7. After reading the article about gaudium et spes what important learning, insight, and reflection you have in mind?​


GAUDIUM ET SPES

It is challenging to keep up with the repercussions of rapid economic, technological, and political development, according to Gaudium et Spes. While many individuals have come to believe in God as a result of these events, others have abandoned it.

The human race is currently a part of a new historical phase. Around the entire planet, profound and quick changes are occurring.

These changes, which are the result of man's knowledge and creative powers, have a reversing effect on him, his decisions and wants, both individually and collectively, as well as his way of thinking and behaving toward things and others.

Thus, we can already refer to a genuine cultural and social change that also affects a person's religious beliefs.

The expansive document Gaudium et Spes (Joy and Hope), issued during the Second Vatican Council in 1965, is widely hailed as a turning point in Catholic social teaching. It focuses on the role of the Church and its adherents in the world in areas like politics, peace, and the difficulties presented by modern atheism.

Conscience "holds us in obedience"—it "summons" us, according to Gaudium et Spes. True, the word "freedom" is often used in conjunction with the concept of conscience, but this is not a license to do whatever we like. Instead, the plea for freedom of conscience seeks to prevent restrictions on how we can follow our conscience.

The paper summarizes the Catholic Church's teachings on man's place in society, with particular emphasis on economics, poverty, social justice, culture, science, and ecumenism.

Gaudium et Spes is fairly unusual in terms of the church's general councils and history. The decree marks the first time the church, with its members present in such large numbers from all over the world, attempted to delve deeply into such a broad variety of concerns impacting people's daily lives.
