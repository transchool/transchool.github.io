4. What are the things that need to be changed in the landscape of Philippine Politics?


Answer:

1. Strengthen the Anti-Corruption Campaign: The Philippines needs to strengthen its anti-corruption campaigns and initiatives in order to curb corruption in politics. This could be done by providing more oversight and transparency in government processes, offering better training and resources to law enforcement, and implementing tougher penalties for those guilty of corruption.

2. Improve Public Services: The Philippines should also prioritize improving public services such as healthcare, education, and transportation. This could be done by increasing government spending in these areas and creating better infrastructure.

3. Increase Political Participation: The Philippines should also increase political participation by encouraging more citizens to register and vote in elections. This could be done by making the registration process simpler and easier, and by providing more resources to educate citizens on the importance of voting.

4. Strengthen the Rule of Law: The Philippines needs to strengthen the rule of law by improving the legal system and increasing access to justice for all citizens. This could be done by providing better training for judges and lawyers, and by providing more resources for victims of injustice.

5. Combat Poverty: The Philippines should also take steps to combat poverty by creating more jobs, increasing wages, and providing assistance to those in need. This could be done by providing better economic opportunities, increasing access to financial services, and creating better social safety nets.

7. what are the basic principles to be followed while designing your landscapes​
