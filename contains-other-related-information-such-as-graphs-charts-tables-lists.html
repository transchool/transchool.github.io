Research Paper

It is an essay which explains one's learning after exploring a topic in-depth. It includes information from various sources. Basically, a research paper has ten major parts. The purpose of which is to bring together different views, evidence, and facts about a topic from books, articles, and interviews. It interprets information gathered into own writing.

The appendix contains other related information such as graphs, charts, tables, and the list of figures.

The conclusion provides the summary of the research.

Research is a systematic investigation to contribute to an existing body of knowledge.

Reference is a list of the sources in the research.

The results present the information gathered through the research.

The literature review provides a review of what others have written or researched on concerning the topic.

The methodology explains how the research was conducted.

The introduction presents background information, scope, and focus of the research paper.

Abstract summarizes all sections and helps readers decide whether or not to read the entire report.

The discussion evaluates the results of the study or research.
