1. Three boys sold roses in the ration of 2:3:4. Together they sold 225 roses. How many roses did each boy sell?​solution po plssssssss


Answer:

The total ratio of the boys is 2+3+4 = 9

The number of roses each boy sold is 225/9 = <<225/9=25>>25. Answer: 25.

Step-by-step explanation:

Answer:

Thus, each boy sold 50 roses, 75 roses, and 100 roses respectively.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, find the sum of the ratio

2:3:4 = 2 + 3 + 4 = 9

divide 225 by 9

225/9 = 25

next is multiply 9 by the ratio to get the number of roses sold by each boy

2 x 25 = 50 roses

3 x 25 = 75 roses

4 x 25 = 100 roses

check: 50 + 75 + 100 = 225

Thus, each boy sold 50 roses, 75 roses, and 100 roses respectively

15. Can we change the color of a colored flower (e.g. red rose into blue rose) using the transpiration method? Why or Why not?


Answer:

You can change a flowers color by altering the pH levels of its plant cells. By changing the soil to be more acidic or more alkaline, you can actually change the color of the flower itself. Hydrangeas are the only flowers that react with changes in soil conditions.
