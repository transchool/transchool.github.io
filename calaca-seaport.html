Answer:

As nouns the difference between seaport and airport is that seaport is a town or harbour with facilities for seagoing ships to dock and take on or discharge cargo while airport is an airfield (a place where airplanes can take off and land), including one or more runways and one or more passenger terminals.

3. Pharagrap about the old Seaport in Brgy. Mambulac​


Answer:

The local people in Barangay (Village) Mambulac in Silay City of the province of Negros Occidental call the old seaport as "Pantalan."

The Pantalan is more than 1.7 kilometers long and was the longest wharf and one of the busiest in Asia before it was bombed during World War II.

The village council is scouting for investors and funding for the Pantalan's redevelopment into making it into a boardwalk so the local people and their visitors can enjoy and relax amid a variety of activities and its magnificent sunset!
