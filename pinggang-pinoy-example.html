1. explain what is pinggang pinoy


Answer:

Pinggang Pinoy is a new and easy-to-understand food guide that uses a familiar food plate model to convey the right food group proportions on a per-meal basis.

Answer:

Pinggang pinoy is a tool intended to guide planning a healthy meal per daily basis. The guideline is in proportion with the current nutrition guidelines at different age group. The pinggang pinoy includes the three food groups namely Go, Grow, and Glow. Each food groups contains different foods in accordance to the nutrients it provide and the right amount and daily consume of the foods. The pinggang pinoy has a feature of plate like that contains the right proportion of food with right content of nutrients that an average Filipino need. The purpose of this pinggang pinoy is to help Filipino people develop healthy eating habits.

13. what are the benefits of pinggang pinoy give at least 5 benefits​


Explanation:

Promotes healthy eating habits: The Pinggang Pinoy program encourages the consumption of locally-sourced, nutrient-dense foods such as fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins. This can help improve overall diet quality and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as obesity and diabetes.

Supports local agriculture: By promoting the consumption of locally-sourced foods, Pinggang Pinoy helps to support the livelihoods of local farmers and fisherfolk. This can have a positive impact on the local economy and help to preserve traditional food culture.

Encourages cultural preservation: Pinggang Pinoy promotes the traditional Filipino diet and food culture. This can help preserve the country's culinary heritage and promote a sense of national identity.

Reduces food waste: By promoting the consumption of locally-sourced foods, Pinggang Pinoy can help to reduce food waste by reducing the need for imported foods. This can help to conserve resources and reduce the environmental impact of food production and transportation.

Improves food security: By supporting local agriculture and promoting the consumption of locally-sourced foods, Pinggang Pinoy can help to improve food security in the Philippines. This can help to ensure that all Filipinos have access to nutritious and affordable food.
