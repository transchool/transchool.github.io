12. what is academia in food safety management​


ISO 22000 is academia in food safety management.

Detail of Explanation

Food is a product that has health consequences for consumers. Therefore, food companies and organizations involved in them are obliged to ensure the safety of the food supply chain. They must also take steps to ensure the product is safe for consumption. Food safety management system is a process that includes raw material selection, processing, production, packaging, and storage. In addition, food safety management also includes distribution and consumption.

The Food Safety Management System is a training program that aims to develop competencies related to the food safety management system in an organization.

The progress of the times is also accompanied by the development of the food industry which is increasing rapidly. The more varied types of food consumed by humans trigger the increasing problems caused by food. It is undeniable that food safety is associated with the dangers of food origin. Therefore, it is appropriate that an organization engaged in the food industry needs to demonstrate its ability to control food safety hazards in order to ensure that the food it produces is safe when consumed by humans.

Food Safety Management with ISO 22000

ISO 22000 states the requirements for a food safety management system. And decide the requirements an organization must fill. That is to indicate that the management system can control food safety hazards. Unsafe food can have serious health consequences. So it is most important for organizations to make sure their processes and products are safe.

Today, many food products cross national borders, highlighting the need for global standards for food safety management. ISO 22000 meets this need by providing guidelines that organizations can follow to help identify and control hazards associated with food safety.
