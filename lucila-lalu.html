Bingo is a game of chance in which each player matches numbers printed in different arrangements on cards with the numbers the game host (caller) draws at random, marking the selected numbers with tiles. 21. Lucila had 3/5 of a pizza she shared 2/5 with her friend. What partof pizza is left?


Subtraction of Similar Fractions:

Answer:

1/5

Solutions:

1. Identify the given.

3/5 - Lucila's pizza

2/5 - part which Lucila shared with her friend

2. Know what is asked.

part of the pizza that was left to Lucila

3. Write the equation.

n = 3/5 - 2/5

4. Solve the equation.

n = 3/5 - 2/5

n = 1/5

Therefore, 1/5 of the pizza that was left to Lucila.

Remember:

In subtracting similar fractions, find the difference between the numerator then copy the denominator.

Examples:

2/3 - 1/3 = 2 - 1/3 = 1/3

4/5 - 2/5 = 4- 2/5 = 2/5

8/10 - 3/10 = 8 - 3/10 = 5/10 or 1/2

Dissimilar Fractions: https://brainly.ph/question/234202

There is 1/5 of pizza that’s left.

Further explanation:

Fraction is usually used as a representation of a part from a whole object. There are 3 types of fractions namely:

1. Proper fraction – Is when the numerator is smaller than the denominator.

2. Improper fraction – Is when the numerator is greater than or equal to the denominator.

3. Mixed fraction – Is when proper fraction is combined with a whole number.  

Solution:  

Since both the denominator of the given fractions are similar, we can now proceed to the subtraction of the numerators.  

3/5 – 2/5      Subtract the numerators and retain the denominator.  

    1/5       Leftover pizza of Lucila.
