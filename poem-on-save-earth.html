12. saving the earth: cooling down our world! poem english​


POEM OF "COOLING DOWN OUR WORLD"

TITLE: Green the Earth, Until Death

If the earth is green, disaster is ashamed to come

If the ocean is blue, coral reefs smile charmingly

Well, that's my earth

No pollutant

Relatives enjoy life

No bad smell and stagnant waste

Black smoke never seen

Evidence of human charity to nature

Come on friends, green the earth

Strong energy and a hunk of spirit

Until the last breath blows

For the sake of grandchildren

And half hope

Come on friends, blue the sea

So that the mind does not drift

Leave fishing explosives

For the sake of the beloved ecosystem

Save our earth!

EXPLANATION:

Looking at the current state of the earth with global warming that continues to increase, the amount of plastic scattered on land and oceans whose production and consumption can no longer be stopped, as well as clean water sources that are starting to become scarce, of course these events are the impact of human activities on earth so far. Who pay less attention to the environment? Daily human activities that directly or indirectly can trigger global warming and other natural disasters. Therefore, as living beings who inhabit the earth, we are also obliged to be able to take care of our aging earth. There are many ways that our generation can do to prevent our earth from being damaged by changing our daily habits.

14. short poem about how you will help save planet earth​


Outside my bedroom window

Stands an old majestic tree.

She's been standing there for decades,

Just as proud as she can be.

And every morning when I wake,

She's the first thing I see.

This lovely constant in my life,

So magnificent and free.

I can see the early, rising sun

Peeking through her lush green leaves.

I can hear her moving gently,

In the summer's warming breeze.

She has a personality

That she wants the world to see.

And her moods are ever changing

Behind her silent dignity.

She responds to every season,

In her own unfettered way.

All we have to do is gaze at her

To know what season's on its way.

In the Spring she comes alive again,

As her leaves begin to bloom.

And beneath the sun's attention,

You can almost see her swoon.

And in the heat of the summertime,

When her joy is magnified.

She has never looked more beautiful,

All "decked out to the nines."

And when the Fall comes creeping in,

And a chill floats on the breeze,

She prefers to don more earthy tones,

And does just what she please.

But when the Winter's looming large,

It's impossible to hide

As her barren limbs droop soulfully.

They betray her grief inside.

She's stood by me through all my days.

She's watched me laugh and cry.

She's listened so intently

While I've pondered on my life.

And when I see her dank and weary,

I will sit with her sometimes.

And all the years we've shared together

Will come rushing back to mind.

But both of us are strong and true.

We've become the closest friends,

And we will weather every storm

Together till the end
