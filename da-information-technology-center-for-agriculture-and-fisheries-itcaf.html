7. What is the importance/contribution of agriculture and fisheries​


Answer:

The agriculture and fisheries sector provides food and vital raw materials for the rest of the economy. It is itself a significant market for the products and services of the non- agricultural economy. As the sector grows and modernizes, it releases surplus labor to the industry and services sectors. Sustainable, productive fisheries and aquaculture improve food and nutrition security, increase income and improve livelihoods, promote economic growth and protect our environment and natural resources.
