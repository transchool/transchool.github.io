24. kailan nag simula ang dagami revolt ​


Answer:

The Dagami revolt (Spanish: La Revuelto del Dagami) was a revolt against Spanish colonial rule in the Philippines. It was led by Chief Dagami from Gabi (now part of Palo). The revolt actually began in 1565, but is sometimes dated as 1567, the year of Dagami's execution.

Answer:

Tandaya

Explanation:

Leyte, known as Tandaya to early Filipinos, was renamed Filipina by Roy Lopez de Villalobos in 1543, a name that later extended to the entire archipelago. Four centuries later, in 1944, the Battle of Leyte Gulf, the biggest naval battle between the Americans and the Japanese in world history, transpired in Leyte.
