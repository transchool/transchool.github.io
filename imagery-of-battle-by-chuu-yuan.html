The emotions that I feel after reading the poem

For the first poem, I found it sad that two different clans had to clash and appreciate each other's differences in terms of their values and ideals rather than fighting for their own perspective. They shouldn't have engaged in a brutal conflict, in my opinion, as it did them no favor and only resulted in the deaths of their own citizens. With regard to the second poem, I was pleased to learn that people had already recognized the value of their fields and were appreciative of the prosperity their land and other blessings—including crops like rice and wheat—had brought to their life. I had the impression that life before was superior to life now.

Comparison of the two poems

The similarities and differences between them will be compared. Because of the parallels, I think they both deal with nationalism because they displayed commitment and devotion. Additionally, I believe that both poems made use of sight or visual imagery. Finally, as they both tell a story, I think both can be categorized as narrative poetry. The difference is that the other poetry is about the basic existence that is oriented toward agriculture, however, in the battle, the main subject matter is patriotism because it is about a struggle between two clans where their loyalty to their homeland was evident. On the Threshing Floor, I Chase Chickens Away uses audio imagery in addition to visual imagery when creating its images. Last but not least, I think that based on their styles, the first poem can be classified as a ballad since it is an oral narrative song, and the second can be classified as a soliloquy because the character conveyed his thoughts and feelings.

The message each poem conveys

The first poem, Battle by Chu'ü Yüan, emphasizes peace more than battle, which wasn't represented in the poem because it was about a conflict between two clans. It sort of implies that we shouldn't make the same mistakes or follow the same pattern as they did, which led them to resort to war. The author seems to favor promoting togetherness despite the fact that different clans or groups may hold different philosophies. We should learn to value diversity in order to improve interactions between individuals, regardless of how dissimilar they may be.

The poem On the Threshing Floor, I Chase Chickens Away by Yu Xiuhua, on the other hand, conveys a message about the value of time. It was emphasized in the poem that time doesn't always remain the same and that is why we should pursue whatever we want to, as long as it is right. The poem also showcased that God will always shower us with His blessings and that it is our responsibility to be thankful for the prosperity that we experience.

11. What are the difference and similarities between battle by chu u yuan and on the threshing floor, i chase the chicken away by yu xiuhua​


THE DIFFERENCE AND SIMILARITIES ASPECTS

The distinction between the two is that the Battle of Chu u yuan focuses on more than just grief and despair, whereas the threshing floor I chased away focuses more on an ordinary, realistic circumstance.

They both experience life with sorrow, anguish, pain, misery, and uncertainty.

Other details will be explained in the picture below.

CHU U YUAN

The poem Battle in Chinese literature was composed by Chu U Yuan, author of the classic poetry "Li Sao" or "Falling into Trouble" (332–295 B.C. ). Finding that he could not alter his prince's behavior, he committed suicide by drowning in the river Mi-lo. Supposedly, the present Dragon Boat Festival is held in his honor.

The subject of Chu U Yuan's poem "Battle" is war. He emphasizes the two clans fighting for their beliefs with their lives on the line.

YU XIUHUA

Yu Xiuhua's poem "On the Rice Thresh1ng Ground, I Chase Chickens Away"

"On the Rice Thresh1ng Ground, I Chase Chickens Away" is a common title for her poems on her daily life. She lives her life with a poetic mind and a detached view of her immediate environment. She can only type with her left index finger, as suggested by the title of her novel, "Moon Falls on the Left Hand," therefore she may not write without the internet. But the poet, not the poet, is disabled. Or she may be physically but not mentally impaired. There are numerous contradictory opinions regarding her writing among Chinese poets. At the intersection of three thousand years of indigenous traditions and one hundred years of foreign influence, there is considerable uncertainty about what constitutes good poetry.

For decades, the ethnic minority has been one of the most contentious subjects in China's political and cultural life. Numerous individuals have lost their native tongues but continue to write in Chinese. Whether chants, epics, or ballads, ethnic traditional poetry has different traditions. Most raw beauty is lost due to the "contamination" of secondhand modernism. However, many surprisingly excellent things can be discovered in the age of the internet.

14. 1. Ilarawan ang limang sangay ng pamahalaan ng Taiwan. 1. Executive Yuan 2. Legislative Yuan 3. Judicial Yuan 4. Examination Yuan 5. Control Yuan


English

The Executive Yuan is the executive branch of the government of the Republic of China (Taiwan). Its leader is the Premier, who is appointed by the President of the Republic of China.

The Legislative Yuan is the unicameral legislature of the Republic of China (Taiwan). Located in Taipei, the Legislative Yuan is composed of 113 legislators, which are directly elected by people of the Free Area through a parallel voting system for 4-year terms.

The Judicial Yuan is the judicial branch of the government of the Republic of China on Taiwan.It runs a Constitutional Court and oversees all systems of courts in Taiwan, including ordinary courts like the supreme court, high courts, district courts as well as special courts like administrative courts and disciplinary courts.

The Examination Yuan is an independent civil service commission in charge of validating the qualification of civil servants in the Republic of China (Taiwan).

The Control Yuan is an independent investigatory and auditory agency of the government of the Republic of China on Taiwan.Designed as a hybrid of auditor and ombudsman by Taiwanese law, the Control Yuan holds the following powers:

Impeachment — The Control Yuan has the power to impeach government officials. Successfully impeached cases then go to the Disciplinary Court of the Judicial Yuan for adjudication. Impeachment of the President and the Vice President of the Republic follows a different procedure and does not go through the Control Yuan.

Censure — The Control Yuan also has the power to censure a government official. The censure is sent to the official's superior officer.

Audit — The Executive Yuan (cabinet) presents the annual budget to the Control Yuan each year for audit.

Tagalog

Ang Executive Yuan ay ang sangay ng ehekutibo ng gobyerno ng Republika ng Tsina (Taiwan). Ang pinuno nito ay ang Premier, na hinirang ng Pangulo ng Republika ng Tsina

Ang Lehislatibong Yuan ay ang pambatasan na unicameral ng Republika ng Tsina (Taiwan). Matatagpuan sa Taipei, ang Batasan Yuan ay binubuo ng 113 mambabatas, na direktang inihalal ng mga tao ng Libreng Lugar sa pamamagitan ng isang parallel na sistema ng pagboto para sa 4 na taong termino.

Ang Judicial Yuan ay ang hudisyal na sangay ng gobyerno ng Republika ng Tsina sa Taiwan. Nagpapatakbo ito ng isang Constitutional Court at pinangangasiwaan ang lahat ng mga sistema ng mga korte sa Taiwan, kabilang ang mga ordinaryong korte tulad ng kataas-taasang hukuman, mataas na korte, mga korte ng distrito pati na rin ang mga espesyal na korte tulad ng mga korte administratibo at korte ng disiplina.

Ang Examination Yuan ay isang independiyenteng komisyon sa serbisyo sibil na namumuno sa pagpapatunay ng kwalipikasyon ng mga tagapaglingkod sibil sa Republika ng Tsina (Taiwan).

Ang Control Yuan ay isang malayang pagsisiyasat at pandinig na ahensya ng gobyerno ng Republika ng Tsina sa Taiwan. Idinisenyo bilang isang hybrid ng auditor at ombudsman ng batas ng Taiwan, ang Control Yuan ay nagtataglay ng mga sumusunod na kapangyarihan:

Impeachment - Ang Control Yuan ay may kapangyarihang i-impeach ang mga opisyal ng gobyerno. Matagumpay na na-impeach ang mga kaso pagkatapos ay pumunta sa Disciplinary Court ng Judicial Yuan para sa paghatol. Ang impeachment ng Pangulo at ang Bise Presidente ng Republika ay sumusunod sa ibang pamamaraan at hindi dumaan sa Control Yuan.

Censure - Ang Control Yuan ay may kapangyarihan ring bastusin ang isang opisyal ng gobyerno. Ang censure ay ipinadala sa superior opisyal ng opisyal.

Audit - Ang Executive Yuan (gabinete) ay nagpapakita ng taunang badyet sa Control Yuan bawat taon para sa pag-audit.
