D. Tutularan siya

Para sa akin ang tamang sagot ay letrang D dahil kung hinahangaan mo ang isang artista ay dapat na tularan mo ang magaganda niyang katangian.

Mali ang letrang A dahil ang Diyos lang dapat ang ating sinasamba at hindi ang katulad nating tao lamang at imperpekto.

22. which is the chord on circle O​


Answer:

The chord of a circle is a line segment joining any two points on the circle. The chord of a circle which passes through the centre of the circle is called the diameter of the circle. The diameter of a circle is the distance across a circle. The diameter is also the longest chord of a circle.

29. which is the longest chord of circle O?

Step-by-step explanation:

Properties of a Chord

The diameter is the longest chord of a circle, whereby the perpendicular distance from the center of the circle to the chord is zero. Two radii joining the ends of a chord to the center of a circle form an isosceles triangle.
