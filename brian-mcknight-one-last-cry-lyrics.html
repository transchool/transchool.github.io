Brian: x

Robin: y

Sum of their ages:

y = 45 - x

Therefore:

Robin's present age, y = 45-x.

Brian: x - 7

Equation:

38 - x = (x-7)/2 + 16

(2) [38 - x = (x-7)/2 + 16] (2)

76 - 2x = x - 7 + 32

x + 2x = 76 - 25

3x/3 =  51/3

x = 17
