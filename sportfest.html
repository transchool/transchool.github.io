3. Bilang isang kabataan paano makakatulong ang sportfest


Answer:

Ang sportfest ay isang pamamaraan o avenue na ginagamit para maipabuti ang mga kabuuang holistikal na kondisyon na importante lalo na para sa kabataan.

Explanation:

Ang holistikal na aspekto ng pagiging malusog ay nakabatay sa mga aspektong ito:

1. Pisikal - pinabubuti ng sportsfest ang kakayahan ng bata sa mga isport na kaniyang sinasalihan. Tinutulungan rin nito ang kabuuang kondisyon ng bata sa kaniyang katawan para maiwasan ang sakit.

2. Emosyonal - tinutulungan ng sportsfest ang ating confidence at self-esteem habang patuloy tayong naging magaling sa mga bagay na ginagawa mo.

3. Mental - sa pamamaraan ng sportsfest nabibigyan ang kabataan ng oportunidad na maglaro sa mga isport na gusto nila, at dito nila mapakawalan ang kanilang stress, depression, anxiety, at iba pang mga problema na maaaring makapag-aapekto sa kanila.

4. Sosyal - sa sportsfest, maraming makikilala ang isang tao na maaaring magiging bagong kaibigan niya na nakapagpalaki sa tinatawag na kaniyang social circle.

10. Write One paragraph essay /story about your experient during the recently held Sportfest 2023. Write it in a short bondpaper.


SPORTFEST 2023

It was a beautiful day as the community gathered for the annual Sportfest. Everyone was excited to participate in the various sports activities and games that were lined up for the day.

As a newcomer to the community, I was thrilled to join in the festivities. I decided to participate in the fun run and volleyball games. The fun run was a 5-kilometer race around the neighborhood. I was nervous as I have never run this distance before, but the cheering crowd and the lively music kept me motivated. I crossed the finish line with a sense of accomplishment, feeling proud of myself for finishing the race.

Next up was the volleyball game. Our team was composed of players of different ages and skill levels, but we were determined to do our best. It was a close match, with each team scoring back and forth. As the final minutes approached, we were down by a few points. However, we didn't give up and rallied to win the game.

After the games, we celebrated our victories with a community feast. Everyone shared their stories and experiences from the day, and we all felt a sense of camaraderie and unity. It was a great opportunity to bond with the people in the community and make new friends.

Overall, the Sportfest was an unforgettable experience. It was a reminder that sports and games can bring people together and create a sense of belongingness. I'm already looking forward to next year's event and can't wait to see what new challenges and adventures await.
