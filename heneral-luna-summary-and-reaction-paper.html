It is widely regarded as a masterpiece of Philippine art and was a major award winner at the 1884 Madrid Exposition. Heneral Luna (lit. 'General Luna') is a 2015 Filipino historical biopic film depicting General Antonio Luna's leadership of the Philippine Revolutionary Army during the Philippine–American War. Directed by Jerrold Tarog and produced by Artikulo Uno Productions, the film received critical acclaim from critics, praising its cinematography, writing, acting and plot.
