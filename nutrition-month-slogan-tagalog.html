SLOGAN NUTRITION MONTH 2017

Ang slogan ay isang kasabihan o parirala na tumatalakay sa isang paksa upang manghikayat o magbigay ng maikling kaalaman na gigising sa mga tao. Kadalasan, itinatampok ng mga slogan ang tungkol sa nutrition o kalusugan ng tao.

Narito ang mga halimbawa ng slogan na tumatalakay sa nutrition month:

"Malnutrisyon, Ating Iwasan. Mabuting Kalusugan, ang Susi sa Kaligayahan!" "Maingat na Pagkain, Ating Gawin! " "Pagkain ng Prutas at Gulay, Tutulong Makamit ang Mahabang Buhay!" "Wastong Nutrisyon, Ating Gawin Ngayon!" "Kumain ng gulay at prutas para sa magandang bukas"  "Tamang pagkain ang solusyon sa malnutrison"  "Nutrisyon ang tulay sa magandang buhay" "Kalusugan ang tunay na kayamanan!"  "Halina at sumama sa pagkain ng tama"

13. Slogan for theme of 2017 nutrition month


 “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit - For Life!”, is the theme, quote, or nutrition month slogan 2017  of our nation-wide nutrition awareness month.

This theme of 43rd nutrition month of the country aims to promote healthy diet among Filipinos as well as spread health awareness in the whole archipelago. (If you’re looking for slogan for nutrition month English or slogan about nutrition 2017 in English, this answer might help.)

July 1 to July 31, 2017 is the annual campaign for Philippine’s Nutrition Month pursuant to the Presidential Decree No. 491 - Nutrition Act of the Philippines organized and implemented by the National Nutrition Council (NNC).

With the theme “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit – for Life! aims to promote healthy diets that would contribute to decrease the incidences of overweight, obesity and non-communicable diseases; to teach the Filipinos how to better distinguish between the healthy foods and unhealthy foods for better nourishment choices; and to encourage the  country’s food industries to produce, make, sell, and offer healthier food options to the consumers. The food industry includes farmers, producers, distributors, and food establishments.

These changes also include reduced access to whole foods including fruits and vegetables. Thus, there is an increasing number of Filipinos not consuming healthy diets.

Based on the survey conducted during 2013 of National Nutrition Survey of FNRI-DOST, the population of Filipino households with diets that met energy requirement is only 31.70%.

1. A healthy diet, a healthy lifestyle is a foundation of good health.

There is growing population of prevalence of overweight / obesity among adults. This actually translates to about eighteen (18) million Filipinos.

26. tagalog slogan for this nutrition month based on this theme " Tembang Iwasto sa Tamang Nutriton at ehersisyo"


Ang islogan ay isang kasabihan o motto ng isang kompanya o ng mga aktibista na madali maalaala. Ito ay nagsisimula sa isang pandiwa o salitang kilos at binubuo ng pito (7 ) hanggang 10 salita. Ito ay kabilang sa mga patimpalak na ginaganap tuwing may selebrasyon sa isang paaralan na may kaugnayan na tema. Halimbawa ng buwanang selebrasyon ay ang Nutrition Month tuwing Hulyo. Ngayong taon, ito ay may temang " Timbang Iwasto sa Tamang Nutrisyon at Ehersisyo". Basi sa temang ito maari kang makaggawa ng isa pang islogan o slogan sa wikang Ingles.
