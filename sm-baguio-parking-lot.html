1. B] Solve the following problems. Show complete solution. #1) The fee to park in SM Baguio cost 50 pesos for the first 3 hours and an extra 15 pesos for each hour or a fraction of it. If you park more than 12 hours, you will pay an exact rate of 200 pesos. a) Represent your parking fee using function f(t) where t is the number of hours you parked in SM Baguio. b) How much will you pay if you park your car from 8AM to 6:45PM. (10pts) Pahelp


Answers with step-by-step explanation:

a.) 50, t ≤ 3

f(t) = { 50 + 15t, 3 < t < 12

200, t ≥ 12

This is an example of a piecewise function. For the first part, we put f(t) = 50 when t ≤ 3 since you will pay 50 pesos for the first 3 hours of parking fee. For the next part, we put f(t) = 50 + 15t when t is more than 3 hours but less than 12 hours. For the last part of the piecewise function, we put f(t) = 200 when t is equal to or more than 12 hours of parking. So whatever will be the parking fee for the time spent parking in SM Baguio, it depends on the duration of the time.

b.) We have to solve for the parking fee from 8 AM to 6:45 PM but first we need to get how many hours spent by the car in parking.

We need to convert first 6:45 PM to 24-hour time format which is 6:45 + 12:00 = 18:45. So, 6:45 PM can also be expressed as 18:45.

Now we have to subtract 18:45 and 8:00.

18:45

– 8:00

----------------------

10:45

Therefore, the car was parked in SM Baguio for 10 hours and 45 minutes. However, we cannot solve for the time given 45 minutes so we need to convert it first to hours. So 45 minutes is 45/60 = ¾ or 0.75. Therefore, 10 hours, 45 minutes is 10.75 hours. We can already substitute it to the piecewise function.

Since t = 10.75, it lies between 3 < t < 12, so it will be substituted to the second part of the piecewise function.

f(10.75) = 50 + 15(10.75)

f(10.75) = 50 + 161.25

f(10.75) = 211.25

Therefore, you will be paying a parking fee of P 211.25 when you parked from 8 AM to 6:45 PM in SM Baguio.

Ang pinaka una at pinakamatandang park sa Baguio ayon sa aking pananaliksik ay ang Burnham Park.

Ang pangalan ng Burnham Park ay pinangalanan kay Daniel Burnham, isang arkitekturang Amerikano na naging tagaplano ng lungsod para sa Baguio. Dinisenyo niya ang parke at ang orihinal na mga plano para sa lungsod ng sabay-sabay, at nagsimula ang konstruksiyon sa paligid ng park noong 1904.

Sinasaklaw ng Burnham Park ang tanging malaking kalawakan ng antas ng lupa sa loob ng maburol na paligid. Ang modernong parke, kahit na mas maliit kaysa sa orihinal na parke dahil sa pag-unlad ng lunsod, ay napanatili ang karamihan sa orihinal na disenyo at layout nito.

Ang parke ay may mahalagang papel na ginampanan nang ito ay magsilbing lugar ng kanlungan para sa mga taga-Baguio kasunod ng isang malakas at mapaminsalang lindol noong 1990.
