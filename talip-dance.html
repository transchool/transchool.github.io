3. The courtship dance wherein a man lures a woman with an attractive blanket to place on her shoulders. Select one: a. Talip b. Dinnuyya c. Mumbaki d. Intaneg


Talip is an ethnic dance and a courtship dance from Cordillera where the man lures a woman with an attractive blanket to place on her shapely shoulders.

Other regions also have other terms for their courtship dance. In Visayas, they call it Inday na Kunday. In Bulacan, Sayaw ng Pag-ibig. In Jolo, Sulo, it’s Sua-Sua. And in Ilocos, it’s Imunan.
