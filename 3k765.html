[tex]\red{⊱┈───────────────────────┈⊰}[/tex]

PROBLEM :What amount must be invested in order to have 128 3765 to after 8 years if money is worth 6% compounded semi-annually?

GIVEN :128 3765Years - 8Worth - 6%

ASKED :What amount must be invested?

SOLUTION :[tex]\rm \: n = 8 \div \frac{1}{2} = 16 \\ \rm \: i = 6\% \div 2 = 3\% \\ \rm1283765 = PV \times (1 + 3\%) {}^{16} \\\rm PV = 800,000[/tex]ANSWER :Therefore , The answer is P800,000
