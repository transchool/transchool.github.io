30. how can you identify the different types of thai massage products​


Answer:

As it turns out, there's a very good reason for that diversity of massage therapy options, and it all comes down to how those different therapy techniques can benefit you. All massage therapy tends to focus on the same basic type of manual manipulation of muscle tissue.

How can you identify the different types of Thai massage products​?

Thai massage is a centuries-old practice that is used on the entire body, from head to toe. This is a thorough massage with stretching.

What are the various kinds of Thai massage?

Traditional Thai massage. This is both the most popular and the most traditional Thai massage. It's called a "Yoga massage," and it's done while you're fully dressed and on a padded mat on the floor. Acupressure and yoga techniques are combined in this treatment. It relieves tension in the joints, muscles, and tendons. It also gives you energy and helps you relax completely.

Thai oil massage. This is an oil-based Thai massage that is exceptionally smooth. Traditional Thai massage is significantly more calming. It's done on a standard massage stretcher with the body wrapped in a towel. Its purpose is to stimulate the body's meridian or energy pathways using acupressure. This massage promotes blood circulation, softens the skin, and relaxes the mind thanks to the aromatherapy oils.

Thai pinda massage. This is a hybrid of traditional Thai massage and ancestral healing techniques based on the placement of plant pouches around the body to promote energy flow. Pindas (a hot poultice filled with medicinal herbs) are used to relax the body by working on the acupuncture points. It promotes blood circulation and relieves muscle and joint pain.

Thai foot massage. It entails massaging the feet with massage oil and exerting pressure to particular areas of the soles of the feet that correspond to various body organs. The pressure on these points helps to release toxins and improve the body's overall function.
