Answer:

1. Uri ng Pangngalan Ayon sa Kayarian_1 ; Mga sagot sa Uri ng Pangngalan Ayon sa Kayarian_1: This 40-item worksheet has two parts. The first part asks the student to tell whether a given noun is payak, maylapi, inuulit, or tambalan. The second part asks the students to classify the given nouns into the same four categories.

2. Uri ng Pangngalan Ayon sa Kayarian_2 ; Mga sagot sa Uri ng Pangngalan Ayon sa Kayarian_2: This 30-item worksheet has two parts. The first part asks the student to write the second word that forms the compound noun. The student is asked to choose from a set of given words.

The second part of the worksheet asks the student to tell whether the given noun is repeated or inuulit. Note that the root word (which is a noun) or part of the root word (usually the first two syllables) is repeated. These words may also have an affix but they are considered to fall under the inuulit category.

Words such as daldal, siksik, and dingding, although nouns, are not considered to have the structure of inuulit because there are no root words dal, sik, or ding.

10. Search for an example of a worksheet. Explain the different columns included in the worksheet.


Accounting Worksheet Columns Included:

-Unadjusted Trial Balance

-Adjusting Entries

-Adjusted Trial Balance

-Income Statement

-Balance Sheet

Explanations:

Unadjusted Trial Balance

A column contains all the company’s assets, liability, expenses, and revenue accounts, which are used in a concerned year.

Adjusting Entries

All the accounts of the company, which requires the passing of the adjustment entry will get listed in the adjustment column.

Adjusted Trial Balance

The adjusted trial balance will be prepared by combining the entries of the previous two columns, Unadjusted trial balance, and adjustments.

Income Statement

A column contains the values concerning the expenses and revenue accounts only. In this case, if the value of total revenue exceeds the expense column, then the difference will be the net income of the year of the company as it is earning more revenues in the year than what it is spending on its expenses.

Balance Sheet

A column contains the values with respect to the assets, liabilities, and owner’s capital only.

Ano ang konkreto at di-konkretong pangngalan?

Ang konkreto at di-konkreto ay ang dalawang uri ng pangngalang pambalana. Ang pambalana ay tumutukoy sa pangkalahatang ngalan ng pangngalan. Ito ang ngalan ng tao, bagay, hayop, lugar o pangyayari na hindi partikular o tiyak. Nagsisimula rin ito sa maliit na titik.

Ang konkreto ay tumutukoy sa mga pambalana na nakikita, nahihipo o nahahawakan. Ito ay gumagamit ng pandama upang mabigyang pansin. Ito ay tinatawag din na tahas.

Ang di-konkreto naman ay tumutukoy sa mga ideya, saloobin o damdamin. Ito ay tinatawag din na basal.
