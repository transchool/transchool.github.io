16. 19. Each of four siblings (Edmund, Genalyn, Madelyn,and Sonia) bought four different cars. One choosesa Honda, a Mitsubishi, a Toyota, and a Suzuki car,From the following clues, determine which siblingbought which car.(1) Edmund, living alone, stays next door to hissister who bought the Honda car and very farfrom his sister who bought the Suzuki car.(2) Genalyn, also living alone, is younger than theone who bought the Mitsubishi car and olderthan her sibling who bought the Toyota car.(3) Madelyn did not like Toyota and Suzuki cars.But she and her sibling, who bought the Toyotacar, live in the same house.​


Edmund - Mitsubishi Genalyn - Suzuki Madelyn - Honda Sonia - Toyota

Further explanation:

This type of problem can be easily solved by listing and analysing all the given data.

 

Given:

List all the data.  

Edmund lives alone. Edmund doesn’t own HONDA because he lives next door to his sister who owns it. Edmund doesn’t own SUZUKI because he lives very far from his sister who owns it. Genalyn lives alone.  Genalyn doesn’t own MITSUBISHI because she’s younger than the one who owns it. Genalyn doesn’t own TOYOTA because she’s older than the one who owns it. Madelyn doesn’t own TOYOTA and SUZUKI because she doesn’t like those. Madelyn lives in the same house with TOYOTA.  

Analysis:

1. Edmund and Genalyn both lives alone (See Given #1&2) and Madelyn lives together with the sibling that owns a Toyota (See Given #8). Therefore, Sonia is the sibling that lives together with Madelyn and she’s the one that owns a Toyota. SONIA = TOYOTA

2. Edmund doesn’t own a Honda (See Given #2) nor a Suzuki (See Given #3).  And Sonia is the one that owns a Toyota (See Analysis #1) so that leaves Edmund with a Mitsubishi. EDMUND = MITSUBISHI

3. Madelyn doesn’t own a Toyota nor a Suzuki (See Given #7). And Edmund is the one that owns a Mitsubishi (See Analysis #2) so that leaves Madelyn with Honda. MADELYN = HONDA

4. Genalyn doesn’t own a Mitsubishi nor a Toyota (See Given #5&6). And Madelyn is the one that owns a Honda (See Analysis #3) so that leaves Genalyn with a Suzuki. GENALYN = SUZUKI
