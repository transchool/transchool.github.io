Visualizing Fractions in Multiplication

Visualizing fractions simply refers to making models of fractions. This visualization helps to find the answer in the multiplication of fractions. There are three steps to do this.

Draw a figure to model the fractions. It can be a square, rectangle or circle. It depends on your choice.Divide the figure into equal parts vertically and color some parts of it depending on the numerator and the denominator of the first fraction. Divide the figure into equal parts horizontally and color some parts of it depending on the numerator and the denominator of the second fraction.

How To Multiply Fractions

After visualizing the fractions, you can now start solving. To multiply, just multiply the numerators across and the denominators across. Simplify, if needed.
