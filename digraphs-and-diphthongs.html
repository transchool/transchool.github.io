29. Example of diphthongs 2each


In phonetics, a diphthong is a vowel in which there is a noticeable sound change within the same syllable. (In contrast, a single or simple vowel is known as a monophthong.) ... The process of moving from one vowel sound to another is called gliding, and thus another name for diphthong is gliding vowel.
