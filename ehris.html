12. Andre and chris are traveling to a business conference. Andre travels 110 km in the same time that chris travels 140 km. Chris travels 15km per hour faster than andre. Find the average rate of each person.


Answer:

Andre = 55 kphChris = 55 + 15 = 70 kph

Step-by-step explanation:

Andre travels 110 km in the same time that Chris travels 140 km. If we can solve the time, then we can find the rate.

[tex]time = \frac{distance}{rate}[/tex]

Hence,

Let A be time for Andre and C be time for Chris:

[tex]a = \frac{110}{x}[/tex]

[tex]c=\frac{140}{x+15}[/tex]

The rate for Chris is x + 15 since he travels 15 km per hour faster than Andre.

Since it says that Andre travels in the same time as Chris. Then we know that:

[tex]a = c[/tex]

or

[tex]\frac{110}{x} = \frac{140}{x+15}[/tex]

Simplify:

[tex]\frac{110}{x} = \frac{140}{x+15}[/tex]

[tex]110x + 1650 = 140x[/tex]

[tex]110x = 140x - 1650[/tex]

[tex]-30x = -1650[/tex]

[tex]x = 55[/tex]

∴ Andre = 55 kph and Chris = 55 + 15 = 70 kph
