2. compare courtship before and at present time


Courtship before and courtship at present are a bit different. In the past, the men would typically spend at least a year to be able to court a woman they like. They also have to seek the permission of the parents first and men would need to serve the family to show their sincerity. In modern times, these practices are now gone and men usually just court women through text messages or chat messages. The consent of the parents are also not sought anymore.

One can say that there are many changes in how courtship was and how it is at present because our morals, values and practices have changed through the years. What is important is that when courting someone, love should be present and not just to seek pleasure. Love and trust are two key components of any relationship which is unchanged over time.
