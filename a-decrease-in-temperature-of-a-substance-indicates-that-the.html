3. the decrease in temperature of a substance indicates that


Answer:

The answer is letter d) the average kinetic energy of its particles decreases.

Temperature is the manifestation of heat energy, present in all matter. Heat is the kinetic energy in all matter. When the kinetic energy is decreasing, it corresponds to a cold temperature. Meanwhile, if the kinetic energy is increasing, it corresponds to hot temperature. Temperature is a physical quantity that described hot and cold measured by a thermometer.

Body Temperature and Fever

Body temperature is a vital sign (brainly.ph/question/3608029) to observe the occurrence of fever in a person. Below are the body temperature and fever range:

Hypothermia (below 35 °C) occurs when body heat drops faster than the body can produce it .

The normal body temperature (36.5 to 37.5 °C) depends on the person and their activities.  

Hyperthermia (greater than 38°C) is a low-grade fever usually of mild infectious diseases and adverse drug reactions.  

Hyperpyrexia (greater than 40°C) signifies a higher temperature resulting in brain hemorrhage, sepsis, or serious infection.

Ways to Measure Body Temperature

Below are the different ways to measure body temperature using a thermometer:

Oral temperature

Ear or tympanic temperature

Rectal temperature

Forehead or temporal temperature

Armpit or axillary temperature  (brainly.ph/question/6660066)
