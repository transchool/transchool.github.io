24. BMI HEIGHT: 5'5 BMI WEIGHT: 42 WHAT IS THE BMI? ​


Answer:

BMI weight (kg) / [height (m)]2

First you need to convert the bmi height to meter; 5'5"=1.651m

For better explanation on how to convert 5'5" to m:

Multiply the value in feet by 30.48 to get the result of the conversion of feet in cm: 5 x 30.48 = 152.4cmMultiply the value in inches by 2.54 to get the result of the conversion from inches to cm: 5 x 2.54 = 12.7cmNow, add the two partial results to obtain the final value in cm: 152.4 + 12.7 = 165.1cm.Divide the value 165.1cm by 100 to get the result in meters. 165.1 ÷ 100 = 1.651m

2.

[tex]bmi = (kg) \div (m) {}^{2} \\ bmi = 42 \: kg \: \div (1.651m) {}^{2} \\ bmi = 42 \: kg \div 2.725801m {}^{2} \\ bmi = 15.40831484 \frac{kg}{ m ^{2} } \\ bmi = 15.41 \frac{kg}{ {m}^{2} } [/tex]

Therefore it is underweight.
