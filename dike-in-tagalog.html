20. TARLAC DIKE SUMMARY by kerima polotan


Tarlac Dike

Summary:

This is a story about the real life of the author during her childhood. She had narrated several reasons why the dike have cracked and had caused thousands of people to move out of their homes and find another shelter just to secure themselves. According to her, the lifestyle of the people is the main reason why this had to happen. She remembers how friendly it was during her childhood since it is not flooded with any garbage but just the waters that runs to the ledge as it rains that laps against the wall. But this water even the river had disappeared on summer for reason she do not know. She remembers old friends and classmates who used to pick some ripe fruits along the way and eat them while walking for home. She particularly remembers a good friend who had become one of the richest woman of their place who used to live in an old nipa house. It was the dike who had witnesses every changes that took place. The old dike that was clean and right, little by little had been covered by little dark secrets. The life that was so admirable for her seem to overcome her. She had seen the simplicity of their lives compared to those who have experienced advances in their lives. Her friend had gone with their wealth and status and can no longer go with them. Seeing her successful is not an issue for her but a source of inspiration that someday she should also be and their living condition will improve. Long enough, their lives have changed and they have developed but the dike stood still though it is now cracked, it still holds memory of their lives. In her mind still lies the memory of their childhood. The memory that they had once walked through that dike and have done several things that only the dike have seen them do together.
