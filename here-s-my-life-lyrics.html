23. where am i now in my life how did i get here


I have been living for 23 years. I have through so many things in my life that have shaped me who I am today. If I can make stages in this life, it would have three stages. The time when I just followed where this life would take me, the time when I started to organize and plan what kind of life I want to live in. Living in the time when I enjoy it until I die.

From those three stages, I am today living between two stages, the stage of when I started to plan what kind of life I want to live and the stage of when I enjoy it for the rest of my lifetime. I am still trying to reach out all of the life goals I perfectly plan. I already know where I want to be, whom I want to be with, and what kind of life I would have in the future. What I have learned on my life journey is that everything does not always go as how I planned and expected. It has ups and downs and deep valleys that have brought me to where and what I am now.

I was planning to be a teacher, so I tried to go to an education school but I always failed.  That was where my life shifted into not like how I have dreamed of. After several unsuccessful attempts to study education at several different universities, I finally started to change my dream. I went to nurse school and graduate three years after.

And here I am now. A nursing student and became an assistant to one of the lecturers at my campus. I never planned to continue my education to nursing school. I also never thought of becoming a teaching assistant. However, I am grateful for what I am now. I'm still trying to have the life I have dreamed of and I hope it will be much more delightful.
