INGREDIENTS

2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces 3 pieces dried bay leaves 8 tablespoons soy sauce 4 tablespoons white vinegar 5 cloves garlic crushed 1 1/2 cups water 3 tablespoons cooking oil 1 teaspoon sugar 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional) 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

PROCEDURE

Combine chicken, soy sauce, and garlic in a large bowl. Mix well. Marinate the chicken for at least 1 hour. Note: the longer the time, the better Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.
