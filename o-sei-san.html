7. what is seis in science​


Answer:

The Seismic Experiment for Interior Structure (SEIS) is part of NASA's InSight mission's scientific payload. SEIS, which will land on Mars in 2018, will be the first European seismometer on another planet. The instrument can record ground motions as small as 0.001 mm over a wide frequency range of 0.001 Hz to 50 Hz. It can thus detect signals from marsquakes, meteorite impacts, local events such as dustdevils or landslides, and even the tiny tidal deformation of Mars caused by its moon Phobos. Seismic waves transmitted through the interior of Mars will be recorded and analyzed, providing important insights into the planet's interior structure.

22. Art period of fish vendors by sey perez


Answer:

Title of the Contemporary Artwork: FISH VENDORS

Artist: SEY PEREZ

Region: Region III PAMPANGA, PHILIPPINES

1. What is artwork all about? Pictured are two fishermen and a farmer who show that they live on a daily basis and what we usually eat as Filipinos which is from agricultural like fish.

2. What does the artist want to convey to his/her audience? He wants to convey to us that the Philippines is a rich country with natural resources and at the present time it seems that our Government is no longer paying enough attention to the Agricultural sector where we prefer to just import to other countries. But we can keep up with the field of Agriculture, only support for the Gov’t and adequate funding is really a big help so that we can further enrich our own resources.

3. What evidence is present in the artwork for you to consider it as a contemporary Philippine art? Until now, farming and fishing (fish vendors) are one of the common livelihoods that we Filipinos have. It is part of our cultural identity as a Filipino living on a rich island. We can say that it is a contemporary art because it is an identification of a culture that still lives as part of everyday living.
