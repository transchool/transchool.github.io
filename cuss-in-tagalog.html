What is Acceleration?

Acceleration is a physical quantity that determines the rate of change of velocity as a function of time. It is considered as a vector quantity, so it has magnitude, direction and direction.

What is Speed?

The distance traveled by an object divided by the amount of time needed to cover that distance is what is referred to as its speed. It is a fundamental idea in mechanics and is crucial to comprehending a number of physical phenomena, such as the motion of planets, the characteristics of waves, and the movement of atomic-scale particles.

What is Displacement?

Displacement is the term used to describe how an object has changed overall, usually as measured between the object's initial and final positions. It has both a direction and a magnitude because it is a vector quantity.

What is Distance?

Distance is the spatial separation between objects or points in space.

26. Dis cuss and give your own insights about "Atheistic".​


Discuss and give your own insights about "Atheistic"?

The term atheistic is used to indicate things that incorporate atheism, or the belief in the absence of a supreme being or deity. In other terms, atheism is the denial of God's or any gods' existence. Atheist is a term used to describe someone who holds such a view. The adjective atheist can be used to characterize such beliefs or anything that involve such beliefs. In the same way, the term atheistic is employed. An atheist's writings, for example, could be classified as atheistic. Themes in a book or film that are perceived to encourage or deal with atheism could be classified as atheistic. Those who disagree with atheism frequently use the term negatively. The term atheistic is distinct from the term agnostic, which refers to or identifies a person who feels that knowing whether or not a supreme entity exists is impossible. An atheist would say "No" if asked if God or gods exist, but an agnostic would say "It is impossible to know."
