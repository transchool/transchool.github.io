Meanwhile, opinion is a person's product of thinking process, which generally falls to the category of subjective view.

Scientific facts are examples of truth, since scientists use particul4r methods to prove whether a cl4im or hypothesis is proven true or not. Whereas opinions come from individuals, and are something that can be proven to be false.
