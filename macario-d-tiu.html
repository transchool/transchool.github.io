Si Macario Peralta, Jr. (Hulyo 30, 1913 – Enero 7, 1975) ay isang tunay na bayaning Pilipino na kinilala ng Amerika noong pangalawang giyerang pangmundo (WWII) bago naging politiko bilang isang senador ng Pilipinas (1949-1955). 15. What is the republic established by Macario?


Republika ng Katagalugan

Republika ng Katagalugan was founded by Macario Sakay in 1902 with attempts to revolt against the American soldiers and free the Philippines from the shackles of the colonizers.

Along with the hiding Katipuneros in the mountains, he established the Republic in hand with a constitution and a flag similarly assosciated with Andres Bonifacio.

22. Macario Sakaycontribution ni macario sakay ​


Macario sakay y de León (March 1, 1878 – September 13, 1907) was a Filipino general who took part in the 1896 Philippine Revolution against the Spanish Empire and in the Philippine–American War. After the war was declared over by the United States in 1902, Sakay continued resistance by leading guerrilla raids.


23. who is macario fronda?


Answer:

BORN on December 8, 1921 in Agoo, La Union, Macario Gagaza Fronda started his teaching career at the Saint Louis School elementary department in 1947. Six years later in 1953, he became a high school teacher in Physical Education. He was married to Ildefonsa Estoque Fronda and were blessed with eight children. Mr. Fronda was appointed in 1967 as the Dean of Student Affairs of Saint Louis University until he retired in 1987. Although officially retired, he continued to serve as a consultant to the band and music lecturer until 2006.
