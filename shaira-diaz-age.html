Summary of the centipede of Ronny Diaz.

The story was about a misunderstanding between Eddie and his sister Delia. The conflict of between the two started when Delia provoke her brother Eddie by way of attacking and destroying Eddie’s pet and other Eddie’s Important things in life. Delia does not like Eddie to be happy in life. She likes watching Eddie suffering from what she has done. Delia treated Eddie as rival and enemy not a brother. Delia always blaming her brother about the death of her mother. Delia always thinking that Eddie is more important by her father rather than her. She has a very bad character and a very jealous girl. She used her illness as an alibi so she can do more bad things to her brother Eddie. She knew that Eddie is a good brother with her and he cannot fight back her. Eddie endures all the bad things did by his sister. As a younger brother of Delia he tried to understand all the bad things done by his sister due to her illness. Eddie did nothing against his sister even Delia did something bad to his monkey, his pigeons and his butterflies. After all things, Eddie’s patience has and end, when Eddie saw Delia stabbing the eye of his dog. This time around, Eddie cannot control his anger anymore, he revenged against his sister by using centipede to frighten his sister Delia. A while after, Delia fell down and cried with pained. This time, Eddie realized that he was not supposed to do it with his sister even how bad Delia with him. He realized that damaged has already been done and cannot be reversed anytime.

The lessons of the story of the centipede .

We should not make hasty decisions in life without thinking it over and over again to avoid regrets. We should not take revenge instead pray and ask God's help to escape from trouble. The bible always remind us to love our enemy, it is hard commandments of God but it is true. Like what happened to Eddie he loved his sister despite of all bitterness, but the time came Eddie lost his temper and did something wrong against his sister.  There are sometimes in our lives that we cannot take the pain anymore thus we take revenge. But God commanded us not to take revenge against anyone else. We should give maximum tolerance especially to our family, even if they have done something wrong to us.

The characteristic of the two Eddie and Delia.

Eddie is a patience brother to his sister Delia. Eddie is a good brother to his sister. Eddie is a loving brother.  Eddie has a human nature, he knows how to be angry when things getting worse. Eddie learned to accept his wrongness and is willing to repent. Delia has belt in bad character even she has an illness. Delia is a jealous girl to her brother. Delia has a high temper. Delia has no love with her brother. Delia has no love with the animals. Delia is a spoiled girl.

18. summary of the centipede of rony diaz


The Centipede

Answer:

When Eddie saw his sister, her demeanor and appearance seemed like a dagger completed with her brown dress. She was hitting his dog Biryuk with a stick, although hate and rage is fueling inside of Eddie while watching her sister he could not do anything for her sister has a weak heart. This scene was like of many others that had happened before.

Characters in The Centipede:

Eddie- young boy  Delia- Eddie’s sister Berto- worker in their house Biryuk- stray dog Eddie found

He met the dog Biryuk when he and his father were out hunting for birds. With deemed patience they awaited for birds to take a leap to the sky before firing the shotgun. He was taught that hunters spit for good luck and do Eddie did. As they are waiting, a dog suddenly leaped towards the bird who were taking a long time to fly. Eddie fired his father’s shotgun and shot three with one shell.

Eddie came to gather the shot birds, though the grass made it complicated to search. Biryuk pinpointed where the birds were and befriended Eddie. Eddie asked his father’s permission to keep Biryuk for the meantime, his father said that the dog will make a good pointer.

Eddie followed Biryuk to where he was hiding and he noticed that Biryuk’s eye was pierced from the stick his sister used. With rage piling up more and more he screamed, he walked towards their house when Berto called Eddie to show him a large centipede that almost bit him. Eddie killed the centipede and kept its shell.

He used the centipede to get back to his sister who was embroidering in the house. He tossed the white handkerchief towards his sister then she shrieked back. Her sister did see that it was Eddie who did it, as she struggles to keep her breathing she collapsed. And then Eddie panicked and insisted that the centipede was already dead.

Eddie’s bad memory of his sister Delia:

When Eddie came inside his sister’s playhouse and broke her doll, she grabbed a sapling and hit Eddie over and over. She told Berto to kill Eddie’s monkey because it smiled at her. She made Eddie’s pigeon house destroyed for kindling because it stank. She burned his butterfly collection because she said it was attracting ants.

30. nagawa ni Bartolome diaz


Answer:Bartolomeu Dias ay upang makibahagi sa mga mas mahalagang paglalakbay ng pagtuklas sa anumang iba pang mga explorer. Bago ang kanyang paglalakbay sa timog na dulo ng Africa, Bartolomeu Dias lumahok sa paglalakbay at pagtuklas sa kahabaan ng kanlurang baybayin ng Africa sa Diogo da Azambuja. Pagkatapos ng Christopher Columbus, ginawa ang kanyang paglalakbay ng pagtuklas, Bartolomeu Dias organisado at sinamahan Vasco da Gama ni fleet nito sa paglalakbay sa Indya. Paglalakbay-dagat ay posible lamang dahil sa ang mas maaga pagtuklas ng Bartolomeu Dias. Dahil sa paglalakbay ng Bartolomeu Dias at Vasco da Gama, Portugal naging madre de pamilya ng mga pinakikinabangan ng dagat-ruta sa paligid ng Africa sa Indya. Bartolomeu Dias sa wakas captained isang barko sa fleet ng Pedro Cabral, na kung saan ay isa sa mga pinakamalaking fleets na si kailanman sailed ang Atlantic. Ito kasama ang pagtuklas ng Brazil bilang isa sa kanyang mga nagawa. Ito ay ang pinakamahabang paglalakbay sa kasaysayan hanggang sa oras na iyon, at isa sa mga pinakamalaking at pinaka-maimpluwensyang paglalakbay ng pagtuklas kailanman ginawa
