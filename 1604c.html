5. A balloon has a pressure of 2.05 atm at 280C. If the temperature is reduced to 160C, what will be the new pressure of the balloon? (Ex. 450.60 atm)​


Answer:

P₂ = 1.61 atm

Explanation:

Converting °C to K

T₁ = 280°C + 273 = 553 K

T₂ = 160°C + 273 = 433 K

Calculating the final pressure

P₂ = P₁ × (T₂ / T₁)

P₂ = 2.05 atm × (433 K / 553 K)

P₂ = 1.61 atm
