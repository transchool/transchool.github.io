19. a kiddie pool with 52200 cm3 is shown in the diagram,what volume of water in (L) can it hold?​


1 Liter = 1000 cm³

[tex] 52200 \: cm^{3} \: \times \frac{1 \: liter}{1000 \: cm^{3}} \\ \\ 52200 \: \cancel{cm^{3}} \: \times \frac{1 \: liter}{1000 \: \cancel{cm^{3}}} \\ \\ 52.2 \: liters[/tex]

Therefore the pool can hold 52.2 liters of water.
