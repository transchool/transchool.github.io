29. A,R,and M are pints on the line. If AR=x+7m, RM = 2x-3m and AM =58m,what will be the length of RM? ​


Answer:

the length of RM is 33 meters.

Answer:
We know that AM = AR + RM, so substituting given expressions:

58m = (x + 7m) + (2x - 3m)

Simplifying and solving for x:

58m = 3x + 4m

54m = 3x

x = 18m

Now we can substitute x back into the expression for RM:

RM = 2x - 3m = 2(18m) - 3m = 33m

Therefore, the length of RM is 33m.
