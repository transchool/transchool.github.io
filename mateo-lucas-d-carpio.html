18. 11. Who discover the value of 1 mole?A. Avogadro MateoB. Madeo AvogadroC. Amadeo AvogadroD. Mateo Avogado​


Answer:

C. Amedeo Avogadro

It is named after the 19th-century Italian physicist Amedeo Avogadro, who found that under the same temperature and pressure, two gases with the same volume have the same number of molecules. It was the French physicist Jean Perrin who in the early 20th century dubbed the amount of units in a mole as Avogadro's number.

L = W + 4, 000

Use the substitution method

L + W = 20,000

(W + 4000) + W = 20,000

2W + 4000 = 20,000

2W = 20,000 - 4000

2W = 16,000

W = 8,000 William's score

L = W + 4000

L = 8,000 + 4000

L = 12,000 Luca's score

Ipinakikita ng ating pamumuhay kung ano ang ating pinaniniwalaan at kung ang ipinahahayag nating pananampalataya ay isang buhay na pananampalataya.
