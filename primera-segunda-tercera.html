2. mi Primera Inspiracion​


Answer:

Mi Primera Inspiracion (My First Inspiration) is a Spanish poem that is believed to have been written by Jose Rizal when he was nine years old.

Mi Primera Inspiracion (My First Inspiration) is a Spanish poem that is believed to have been written by Jose Rizal when he was nine years old. There are claims, however, that this poem was actually written by his nephew, Antonio Lopez-Rizal (Narcisa’s son) because of the seeming likeness in handwriting of the two Rizals.

The poem:

¿Porqué exhalan a porfía

del cáliz dulces olores

las embalsamadas flores

en este festivo dia?

Y ¿porqué, en la selva amena,

se oye dulce melodía

que asemeja la armonía

de la arpada filomena?

¿Porqué en la mullida grama

las aves, al son del viento,

exhalan meloso acento

y saltan de rama en rama?

Y la fuente cristalina,

formando dulce murmullo,

del cefiro al suave arrullo

entre las flores camina?

Es que hoy celebran tu día

¡oh, mi Madre cariñosa!

con su perfume la rosa

y el ave con su armonía.

Y la fuente rumorosa,

en este día feliz,

con su murmullo te dice

¡que vivas siempre gozosa!

Y, de esa fuente al rumor,

oye la primera nota,

que ahora de mi laud brota

al impulso de mi amor!

4. Mi primera inspiracion meaning per stanza


My First Inspiration” Mi Primera Inspiracion was the first poem written by Dr. Jose Rizal during his third academic year in Ateneo de Municipal. He wrote the poem in 1874, before he turned 14. He was delighted to see his mother, Doña Teodora Alonso, released from prison that same year so he dedicated the poem to her.

Explanation:

He was 16, she was 14: Jose Rizal's first love, first heartbreak

We know Jose Rizal as a national hero, but once upon a time, he was also a teenager whose heart was broken by his first love.

Si Segunda Katigbak, ang unang babaeng nagpatibok sa puso ni Jose Rizal

Puppy love, last love ni Dr. Jose Rizal

Ang mga babaeng minahal ni Dr. Jose Rizal, kilalanin sa 'i Juander'

At 16, Rizal crossed paths with Segunda Katigbak, a sister of his friend. She was just 14 when she first met him in Trozo, Manila.

"He has a friend by the namen of Mariano Katigbak and they went to the house of the grandmother of Rizal and it so happen that it that place in Trozo, Manila, that's where Tutuban is right now, he saw Segunda or Segunda saw him kasi it was Segunda who fell in love with him," historian Corazon Manalo narrated in a 24 Oras report by Howie Severino.

Love blossomed between the two teenagers when Rizal visited his siblings at La Concordia College in Paco, Manila, the same school attended by Segunda.

It seemed that Segunda was the one who made the first move to engage Rizal in a conversation.
