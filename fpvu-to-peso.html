Answer:

D. 10x²-x-3 pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

Hope it Helps!

Answer:

D.  (10x² - x - 3) pesos

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Number of desks:  (2x + 1)Cost per desk: (5x - 3)

Total Cost = (number of desks) × (costs per desk)

Find the total cost:

Total cost = (2x + 1) (5x - 3)

= (2x)(5x) + (2x)(-3) + (1)(5x) + (1)(-3)

= 10x² -6x + 5x - 3

= 10x² - x - 3
