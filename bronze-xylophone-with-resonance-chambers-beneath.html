Explanation:

The Kudyapi is a long-necked string instrument used by the Mangyans of Mindoro. It has two strings that run from the base up till the end of its long neck. The base also has the resonating chamber that produces all the sounds.


13. a bronze xylophone consist of 10-14 tuned metal bars is called​


Answer:

Gendèr

Explanation:

A gendèr is a type of metallophone used in Balinese and Javanese gamelan music. It consists of 10 to 14 tuned metal bars suspended over a tuned resonator of bamboo or metal, which are tapped with a mallet made of wooden disks (Bali) or a padded wooden disk (Java).
