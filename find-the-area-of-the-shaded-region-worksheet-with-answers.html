4. Finding the nth term of an arithmetic sequence worksheet


aₙ = nth term

a₁ = first term

d = common difference

aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1)*d

The nth term is equal to the first term plus the product of the common difference and the number of term minus one.

spreadsheet - a table in which you can enter and manipulate data.
