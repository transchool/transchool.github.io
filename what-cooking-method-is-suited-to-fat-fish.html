16. IDENTIFY THE MISSING WORD: Fat fish tastes better when cooked using dry heat method either by broiling or ________.


Aside from broiling, another dry heat method that can be used in cooking fat fish and have a better taste is grilling.

These two cooking methods have the same process which is using heat to cook food. The only difference is that in broiling, the heat above (e.g. oven) is used in cooking the fish, while in grilling, the heat below (e.g barbecue grill) is used in cooking the fish. These two methods are the fastest way to cook fish without adding fats to it (unlike sauté or deep frying).
