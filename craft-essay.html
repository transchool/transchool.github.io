What is a craft essay?

a practice of making things which is done by a group of people, using local available material to fulfil their requirements. It's a term that involves skill, knowledge, tradition, culture, beliefs, society and many other numerous aspects which are intangible to be conscripted.

How do you write a craft essay?

Focus on powerful craft moves, like symbolism. Write a claim for author's craft, either focusing on one or many craft moves. Plan how your essay will go and begin drafting. Write introductions that summarize the story, explain the essay's relevance, state the claim, and write strong conclusions.
