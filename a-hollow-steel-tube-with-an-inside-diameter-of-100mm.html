3. determine the outside diameter of a hollow steel tube that will carry a tensile load of 500KN with a stress of 140MPa assume the wall thickness to be 1/10 of the outside diameter


Given:

Load = 500 kN

Normal Stress = 140 Mpa

We will use the equation for Normal Stress,

Solution:

Ns= P/A

where

P = Pressure

A = Area

and let's derive it to get the area so that we could determine the diameter.

--> A = P/Ns

A= (500,000)/(140×10^6) = .00357 m²

A= π/4 • D² - (π/4){D-2(.1 D)}²

A= 0.2827 D²

0.00357 = 0.2827 D²

so the outer D is 112.4 m
