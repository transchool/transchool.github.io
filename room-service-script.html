4. Upon arrival in at your destination you talk to the hotel concierge about your reservation you inquire about breakfast schedule hotel case extra bedding and room service. Make a script?​.


Answer:

Here is a script for inquiring about these topics with a hotel concierge:

You: "Hi, I just arrived at the hotel and I wanted to ask a few questions about my reservation. Could you help me with that?"

Concierge: "Of course, what would you like to know?"

You: "I was wondering about the breakfast schedule. What time does it start and what options are available?"

Concierge: "Breakfast is served from 7:00 am to 10:00 am in the hotel restaurant. We offer a variety of hot and cold options, including eggs, bacon, sausage, cereals, fruits, pastries, and more."

You: "Great, thank you. I was also wondering if it's possible to request an extra bed or some extra bedding for my room. I have a few friends staying with me and we need a bit more space."

Concierge: "Certainly, we can arrange an extra bed or bedding for you. Please let us know if you have any specific requests or preferences."

You: "Thanks, that would be really helpful. One more question: is there room service available at the hotel? I'm not sure if we'll have time to go out for dinner, so it would be nice to have the option to order food to our room."

Concierge: "Yes, we do offer room service at the hotel. The menu is available in your room and you can place an order by calling the front desk. We have a wide selection of dishes to choose from, including entrees, desserts, and drinks."

You: "Great, thank you for all your help.

Employee: Okay, do you have a special request regarding the order?
