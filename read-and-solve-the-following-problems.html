5 apples, Php 125, Php 290.75, Php 500.

6. Read, analyze and solve. Follow the steps in problem solving.​


Answer:

1. 18 months.

If Mario needs to repay his brother for Php202.50 per month for the total of Php3,645, divide the total money that he need to repay by how much will he pay per month.

3,645 ÷ 202.50 = 18.

Therefore, Mario will finish repaying the money he borrowed from his brother for 18 months.

2. Php125.5

If Luis sold 94 watermelons for Php11,797, divide the total money he earned by the number of watermelons that he sold.

11,797 ÷ 94 = 125.5

Therefore, Luis sold watermelons for 125.5 each.

3. Php135.15

If the total cost of the fare was Php540.60 for four persons, divide the total cost of the fare by the number of persons.

540.60 ÷ 4 = 135.15

Therefore, each person needs to pay 135.15 for the fare.

4. 8

If Mr.Castro will give his land that measures 12 hectars equally to all of his sons by 1.5, divide the total measure of the land to the number of measures that each of his sons will get.

12 ÷ 1.5 = 8

Therefore, Mr. Castro has a total of 8 sons.

5. 34

If the rope that was 119 meters were cut to several pieces by 3.5 meters each, divide the total measure of the rope to the measure of each pieces that were cut.

119 ÷ 3.5 = 34.

Therefore, there were a total of 34 pieces of the rope were cut.
