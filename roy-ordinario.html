1. ano ang mga gawain ng alcalde ordinario?​


Answer:

Alcalde ordinario refers to the judicial and administrative officials in the cabildos in the Spanish Viceroyalties in the Americas during the times of the Spanish Empire in the 16th through 19th centuries. Always existing in pairs, they were called Alcalde de primer voto (roughly, "first mayor") and Alcalde de segundo voto (roughly, "second mayor"). The alcalde ordinario was a judicial magistrate who, with some exceptions, was responsible for the administration of civil and criminal justice within their municipal jurisdiction.[1]

9. David is 5 years younger than Roy. Four years later, Roy will be twice as old as David. Find their present ages. ​


Let Roy present age be x

Then David present age = x - 5

After 4 years Roy's age = x + 4, David age x - 5 + 4

Roy will be twice as old as David

Therefore x + 4 = 2(x - 5 + 4)

⇒ x + 4 = 2(x - 1)

⇒ x + 4 = 2x - 2

⇒ x + 4 = 2x - 2

⇒ x - 2x = -2 - 4

⇒ -x = -6

⇒ x = 6

Therefore David's present age = x - 5 = 6 - 5 = 1

present age of Roy = 6 years and present age of David = 1 year

13. 3.The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12. Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy and Roy is 6years older than Bong. How old is Boyet?​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in solving linear equation.

The problem is an application of linear equation having three unknown variables.

Let

x is age of Roy

y is age of Boyet

z is age of Bong

Solution

Create three equation to solve three unknown variables.

First Equation

The sum of The sumof the ages of Roy, Boyet and Bong is 12.

x + y + z = 12

Second Equation

Boyet is 3 years younger than Roy.

y = x - 3

Third Equation

Roy is 6 years older than Bong.

x = z + 6

Solution

x + y + z = 12

y = x - 3

x = z + 6

y = x - 3 → y = (z + 6) - 3

x + y + z = 12 → (z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

Simplify

(z+6) + [(z+6)-3] + z = 12

z + 6 + z + 3 + z = 12

3z + 9 = 12

Solve for z

3z + 9 = 12

3z = 12-9

3z = 3

z = 1

z = 1

x = z+6

x = 1+6

x = 7

y = x-3

y = 7-3

y = 4

Check:

x+y+z= 12

7+4+1 = 12 ✓

Answer: 4 years old

Loi Krathong is a lantern festival in Thailand. "Loy" means "float" and "Krathong" is a little boat (or a small float) made from banana leaves. So "Loy Krathong" is roughly "float banana leaf boats".
