30. Emotions such as nervousness or sad ess can be heard through this paralanguage element.


The answer is pitch. Emotions such as nervousness or sadness can heard through the paralanguage element called pitch. Pitch is a paralanguage element that refers to the high or low tone of the voice. Because of this feature, pitch related to the emotions that is evident in a person's speech such as your low tone when you're sad, or your high tone when you're nervous.

What is Paralanguage?Paralanguage is a part of meta-communication that deals with the relationship between emotions, meaning, and language techniques. It determines the connection between meaning and emotions to a deeper level of human communication. The study of Paralanguage is called Paralinguistics.
