Quezon City, the most populous city in the Philippines, is politically subdivided into 142 barangays. All of Quezon City's barangays are classified as urban.

These barangays are grouped into six congressional districts, with each district represented by a congressman in the House of Representatives. As of July 2, 2012, President Be-n-i-g-no S. Aquino III signed into law Republic Act No. 10170, dividing and reapportioning the Second District into three legislative districts, namely the Second, Fifth and Sixth Districts.

Answer:

energy- the strength and vitality required for sustained physical or mental activity

27. Sino ang namumuno sa quezon city police district


Ang kasalukuyang namumuno sa Quezon City Police District ay si Chief Supt. Joselito Teodoro Esquivel Jr. Siya rin ang tinatawag na District Director.

Si Chief Supt Esquivel ay nanumpa noong ika-25 ng Abril, taong 2018. Pinalitan niya ang Region 4A Director na si Director Guillermo Eleazar. Sa mensahe niya sa kanyang panunumpa, sinabi niya na di niya bibiguin si PNP Chief Director Oscar Albayalde dahil ang PNP Chief mismo ang kumuha sa kanya upang mamuno sa QCPD.

Nakatanggap rin ang QCPD ng iba't ibang parangal at nakatanggap si Chief Supt Esquivel ng "Plaque of Recognition" dahil sa kanyang maganda at maayos na pamumuno sa QCPD.
