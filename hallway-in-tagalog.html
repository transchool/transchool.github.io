3. How many thirds of a hallway are there to decorate in 5 hallways? thirds


Hi! So first, in one hallway, there are 3 thirds right? This is by definition of "a third". This means that in 5 hallways, there are 5 x 3 thirds = 15 thirds.

Another way of thinking about this is: how many thirds are in 5? Writing the equation for that would be:

5 ÷ 1/3 = 5 x 3 = 15.
