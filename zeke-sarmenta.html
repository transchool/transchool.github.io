7. Zeke is 2 years older than Janrhey. After 3 years the sum of their ages will be 40. How old are they now?


Answer: Janrhey is 16 yrs old now while Zeke is 18 yrs old.

Step-by-step explanation:

Age problems are solved by careful representation of knowns and unknowns and mathematical expressions.

Let x=Janrhey's age

Then x+2 = Zeke's age

In 3 yrs, their age would be,

x+3=Janrhey

x+5=Zeke

Acc to the problem the sum of their ages in 3 yrs would be 40. This allows you to solve for x.

(x+3) + (x+5) = 40

2x+8=40

x=16=Janrhey's age

x+2=18= Zeke's age
