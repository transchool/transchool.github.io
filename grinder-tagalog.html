28. parts and function of a meat grinder​


Answer:

Parts of Meat Grinder and their Function

What is meat grinder?

Obviously, from the name itself, meat grinder is use to grind up meat. It can be manual or electrical.

Electrical meat grinder is more advisable to use as its ability to quickly cut through most kitchen meats unlike manual meat grinder as it takes a lot longer to cut through a large volume of meat.

What are the parts of meat grinder and their function?

Here are the main parts of meat grinder:

Housing with Motor and Gearbox - main component of electrical meat grinder. The gearbox reduces engine speed and increases the force with which the shaft rotates.

Screw - the working shaft, twisted in a spiral. Its task is to move products to the exit.

Tray - it is put on top of the neck, facilitates and speeds up the loading of products.

Knife - for cutting and grinding of products before passing them through the grate.

Lattices - the gratings are divided into 3 categories: small (0.3 - 0.35 cm), average (0.4 - 0.5 cm) and large (0.6 - 0.8 cm). Different sizes allow us to get different consistency stuffing.
