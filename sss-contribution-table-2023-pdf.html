Answer:

Payroll System

Explanation:

A payroll is a system is an application or program that helps the Human Resource or Accounting department of the company to easily come up with the total amount of salary each employee or worker will receive during every payday.

A good payroll system should be a well-written script that will not only multiply the rate of pay with the number of work hours/days rendered but it must also automatically compute the deductions for each employee based on the contributions for their mandatory premiums for SSS, Philhealth, Pag-ibig, insurances, and/or loans.
