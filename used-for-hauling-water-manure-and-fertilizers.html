25. used for hauling fertilizer and soil ​


Answer:

WHEEL BARROW

Explanation:

Wheelbarrows for farms and agricultural holdings, landscaping, animal feed transportation, hay and straw. They resist to corrosive materials, and they are equipped with big capacity tray in order to carry quickly bulky and voluminous loads. Most often equipped with 2 wheels to reduce strains and onerousness during use.

The wheelbarrow is designed to distribute the weight of the load it is carrying between the wheel and the operator. This means that heavier or bulkier loads which would have been impossible to carry by hand are now easier to manoeuvre.
