KARLHEINZ STOCKHAUSEN

Music that has faced resistance because of its heavy atonal content without a clear melody or rhythm is Karlheinz Stockhausens music. Karlheinz Stockhausen was remarkable in that there was no discernible melodic or rhythmic sense in his music. Karlheinz Stockhausen had a musical aesthetic that was influenced by serial and electronic music.

During his lifetime, Stockhausen had a significant impact on avant-garde music in general. Stockhausen's "compositional series" and his contribution to musical theory, particularly in the areas of serialism and analytical music, are his lasting legacies. The greatest problem facing Wegener was the lack of direct evidence for the movements of continents (no GPS at the time!) and no mechanism was known to be powerful enough to move entire continents. The main reason that Wegener's hypothesis was not accepted was because he suggested no mechanism for moving the continents. He thought the force of Earth's spin was sufficient to cause continents to move, but geologists knew that rocks are too strong for this to be true.
