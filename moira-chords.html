26. Ben answered the module in mathematics 4 hours less than thrice as fast as moira. if moira answered the module in 3 hours, how long would it Ben to answer the module?


Ben would take 5 hours to answer the module.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem

The problem is asking how long it would take Ben to answer the module. To find the answer, translate the given mathematical phrase to a mathematical symbol.

Given:

Moira - 3 hours

Ben - 4 hours less than thrice as fast as Moira

Equation:

To translate the mathematical phrase to a mathematical symbol, you have to look for the signal words. The signal words "thrice" means that you have to multiply Moira's number of hours by 3 and "less than" means that you have to subtract 4 from it.

Ben = 3 (3 hrs) - 4 hrs

Solution:

Ben = 3 (3 hrs) - 4 hrs

= 9 hrs - 4 hrs

= 5 hrs

Final Answer:

5 hours
