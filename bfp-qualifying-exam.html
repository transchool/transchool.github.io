Step-by-step explanation:

5.)

The statement "I will go if he stays" is a conditional statement. It means that the speaker's decision to go is dependent on the other person staying.

6.)

The statement "We leave whenever he comes" is also a conditional statement. It means that the speaker's decision to leave is dependent on the other person coming.

7.)

The statement "You will qualify GATE only if you work hard" is a conditional statement. It means that the speaker believes that the person will only qualify if they work hard.

8.)

The statement "If you are intelligent, then you will pass the exam" is also a conditional statement. It means that the speaker believes that if the person is intelligent, they will pass the exam.
