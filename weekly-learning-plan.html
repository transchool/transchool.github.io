13. what are the essential elements of the weekly home learning plan will you need to look for​


What are the essential elements of the weekly home learning plan will you need to look for  

The weekly home learning plan shall be prepared by teachers who incorporate distance learning and blended learning. It allows teachers and parents to keep track of general learning processes in school and out of school on a regular basis, as they introduce the most effective and feasible alternative learning methods. These modules are self-contained study units or sets, most suitable for individual students, graduates and undergraduates. A learning experience is offered to students when a group or individual training is not available.

14. paano nakakatulong ang “Weekly Home Learning Plan” o WHLP sa pagsasagot mo ng modyul?​


Paano Nakakatulong ang Weekly Home Learning Plan sa Pagsasagot ng Modyul?

Ano ang Weekly Home Learning plan?

Ang weekly home learning plan ay isang pamamaraan at pagpaplano kung saan naioorganisa ang pang-araw araw na gawain ng estudyante. Nakapaloob dito kung ano ang nararapat na gawin ng mag-aral para sa araw na iyon at kung ano ang mga kinakailangan niyang tapusin. Nakasaayos na din sa plano na ito ang mga gawain,paksa, asignatura at proyekto para sa buong isang linggo, ito ay upang magkaroon ang estudyante ng komprehensibong pag-unawa sa kanyang pinag-aaralan.

Paano nakakatulong ang Weekly Home Learning Plan sa Pagsasagot ng Modyul?Nagkakaroon ng maayos na pamamahala ng oras.Natutupad ang iskedyul ng pagtuturo.Hindi nahuhuli ang estudyante sa paksa.Nakakasabay ang estudyante sa itinuturo ng guro.Hindi nagsisiksikan ang mga dapat gawin o tapusin.Nakakapag-isip at nakakapagsagot ng mas maayos ang estudyante.Hindi namomroblema sa pagsusumite sa huling oras.Mas naiiwasan ang tukso ng pangongopya.Maayos na nagagawa ang mga tungkulin bilang mag-aaral.Nagiging disiplinado ang mag-aaral sa oras at paggawa.

Paano Gumawa ng Weekly Home Learning Plan?

Ang paggawa ng Weekly Home Learning Plan ay ibinabase sa programang aktuwal o kasulukuyang pinag-aaralan ng klase. Kariniwang ginagawa ito ng guro at ibinibigay sa mga estudyante upang matulungan sila na mas mabisang makapag-aral, makapagsagot at makapagsumite sa  oras. Nakapaloob sa ibaba ay ilan sa mga hakbang na kinakailangan ikonsidera sa paggawa ng plano.

Alamin kung ano ang dapat at kinakailangan na matutunan ng estudyanteMagtakda ng layunin ayon sa kayang makamit ng estudyante.Bigyan ng kalayaan ang estudyante na pumili kung paanong paraan ng pag-aaral ang mas makakatulong sa kanila.Suriin ang kakayanan ng estudyante.Subaybayan kung ano ang mga natapos na at hindi pa natatapos.Hikayatin ang mga mag-aaral sa positibong pamamaraan upang mas ganahan sila na tapusin ang mga plano.

21. effectiveness of weekly home learning plan in the modular distance learning was achored to what?​


Answer:

What makes the Weekly Home Learning Plan essential to the process of Teaching and Learning in the new modality? What are the essential elements of the Weekly Home Learning Plan will you need to look for?

Reflection: The Weekly Home Learning Plan (WHLP) is essential to the process of teaching and learning in the new modality since it serves as a blueprint a concrete plan to guide the learners, parents, learning facilitators and stakeholders on what subject, objective and activities should the learner do and accomplish to meet the required competencies in the Modular Learning Modality. The school had provided learning modules or self-learning modules in print and e copy for those who have the device. The school also had provided supplementary materials for the learners to help them on their learning. The parents have conveyed their support in the implementation of this learning modality and expressed their willingness to be partners in carrying out the plans for the modular distance learning. As the parents monitor the progress of the learners, the weekly home learning plan will be their basis to track down the day to day activities of their children. The Weekly Home Learning Plan should have this essential Elements:     

Date and Time indicates schedule of a particular learning area, learning competency and learning tasks. Learning Area which are the subjects required to be taken by the learners of a particular grade level. Learning Competency –that should be anchored on the Most Essential Learning Competencies (MELC’s) Learning Tasks- are formative learning tasks that creates opportunities for the learners to practice and enhance the acquired knowledge and skills Mode of Delivery- refers to the method of submission of learning outputs
