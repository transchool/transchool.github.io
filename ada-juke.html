16. what is meaning of kwentong ada


Answer:

Fairy Tales

Explanation:

"Kwentong Ada" is the Filipinism for fairy tales. Fairy tales are imaginative stories for children that typically features imaginary beings and lands.

Some of the more known fairy tales or kwentong ada in the Philippines are the following:

The Creation Story - where the first man and woman emerged out of a bamboo. This is also the origin of the phrase "putok sa buho".The Flood Story - where Lumawig's sons caused the flooding of the Earth. How the Tingguian Learned to Plant - where Kadaklan sent Kaboniyan to teach Dayapan how to plant rice.

Filipino fairy tales differ from western fairy tales as most of our stories are unique to our own culture and varied depending on which region they are from. There are religious overtones as well since ancient Philippines was deeply religions and devout to worshiping various deities.
