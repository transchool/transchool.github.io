17. summary for the shawl for anita


Summary of Shawl for Anita

'A Shawl for Anita' is a short story about the mother of Anita who loves to knit. She felt a sense of uselessness in life and felt as if she was living for nothing anymore when her mother grew older. Anita then decided to ask her mother to knit a shawl for her. The narrator of the story, who is Anita's older sister thought it was a silly thing to do because their mother is very old to knit anymore. But Anita's purpose in doing so is for their mother to regain her purpose in life by doing the things she loves to do. Anita thought that by so doing, their mother can reminisce the joys she had before and once again discover new joys in her continuing life.

19. What is the theme of a shawl of Anita?


'A Shawl of Anita' is a short story about Anita's mother who loves to knit. When her mother grew older, she felt a sense of uselessness in life and felt as if she was living for nothing anymore. Anita then decided to ask her mother to knit a shawl for her. Her older sister, who is the narrator of the story, thought it was a silly thing to do because their mother is very old to knit anymore. But Anita's purpose in doing so is for their mother to regain her purpose in life by doing the things she loves to do. Anita thought that by so doing, their mother can reminisce the joys she had before and once again discover new joys in her continuing life.

The theme of the story focuses on family love and finding our purpose in life. It also talks about compassion towards one another and the passions we can develop as an individual, as well as the great love of a mother to her children.

27. What is the theme of a shawl of Anita?


THEME OF THE STORY, "SHAWL OF ANITA"

Words to fonder:

shawl - This is a fabric-woven piece that is usually wrapped around a lady's shoulder or head. It is sometimes use to wrapped a baby. Travelers like to wear shawls to protect them from either dust, heat or cold.THEME- It is the very idea or the moral that the story wants to convey.

"SHAWL OF ANITA" is a story about a single mother who raised her own children despite her fragility. Anita is the youngest among the siblings and is sickly making her get more attention by the mother. The narrator tells about how she envied her younger sister for the affection that she gets.

It is also stated that years later, Anita got married but the narrator saw how clingy and dependent is she to her mother even if she is old and could hardly move. She even ask her to make a shawl that made her rage of anger. In the end Anita explained how she saw her mother become lonely when she has nothing to do. Asking her to do a shawl is not being demanding but making her mother busy and avoid boredom. Then, realization came upon the narrator.

THEME: A mother's service and labor for her children is innate and will never stop. This gives energy and fuel her to live. That is her essence.
