18. ano ang kahulugan ng viva la independencia filipina


Viva La Independencia is a Spanish phrase that means "Long Live Philippine Independence". This phrase is very remarkable in the Philippine history because this very phrase was etched by Andres Bonifacio at the Pamitinan Cave located in Montalban, Rizal. The founding members of the KKK, or Katipunan in short, gathered in the said cave. Andres Bonifacio, who stood as the leader of the rebellious organization, wrote this phrase on the walls of the cave to express the ultimate goal of KKK - Philippine Independence.

30. #oA B C D E F GH AA minor chordmidolabolollamimi044dodola1(A-C-E)(C-E-A)(E-C-A)1. What are the notes that formed the a minor chord?2. How do the C major chord and a minor chord differ?​


Answer:

1.To find a minor chord, start by building a major chord. Do this by identifying the 1st, 3rd, and 5th notes in the scale. To get a minor chord, simply move the 3rd note down the fretboard by a half step, by one fret. For example, in the C major scale, the notes are C, E and G.

2.The difference between a major and minor chord comes down to one, simple change: the 3rd in a scale. A major chord contains the 1st, 3rd, and 5th degree of the major scale. A minor chord contains the 1st, flattened 3rd, and 5th degree of the major scale of that note.
