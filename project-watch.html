2. why is project watch being implemented nationwide?​


Explanation:

Project Watch is being implemented nationwide in order to help law enforcement agencies better track and predict crime patterns. By analyzing real-time data from a variety of sources, such as crime reports, surveillance footage, and even social media activity, law enforcement agencies can identify emerging trends, which can help them better allocate resources and take preventative measures to reduce crime. Additionally, Project Watch provides a central platform for law enforcement to share information and collaborate with each other, which can help them better identify, target, and apprehend criminals.
