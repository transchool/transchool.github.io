20. Compare and contrast the notion of Roe’s Theory and Child-rearing Theory


Compare and contrast the notion of Roe’s Theory and Child-rearing Theory

Difference:

-Roe's Theory

Roe's Personality Theory is a theory that explains how people behave. According to Anne Roe's Personality Theory, a person's career is determined by their interactions with their parents. Roe felt that a child's interactions with their parents would determine whether they pursued person-oriented or non-person-oriented careers.

-Child-rearing Theory

The four primary parenting styles — permissive, authoritative, neglectful, and authoritarian — are based on the work of developmental psychologist Diana Baumrind and Stanford scholars Eleanor Maccoby and John Martin.

Each parenting style has a different impact on children's behavior and can be distinguished by certain characteristics, such as responsiveness (how warm and sensitive parents are to their children's needs) and demandingness (how much control parents exert over their children in an attempt to control them).
