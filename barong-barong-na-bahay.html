21. Henry bought a barong with 15℅ discount or 150 the barong must have a tag price of 1,000​


Answer:

850

Step-by-step explanation:

price = 1,000

discount= 15% or .15

final price = ?

1,000 × .15 = 150

the barong has 150 discount price because 150 is 15% of 1,000

subtract the discount price to the original price which is 1,000

1,000 - 150 = 850

850 is the final price
