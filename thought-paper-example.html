Explanation:

Reaction or response papers are designed so that you will consider carefully what you think or feel about something you've read or seen. It is giving something you have read or heard honest opinion or insights whether in a positive way or from a negative point of view.

Here are the steps on how to make a good reaction or response paper.

PART 1: A SUMMARY OF THE WORK

Identify the author and title of the work. Write an informative summary of the material. Condense the content of the work by highlighting its main points and key supporting points.Use direct quotations from the work to illustrate important ideas. Summarize the material so that the reader gets a general sense of all key aspects of the original work. Do not discuss in great detail any single aspect of the work, and do not neglect to mention other equally important points. Also, keep the summary objective and factual. Do not include in the first part of the paper your personal reaction to the work.

PART 2: YOUR REACTION TO THE WORK

Focus on any or all of the following questions. Check with your instructor to see if s/he wants you to emphasize specific points.\Make sure each major paragraph presents and then develops a single main point. Support any general points you make or attitudes you express with specific reasons and details.

For more information about the proper ways of writing a response or reaction paper, please visit this link https://brainly.ph/question/220787.
