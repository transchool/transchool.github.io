2. 11. Ms. Midori needs poster paints and brushes for her art class. The cost of one poster paint is P35 and the cost of one brush is P12, at most how many poster paint can she buy if she needs 17 brushes and she only has a P1000 budget?A. at most 20 poster paints B. at most 26 poster paints C. at most 22 poster paints D.at most 30 poster paints​


Answer:

C. at most 22 poster paints

Step-by-step explanation:

Cost of 17 brushes:

12 x 17 = 204.00

Change from 1,000.00:

1000 - 204 = 796.00

Max paints available to buy:

796 / 35 = 22

Change:

796 - (35x22) = 796 - 770 = 26.00 (not enough to buy 1 more paint)

contemporary practices

Contemporary practices blend studio-based approaches of conceptual discovery and aesthetic research with skill-based instruction—from drawing to new technologies. Contemporary Art Methods (CAP) is a term that refers to a wide range of creative practices used by artists working in galleries, museums, public spaces, online, and a variety of other art-making and exhibition venues. This field, as it is now known, supports an open and critical examination of the world we live in. Today's artists are no longer constrained by traditional means of expression such as drawing, painting, or sculpture, and contemporary art practice reflects this transformation by incorporating a wide range of old and new materials. To study and understand our current circumstances, artists use media from both everyday life and new technologies.
