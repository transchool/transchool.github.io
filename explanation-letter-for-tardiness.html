20. Justice is tarried when the filing of cases is delayed. What word in the sentence is synonymous is with tarried?


Answer:

The word that is synonymous with tarried is delayed.

Explanation:

Justice is tarried when the filing of cases is delayed.

The given sentence above tells that the time for justice is dependent with the filing of case. For this reason, when the filing of case is delayed, justice will be delayed as well. Therefore, the verb tarried should be similar to the word delayed.

Definition of Words:

Tarry verb \ˈta-rē\

To delay an action or an activity being done.

Examples:

The teachers tarry the examination because of the weather.

Nelson’s bank transactions got tarried.

Delay noun \ di-ˈlā\

To slow down or inhibit an action, event, or process.

Example:

The medication delayed the spread of infection.

I have to delay our meeting today.
