6. what parts of the microscope are being connected by the inclination joint


The arm and base of the microscope are being connected by the inclination joint.

The inclination joint also allows the microscope to be tilted backwards and due to this function, you can easily use the microscope even when you were seated.

This function allows one to adjust the microscope in an inclined position, as desired by the user without moving the microscope from one place to another.

However, one must be cautious when dealing with wet slides as the movement might smear the liquid in the slide.
