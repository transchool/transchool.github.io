14. Direction. Identify what are the parts of shuttlecock and size?NOTE: Please follow the numbering below. WRITE YOUR ANSWER IN CAPITAL LETTER.​


Answer:

HEAD

The head of a shuttlecock is the rounded, conical part at the top of the shuttlecock. It is typically made of cork, covered with leather or synthetic materials, and has a diameter of approximately 25 mm.

SKIRT

The skirt of a shuttlecock is the conical part below the head, which is made up of 16 feathers or synthetic materials, which are attached to the head. The feathers are arranged in a circular pattern and have a length of approximately 70 mm.

BOTTOM

The bottom of a shuttlecock is the flat, circular part at the base of the skirt. It is typically made of cork or rubber and has a diameter of approximately 28 mm.

Overall, the size of a shuttlecock is approximately 73 mm in height and 58 mm in diameter.
