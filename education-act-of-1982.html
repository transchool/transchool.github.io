18. What is the year 1982 in Roman Numerals?


1982 in Roman numerals is MCMLXXXII.

Answer:

1982 in Roman numerals is MCMLXXXII.

Step-by-step explanation:

The conversion of 1982 into Roman Numerals includes breaking the number according to its place value.

Thousands = 1000 = M

Hundreds = 900 = CM

Tens = 80 = LXXX

Ones = 2 = II

Number = 1000 + 900 + 80 + 2 = M + CM + LXXX + II = MCMLXXXII
