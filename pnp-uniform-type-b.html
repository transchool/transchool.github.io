Why PNP members must wear uniform properly wherever they may be?

PNP members must wear uniform properly wherever they may be in order to show authority, sincerity to their jobs and in order to tell the people that they are PNP members. They must wear uniform properly because people may not recognize them if they are wearing civilian or simple clothes while they are on duty. 17. Mixtures can be classified in to two types: uniform and non-uniform .They are different because ________.​


Answer:

The difference between uniform and non-uniform mixtures is the degree at which the materials are mixed and the sameness of their composition throughout the mixture. Another word for a uniform mixture is a homogeneous mixture. On the other hand, non-uniform mixtures are also called heterogeneous mixtures.
