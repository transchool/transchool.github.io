R = 22 kΩ x (1000 Ω/1 kΩ) = 22,000 Ω

I = 10 mA x (1 A/1000 mA) = 0.01 A

V = IR

V = (0.01 A) (22,000 Ω)

V = 220 V

26. What is the equation that describes the variation statement, the variable p is directly proportional to the square of q?  a. p = kq² b. p = kq c. p = k / q² d. p = k / q ​


Answer:

A. p = kq²

Step-by-step explanation:

Directly proportional means if the number increases, the other number also increases and if the number decreases, the other number also decreases. The formula is x = ky, where k is the constant of variation.

while,

Inversely proportional means if the number increases the other number decreases and if the number decreases the other number increases. The formula is x= k/y, where k is the constant of variation.

Variable p is directly proportional to the square of q

p = kq² A.
