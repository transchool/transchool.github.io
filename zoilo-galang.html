1. ANSWER Zoilo M. Galang


Zoilo Galang was a Filipino writer from Pampanga. He is credited as one of the pioneering Filipino writers who worked with the English language. He is the author of the first Philippine novel written in the English language, A Child of Sorrow, published in 1921.

3. Zoilo Galang's A Child of Sorrow was famous for being:


Zoilo Galang, a Filipino author wrote the novel ‘A Child of Sorrow’. This novel was published in 1921 and has been famous to be the first Philippine novel written in English.

 

The novel is a story about two lovers Rosa and Lucio, who have experienced great trials in their relationship which came to an end when Oscar who was a rival of Lucio with Rosa raped her helplessly. Rosa and Lucio still in love with each other felt deep regret with what happened to their relationship. Rosa wept every day since the day Lucio bid goodbye while Lucio drowned himself to work. The story ended in a tragic way when Rosa died because of sorrow.
