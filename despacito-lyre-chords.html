10. despacito meaning in tagalog


[tex]\huge\red{\overbrace{\underbrace{\tt\blue{\:\:\:\:\:\:\:\: KASAGUTAN\:\:\:\:\:\:\:\:}}}}[/tex]

Sa Espanyol, ang pang-abay na despacito ay ang maliit na anyo ng despacio, "mabagal," na nagbibigay ng kahulugan nito ng "dahan-dahan" isang banayad, malambot na konotasyon. Ang Despacito ay isang napakalaking tanyag na awit na may wikang Espanyol na 2017 sa pamamagitan nina Luis Fonsi at Daddy Yankee, na kalaunan ay nag-remix ng superstar na si Justin Bieber.

19. characteristics of salsa (Despacito)?​


Answer:

Salsa music:

has a time signature of 4/4

uses clave rhythms (usually played by claves) which provide the rhythmic foundation

has melodic and rhythmic syncopation

uses simple harmony based on repeated chord patterns, often in the minor key

uses call and response vocals. The leader sings a line (the call) and is answered by a chorus (the response)

usually has a structure of simple melodies followed by coro (improvised sections)

uses lyrics of love and everyday life
