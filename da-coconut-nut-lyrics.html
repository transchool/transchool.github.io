4. Da coconut nut song identify its melody, texture, dynamics and tempo​


Answer:

Da coconut nut is composed by Ryan Cayabyab, a famous Filipino National Artist. This song is popularized by Smokey Mountain in 1991.

Melody: it has an upbeat and light melody written in key of D♭ Major

Texture: polyphonic texture (Polyphonic texture, is when there are multiple independent melodies being played or sung at the same time)

Dynamics: Da coconut nut has an intense complexity where it have both high instrumentational and low instrumentational variety.

Tempo: 144 BPM with a time signature of 4 beats per bar
