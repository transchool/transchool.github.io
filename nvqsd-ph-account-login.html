Answer

This security threat is specifically known as Spyware. In general terms, it is called a Malware, a mix of the words malicious and software.

ExplanationWhat is Spyware?

A Spyware is a type of malware that invades the privacy of a computer user. It secretly collects personal information and computer activity which is sent to another computer. A Spyware runs in the background without the user knowing. It can monitor your activities on the computer and what you are typing through keylogging. This is considered risky because it makes the user vulnerable to criminal acts and cybercrime.

What is Malware?

Malware is any program or software that has only one purpose. To inflict damage on the computer, server, and/or network. Malware usually compromises the security and privacy of a computer making it more vulnerable to computer virus attacks. A computer inflicted with malware usually becomes slow in processing data.
