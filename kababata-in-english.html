1. Hindi. Sya ang kababata ko.

Sa pangungusap na ito ipinahahayag na hindi sya ang kabatata mo, marahil ay mayroong ka pang ibang kababata na tinutukoy.

14. the purpose of the written in writing this poem sa akong mga kababata​


Answer:

Sa Aking Mga Kababata

By: Johnisaiah Platino

His love for poetry was awakened by his mother at a young age. That is why at the age of 8 he was able to write his first poem in Tagalog. Moreover, this is arguably one of the best poems Rizal has ever written.

The poem aims instill in young Filipinos the following:

Tell the importance of the Filipino language

Language is God's gift to us

We should love our Language

18. sa aking mga kababata pangtulong na kaisipan​


Answer:

Sa Aking Mga Kabatà" (English: To My Fellow Youth) is a poem about the love of one's native language written in Tagalog. It is widely attributed to the Filipino national hero José Rizal, who supposedly wrote it in 1868 at the age of eight.[1] There is no evidence, however, to support authorship by Rizal and several historians now believe it to be a hoax.[2] The actual author of the poem is suspected to have been the poets Gabriel Beato Francisco or Herminigildo Cruz.[3]
