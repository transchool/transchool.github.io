29. what do you know about the university of southern mindanao​


The University of Southern Mindanao (USM) is a public research university located in Kabacan, Cotabato in the Philippines. It was established in 1967 as the Mindanao State College of Agriculture (MSCA) and later changed its name to the Southern Mindanao Agricultural College (SMAC) before becoming the University of Southern Mindanao (USM) in 1978.

USM offers undergraduate, graduate and doctoral degree programs in various fields of study, including agriculture, engineering, arts, sciences, education, and business. Some of the notable programs offered by USM include Agriculture, Forestry, and Environmental Science, Food Science and Technology, and Veterinary Medicine.

The university is recognized for its research in the field of agriculture and its extension services to the local community. It has a strong emphasis on research and innovation and has several research centers, including the Center for Research and Development, the Center for Innovations in Agriculture and the Environment, and the Center for Food Safety and Quality.

USM is accredited by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and has been recognized as a Center of Development for Agriculture and a Center of Excellence for Environmental Science by the CHED. It has also been recognized as one of the top performing schools in the Licensure Examination for Teachers (LET) and the Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering Board Exam.
