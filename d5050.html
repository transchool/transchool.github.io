26. 5 is what percent of 50 given:p5 B50 R? R=P/B=5/50 5÷5050/0 .1 =10.-->10% R=10%​


Answer:

5 is 10% of 50. The equation is P/B = 5/50, which simplifies to 0.1 or 10%.

Why?:

We can write the given sentence "5 is 10% of 50" as a fraction:

5/50

This fraction represents the part-to-whole relationship where 5 is the part and 50 is the whole. We can also write this fraction as a ratio of P (part) to B (whole):

P/B = 5/50

To simplify this ratio, we can divide both the numerator and the denominator by 5:

P/B = 1/10

Hence, we can say that 5 is 10% of 50, which is represented by the ratio P/B = 1/10 or 10%.
