A hypothesis is a statement guess that can be tested. A research hypothesis is a statement prediction that will be tested by research. Read literature related to the topic before formulating a research hypothesis. An effective hypothesis can be verified. It is also valid and reliable.

Types of Hypothesis and Examples

These are the types of hypothesis and examples:

Null hypothesis

The temperature affects plant pigmentation.

Fertilizers affect plant growth.

There is a significant relationship between salary and job satisfaction among the respondents.

Alternative hypothesis

There is no significant relationship between temperature and plant pigmentation.

There is no significant relationship between fertilizers on plant growth.

There is no significant relationship between salary and job satisfaction among the respondents.

Steps of Hypothesis Testing

These are the steps of hypothesis testing:

State the Null Hypothesis.  State the Alternative Hypothesis.  Identify the Significance Level  Compute the test statistic and corresponding P-Value.  Analyze the result for the conclusion.
