19. Reasons why we should trust online grading system​


Answer:

Grades help students to see where they have a clear understanding of the subject matter, and where they are falling short. Used well, letter grades can give them the motivation to succeed.

Teachers use online grading systems to keep parents (and kids) informed about what students are doing in class and how well they are doing it. Administrators say the improved communication makes students more accountable for their work and eliminates unwelcome "surprises" on report cards.
