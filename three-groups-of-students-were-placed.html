3. Three groups of students were placed in a classroom with controlled room temperatures of 18°C,20°C,25°C. The math exam scores of the students were then taken and compared to the other groups. What is the Independent Variable , Dependent Variable and Extraneous variable?


Answer:

﻿IV: Room Temperature DV: Math Exam Scores EV: Student's IQ/Abilitiesv

Explanation:

The independent variable would be the room temperature. The dependent variable is the scores they attained in exams. The extraneous variable is students' intelligence.

What is an independent variable?

These are the elements that do not depend on others. These are the data that will not affect the overall score of a study, test, or theory. In fact, independent variables can stand alone and will not affect the overall result of a study.

Independent variable: Room Temperature

The independent variable from the question would be room temperature. Every student's intelligence and ability to answer the exam will not depend on the temperature. Although the temperature may affect their performance and concentration, we cannot jump to conclusion that the amount of temperature in a given room can affect their overall scores. Their scoring abilities do not depend on room temperature.

What is a dependent variable?

The dependent variable on the other hand is the one that depends on other elements. The final result would be affected by these variables and could even change the whole outcome of the study. It is also the element that is being measured to satisfy whether the hypothesis is correct or not.

Dependent variable: Math Exam Scores

Since the goal of the said exam is to compare and measure the intelligence of students, the best dependent variable that would help decide the researcher are the students' exam scores. Scores are the element that would give a reasonable explanation of whether the intelligence quotient of individuals is high or not.

What is an extraneous variable?

It is the variable that is not part of the study or investigation but could change the result of the study. This kind of variable plays a very important role in concluding a given study. It also has great potential in deciding the result of the study or investigation.

Extraneous variable: Students' intelligence

Students' percentage of intelligence determines their scores. Although their intelligence is not part of the investigation, it can affect the overall result of the study because their scores depend on their intelligence.
