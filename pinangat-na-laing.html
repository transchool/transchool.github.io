PINANGAT FESTIVAL

CAMALIG ALBAY

JULY 20-27

An annual festival celebrated every month of June 10-24 to coincide with the town fiesta in honor of its Patron Saint, St. John the Baptist. This festival features various sports, cultural, historical, and religous activities, primarily to boost the tourism industry in Camalig.
