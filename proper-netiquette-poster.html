7. How will you show proper netiquette while using audio and video conferencing​


Showing a proper netiquette while in an audio or video conferencing can be done in the following:

1. Stay in focus. Keep from unnecessary distractions

2. Click the "mute" button if it is not your turn to talk.

3. Don't use code names or alias to hide your identity

4. If you want to ask something or state suggestions, ask permission to the moderator to talk.
