3. police administration and management​


police administration and management​

Police administration refers to the control and operation of law enforcement agencies, and the subsequent discharge of policies that keep the peace, increase public safety, and prevent crime.

Management focuses on managing people and their work. On the other hand, administration focuses on making the best possible utilization of the organization's resources.
