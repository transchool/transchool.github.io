29. saan matatagpuan ang kilalang Isla sa ng de Los siete pecados​


Answer:

Iloilo

Ang Isles of the Seven Sins, na mas kilala bilang Siete Pecados, ay isang grupo ng pitong isla na matatagpuan sa pagitan ng mga bayan ng Buenavista, Guimaras at Dumangas, Iloilo. Ito ay isang umuunlad na atraksyong panturista kasama ng isa pang maliit na pulo na tinatawag na Roca Enchantada (o Enchanted Rock) na may kalayuan patungo sa dalampasigan.

30. 2. How did the seven daughters solve this initial conflict?the legend of the siete pecados​


Answer:

According to legend, the seven islands of “Siete Pecados” (Spanish for “Seven Sins”) emerged in the wake of seven sisters who went swimming in the area against their mother’s orders and drowned.

Now, Siete Pecados booms with life as seen in the abundance of corals in all shapes, sizes, and color. Catch sight of various species of fish, including “butandings” (whale sharks) that sometimes wander into the vicinity. As the nearest marine park from Coron town, Siete Pecados is a favorite snorkeling spot of locals and tourists alike.
