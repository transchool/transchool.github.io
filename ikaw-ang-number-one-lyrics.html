12. one sixth of a nimber is one-half of the same number less 3. ehat is the number?


Answer:

9/2

Step-by-step explanation:

let x = the number

1/6 of a number

[tex]( \frac{1}{6} )x[/tex]

1/2 of the same number less 3

[tex]( \frac{1}{2} )(x - 3) [/tex]

solve:

x/6 = (x-3)/2

x=3x-9

[tex]x = \frac{9}{2} [/tex]
