11. list down the different symbols used in the lyrics and what do you think are their meanings? ( Ang pipit?​


Answer:

1. The bird in the song represents the struggling lives of Filipinos.

2. May pumukol sa pipit sa sanga ng isang kahoy

The ‘kahoy’ or wood symbolizes the home of the bird. The branch or twigs of the tree depicts that even on the comfort of their own home, the birds are still hurt by their very own home. In representation, since the ‘pipit’ or the bird are the Filipinos, the Filipinos themselves are experiencing hardships in their own home or country.  

3.  Pumokol na bato

The stone in the song is not part of the tree, and so this is symbolized as a foreign element. It is known that the country suffered through centuries of colonization from foreign lands.

4.  “Pag pumanaw ang buhay ko, may isang pipit na iiyak”

This line represents a plea of mercy to spare them for the future of their family. Since Filipinos are family-oriented, they have to survive to ensure their family’s survival as well.

12. ibig sabihin ng corral belaching??


Answer:

When water is too warm, corals will expel the algae (zooxanthellae) living in their tissues causing the coral to turn completely white. This is called coral bleaching. When a coral bleaches, it is not dead. Corals can survive a bleaching event, but they are under more stress and are subject to mortality.

19. Ano ang nagawa ni jose manuel corrales montalvan


Jose Manuel Corrales Montalvan, nang sumali siya sa US Army noong 1928 bilang isa sa iilang Philippine Scout Officers. Nagbitiw siya sa kanyang komisyon at sumapi sa Hukbo ng Pilipinas noong 1936 ayon sa hinihingi ng pamahalaang Komonwelt ng Pilipinas at hinirang na Cadre Commander ng Camp Bulua na kalaunan ay pinalitan ng pangalan na Camp Evangelista at naging Camp Commander nito hanggang sa pagsiklab ng WWII.

Si Dr. Montalvan, na kilala rin bilang Ñor Peping, ay ipinanganak noong Marso 17, 1903, sa kasalukuyang Cagayan de Oro (na kilala noon bilang Cagayan de Misamis, ang kabiserang bayan ng Segundo Distrito de Misamis) kay Jose Gabriel Montalvan, isang retiradong sundalong Espanyol mula sa Belmonte, Cuenca, Spain na itinalaga ng pamahalaang Espanyol sa Pilipinas at Concepcion Corrales y Roa ng Cagayan de Misamis. Noong 1927, nagtapos siya ng honors (3rd highest) sa Philippine Dental College, Manila na may degree ng Doctor in Dental Surgery (DDS). Sa kanyang pagbabalik sa kanyang bayan noong 1928, nagpraktis siya ng dentistry at naging isa sa mga unang guro ng Ateneo de Cagayan (kasalukuyang Xavier University) at naging Commandant nito ng Corps of Cadets.

Para sa kanyang serbisyo militar bago at noong Ikalawang Digmaang Pandaigdig, si Dr. Montalvan ay tumanggap ng mga sumusunod na parangal at dekorasyon: Philippine Defense Medal; American Defense Medal; Asiatic-Pacific Campaign Medal, World War II Victory Medal; Philippine Republic Unit Citation Badge, ang U.S. Distinguished Unit Badge.
