1. Difference between apartelle and apartments


Answer

The differences between Apartelle and Apartment are the following:

An apartelle or apartment Hotel is a fully furnished apartment that uses a renting system as a Hotel does. This means that the person who's going to stay (leasee) can rent the place like its a hotel. However, an apartelle doesn't have the same service as a Hotel. The purpose of an apartelle is for people who are looking for a place to stay at a price cheaper than a hotel.An apartment is pretty much similar in structure with an apartelle. It is only different from an apartelle in terms of the contract of renting or leasing the place. Typically, the rent has a minimum duration of one year.

The Apartelle and the Apartment don't have those extravagant services seen in a Hotel. Both are just like leaving in your own house where you do your own cleaning, laundry, paying the electrical bills, and so on.
