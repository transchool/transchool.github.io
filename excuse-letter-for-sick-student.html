A format of writing a letter can depend on what the occasion is and where will the person? send it to. Formal letters require much more details from the sender, since it may be passed down to other people for consideration and for acknowledgment of what business a person had written in the letter. Non-formal ones have fewer details but shouldn’t miss the basic parts of a letter which is a greeting or introduction and recipient information.

Common Format for a Formal Letter:

Block Style  Main Address Present Date Greeting The name of the recipient Body of the letter Salutation

Example of an Excuse Letter:

Good Day,

Dear (Name of the Recipient),

I ( Your Name) would like to ask for your utmost consideration for my absence dated (Date of your absence). It was due to the reason of me catching the flu.  

Best Regards,

Ginang/ Ginoong (pangalan ng guro),

Ako po ay humihingi ng paumanhin at pang-unawa sapagkat ang anak ko  ay hindi nakapasok sa klase noong (ilagay mo ang petsa) dahil siya ay (ilagay mo kung ano ang dahilan).

Salamat po sa pang-unawa at konsiderasyon.

Ang sulat ay mas mainam lagyan ng mas malalim na eksplanasyon para sa nasabing dahilan at nararapat na nalagdaan rin ng iyong magulang upang ito ay mas bigyan konsiderasyon ng iyong guro.
