Answer:

C. 81/4 pi

Step-by-step explanation:

Given: d = 18 then r = 9

Asked: Area

Solution:

Get the circumference of the wire in semicircle

C =πr

C = π9 or 9π

Since same circumference will be used to form a circle then the circumference of the circle is 9π.

Please take note that circumference of a circle is given by the formula C = 2πr

Equate :

9π = 2πr

r = 9/2

Area of the circle = πr^2

A = π (9/2)^2

A = 81/4 π

A = 81/4 pi
