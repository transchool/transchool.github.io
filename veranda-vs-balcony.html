Solutions:

2. Asked: The total area of the veranda that will be covered with tiles and grass.

Given: 5 m x 6 m - dimensions of the rectangular garden

           6 m - diameter of the semi - circle garden

Operation: multiplication

Number Sentences: A = l x w - area of the rectangle

                                  A = πr²

Solutions:

Area of the Rectangle

A = l x w

A = 6 m x 5 m

A = 30 m²

Area of the Semi - Circle

A = πr²/2

A = (3.14)(m)²/2

A = (3.14)(3 m)²/2

A = (3.14)(9 m²)/2

A = 28.26 m²/2

A = 14.13 m²

Answer:

Therefore, the area that will be covered with tiles is 30 m².

Therefore, the area that will be covered with grass is 14.13 m².

= (3.14) (3.5 m)²

= 3.14 (12.25 m²)

= 38.465 m²

The area of the circle is 38.465 m².

Step 3: Subtract

49 m² - 38.465 m² = 10.535 m²

Final Answer:

The area of the shaded region is 10.535 m².
