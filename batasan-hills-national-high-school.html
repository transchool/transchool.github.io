28. what is followed on hills with high slpoes


Answer:

Contour System

Explanation:

Contour system: The contour is an imaginary line connecting the points of equal height on a slope. This system has been evolved for the planting of fruit trees on hilly and slopes topography with the objective to minimize soil erosion. The trees are planted on the bench terrace prepared on the contour. The one row of shrubs and grasses may be planted between two rows of fruit trees to reduce water flow and thereby soil erosion more effectively. The double hedge system accommodates about 22% more plants than a single hedge system.
