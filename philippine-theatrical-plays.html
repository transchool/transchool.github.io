An epic poem, or simply an epic, is a long narrative poem regarding extraordinary characters who, through their relationships with gods or other superhuman powers, gave shape to the fatal universe for their successors. Epic poetry is considered the pinnacle of Philippine folk literature, embracing accounts of tribal heroes' exploits. These epics are passed down down the generations by a small number of singers and chanters.

Western Classical

Western classical tradition (1650–1910) (obligatory). The western classical tradition is appointed as art music of (or evolving out of) the European tradition, generally notated, and normally intended for local performance for the purposes of this specification. This term refers to music composed in the late eighteenth and early nineteenth centuries and is marked by the creation of the sonata by composers such as Mozart. In primary usage, however, the phrase refers to any serious art music that is neither jazz, pop, nor folk music.

Philippine Theatrical

The Philippine theatre has thrive into a synthesis of different inspirations, including zarzuela, comedia, bodabil, and western classics. Theatre was primarily manifest in English during the period, as it had become an important aspect of classroom instruction. The Philippine opera is a dramatic piece of music performed in a theater stages. Murder, retribution, and romance are all recurrent themes. Philippine musical play are, in some ways, fruits of theater and are heavily influenced by broadway performances. Its themes include novel adaptations and the like.

Play

A play is a dramatic task that consists mostly of character conversation and is meant for theatrical performance rather than merely reading. Drama, classical or popular music, opera, musicals, ballet, modern dance, spoken word, circus, or any other task in which an artist connects with an audience can all be found in a theatre. Drama is a literary making in the form of prose or verse that depicts dialogue revealing conflict that the main character strives to resolve, whereas play is a dramatic performance on the stage.
