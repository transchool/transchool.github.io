17. explanation of niyog-niyogan​


Answer:

Niyogyugan is a provincial festival that combines all of Quezon's 39 municipalities and 2 cities into one. The name comes from niyog (coconut) and yugyog (to shake). A coconut dancing to a lively beat comes to mind. ... The booths are the main attraction since they're there for the whole duration of the festival.
