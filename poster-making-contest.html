4. In a poster making contest, you are required that the rectangular poster must have a perimeter of 64 inches. You need to find the dimensions of the poster if the length is 5 inches more than twice the width.


Answer: The dimension is 9 inch by 23 inch

Solution and Explanation:

In mathematics, the definition of perimeter is a path that encompasses/surrounds a two-dimensional shape. The formula to find the perimeter of rectangle is 2L + 2W or 2(L+W), where L is a length and W is a width of a rectangle.

In the given problem, first create a formula based on the given situation. Let's solve together.

The given based on the situation are:

Perimeter is 64 inch

Length is 5+2W, and the

Width is W

So, the formula is 2L+2W=P, then

2(5+2W) + 2W = 64, since L= 5+2W

Then simplify:

10+4W+2W = 64

6W + 10 = 64

6W = 54

W = 9

if L = 5+2W, therefore L= 5+2(9), L=23

Therefore, the dimension of the rectangle is 9 by 23 inches..

30. In a poster making contest, you are required that the rectangular poster must have a perimeter of 34 inches. You need to find the dimensions of the poster if the length is 2 inches more than twice the width.​


Given:

Perimeter of Rectangular Poster: 34 inches

Length: 2 inches more than twice the width

Formula of Perimeter of a Rectangle: 2L + 2WSolution:

1. Since the Perimeter of the Poster is given, we know that

                    2L + 2W = 34 inches

2. To find the length of the rectangular poster, let's make a formula based on the given. Thus,

                     L = 2 + 2W

3. Now, let's substitute the formula for length in the formula of the perimeter. Thus,

                     2 (2 + 2W) + 2W = 34

Cross multiply..

                     4 + 4W + 2W = 34

Combine like terms..

                     4W + 2W = 34 - 4

                     6W = 30

Remove the 6 in 6W by dividing it both side.

Now, we have..

                     W = 30/6

                     W = 5

4. We have the value of width which is 5. Now, let's find the value of the length by substitution.

                   2L + 2W = 34

                   2L + 2(5) = 34

                   2L + 10 = 34

Combine like terms..

                  2L = 34 - 10

                  2L = 24

Remove the 2 in 2L by dividing it both side.

                  L = 24/2

                  L = 12

5. Thus, the dimension of the rectangular poster is 12 (length) and 5 (width).

6. To check, substitute the value of length and width to the formula of perimeter.

                2L + 2W = 34

                2(12) + 2(5) = 34

                24 + 10 = 34

                34 = 34
