Answer:

Sales tax rate = 10.71%

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Total cost = ₱28,985.60

Sales tax = ₱3,105.60

Solution:

Formula

[tex]sales \: tax \: rate \: = \frac{value}{total \: value} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{₱3105.60}{₱28985.60} \times 100\%[/tex]

[tex] = \frac{3}{28} \times 100\%[/tex]

Sales tax rate = 10.71%
