13. An ice cream shop is testing some new flavors of ice cream. They invent 25 new flavors for customer to try,and throw out the 13 least popular flavors. The shop makes 80 cartons of each of the remaining flavors. It takes 2 cartons to get one liter of ice cream . How much total ice cream is in the cartons


Answer:

480 L

Step-by-step explanation:

Since the shop will throw out the 13 least popular flavors, that will leave us 25 - 13 or 12 left for them to produce.

Total Ice Cream = Flavors * Cartons * (1 Liter / 2 Cartons)

= 12 * 80 * 1 / 2

= 480

Therefore, there is a total of 480L of ice cream.

30. if ice cream is serve in a cup, in how many ways can loujean choose her five flavor ice cream if there are 5 available flavors?​


Answer:

1 ways can Loujean choose her flavor ice cream

Step-by-step explanation:

Given :

Total available flavor = 5Chosen flavor = 5

Solution :

So, we have select 5 flavours out of 5 flavours, and the no. of ways of selection will be : [tex]\begin{gathered} ^{5}C_{5} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered} \rm{C} = \frac{5!}{5!(5 - 5)!} \end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered} \rm{C} = \frac{5!}{5!}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered} \rm{C} = \frac{5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot \ 2 \cdot 1}{5 \cdot 4 \cdot 3 \cdot \ 2 \cdot 1}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered} \rm{C} = \frac{120}{120}\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered} \rm{C} = 1 \ ways \end{gathered}[/tex]
