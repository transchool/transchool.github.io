8. classification of kiskis​


Answer: Idiophone

Explanation:

Idiophone is a kind of instrument that can produce sound by "scratching", striking, or shaking it.

Kiskis, also known as bamboo scratcher, is an intrument that has ridges and is a percussion instrument. “Kiskis” is a Filipino word for “to scratch”.

To play the Kiskis, you rub a stick or a proper tool against the ridges of the Kiskis.


9. how it is played kiskis​


Answer:

KISKIS. Kiskis is a bamboo piece with ridges and serves as a percussion instrument. “Kiskis” is a Filipino word for “to scratch” or “to rub against something”. It is played by “rubbing” a rattan stick on its ridges.
