3. how did saint camillus de lellius served with charity


How Did Saint Camillus de Lellis Served with Charity?

Saint Camillus de Lellis served with charity by devoting the rest of his life after spiritual conversion to care for the sick. Being struck with an incurable disease himself, he entered San Giacomo Hospital for incurables (also known as St. James Hospital) in Rome. While being a patient there, he also became a servant and an assistant. When he was ordained to religious life, he founded a congregation of priests dedicated to hospital service.

Life of Saint Camillus de Lellis

There are many striking things about Saint Camillus de Lellis aside from being a patron saint of hospitals, sick, and nurses. Many of them are listed below:

Camillus was a soldier in Naples who was an excessive gambler.He had his conversion at the age of 25 with the help of Capuchin friars.He was then appointed as a superintendent of San Giacomo Hospital.His congregation established the first recorded military field ambulance.He was canonized in 1746.

14. what made Camillus decide to devoted his life to the care of the sick ?please help ​


Answer:

He was disgusted by the bad care in the hospital and decided a religious order devoted to helping the sick was the best way to better physical and spiritual care. 

Three times between 1571 and 1584 his abscessed leg forced him to seek care in a Roman hospital; each time he worked there during and after his treatment. He was disgusted by the bad care in the hospital and decided a religious order devoted to helping the sick was the best way to better physical and spiritual care.
