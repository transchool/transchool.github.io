17. What is SHOUTING? computer terms


Shouting in computer terms means you are talking to somebody (in a chat or post) and your words are written in capslock or upper case letters / characters. It is actually one of the netiquettes (Internet Etiquette or proper behavior online). As much as possible, it is not advisable for anybody to talk to someone using capslock or upper case letters as it is consider impolite and a form of disrespect.

Other proper netiquette includes respecting other ideas at all times. Everyone might not agree with you, so you must be sensitive and be mindful or other ideas. Second is, treat somebody as you will in real life. Virtual world is very different to the real world. Think of how you treat someone whether it is a virtual or real world and lastly, avoid posting the same words or images consecutively as it is considered spamming.
