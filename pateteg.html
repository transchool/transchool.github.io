5. What is the classification of Pateteg?


Answer:

Pateteg is a Filipino percussion instrument , also known as bamboo leg xylophone that is made of bamboo , and an instrument of Cordillera. The music made by pateteg is used for worship and rituals , work like planting , sleep and courtship. They belong to the idiophones , a classification of musical instruments which produce sound by vibrating themselves.
