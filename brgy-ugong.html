21. Why does Ugong refuse to wash the dishes?


Ka Ugong was a character in the book authored by Filomena Colendrino entitled Why do Women wash the dishes.

Ka Ugong whose real name is Hugo refused to wash the dishes one day while they were eating there lunch with his wife Ka Maldang whose real name is Imelda. It is because he is the husband and therefore he should have the authority in the house, and that Ka Maldang has a responsibility to take care of the dishes as a wife and a helper in the house.
