6. I WILL GIVE BRAINIEST 100 POINTS PLS ANSWER Ollie has 450 files saved on his laptop. Of those files, 12% are photos. a) Write a percent equation to find the number of files that are photos. b) How many photos does Ollie have saved on his laptop?


Answer:

a. ) 450 x 0.12

b. ) 54 photos

Step-by-step explanation:

a.) To convert a percent to decimal, you should divide it to 100, and that will give us 0.12

The formula for finding the percentage is to multiply the base (450 files) to the rate (12%)

So it should be like this: 450 x 0.12

b.) Evaluate.

450 x 0.12 = 54

Step-by-step explanation:

Files saved on laptop: 450

File photos: 12% of the total files

a.) Equation to solve for the number of files that are photos:

P = Rate × Base   ⇒  P = RB

Where:

P = Percentage or part of the base

R = rate in percent

B = base or principal value

Let  photos be P:

P = 12% × 450

or

P = 0.12 × 450

b.) Find the number of photos:

P = 0.12 × 450

P  = 54

There are 54 photos saved out of 450 files.

26. Between a VD and MUR which room would you prioritize to clean first? Explain your answer ​


Answer:

It would depend on the specific circumstances of the situation, but generally speaking, I would prioritize cleaning a room with a VD (Vacant Dirty) status over a room with a MUR (Making Up Room) status.

A VD room is a room that is currently unoccupied but needs to be cleaned before the next guest arrives. In this case, it is important to clean the room as soon as possible so that it is ready for the next guest and to ensure that the guest has a positive experience.

On the other hand, a MUR room is a room that is currently occupied but is due for cleaning. In this case, the guest is still in the room and the cleaning needs to be done discreetly and with minimal disruption to the guest.

Therefore, in order to ensure that the hotel is able to meet the needs of its guests and maintain a high level of cleanliness, I would prioritize cleaning the VD room first to make sure that it is ready for the next guest.

However, it is also important to consider the urgency of the situation, if the MUR room is needed to be cleaned urgently because the guest is checking out soon or the hotel is fully booked, it would be more appropriate to prioritize cleaning the MUR room first. The decision should be made after evaluating all the factors that are in play.
