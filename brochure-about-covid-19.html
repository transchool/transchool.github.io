2. community services brochure about covid-19​


Answer:

A Community Service Brochure is that small information guide provide in areas around the neighborhood or the community. It can appear as a small booklet or folded piece of construction paper which is the most common form it comes in. It could have information about anything from community programs, legal services, health clinic information schedules and many more. Usually the provider of that brochure prints their mission on one of the pages, mostly at the back. The aim of the brochure is to inform people about programs and activities that benefit the community.
