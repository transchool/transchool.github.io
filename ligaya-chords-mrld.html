Ricardo "Ric" Manrique Jr. was  a Kundiman singer in the Philippines.  He has been known as one of two Hari Ng Kundiman (Kings of Kundiman) in the Philippines, alongside Ruben Tagalog. He'd been a member  of the 1950s Mabuhay Singers.  He recorded his first album in the early 1960s,  and released with Villar Records most of his albums.  Manrique was best known for his songs,  which became successful Filipino movie theme songs.  The song entitled Ang Daigdig Ko'y Ikaw is the theme song from  the same-title 1965 movie that stars Fernando Poe Jr. and Susan Roces.  This was sung  alongside Pilita Corrales. Manrique died in California, United  States on 22 September 2017.

4. kayarian ng salitang ligaya​


KAYARIAN

kayarian ng ligaya

Salita: ligaya  

Kayarian: payak  

Salitang-ugat: ligaya

Panlapi: ---

Ang salitang "ligaya" ay isang salitang tumutukoy sa saya o galak na nararamdaman ng tao o hayop. Ang kayarian ng salitang "ligaya" ay payak dahil binubuo lamang ito ng isang salitang-ugat at walang panlapi. Ang isang pangngalan o pang-uri ay payak kung mayroon lamang itong salitang-ugat, walang panlapi at walang pantig na inuulit. Kung ang salita ay dadagdagan ng panlapi, halimbawa ay kaligayahan, ito ay magiging maylapi. Kung ang salita ay naging liligaya, ang kayarian nito ay inuulit.

19. Which chords are major chords and minor chords ​


ANSWER

MAJOR CHORDS V.S MINOR CHORDS

•A major chord has what's called a “natural third.” It's the third degree of the chord's respective major scale.

•A minor chord has what's called either a “minor third” or a “flat third.” It's the third degree of the chord's respective minor scale.
