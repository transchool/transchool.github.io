Audio-Video Conferencing

Video conferencing is an online conference format in which two or more individuals participate in a live audio-visual call. Participants can see, hear, and communicate with one other in real-time, no matter where they are in the world, if they have a strong internet connection.

Video conferencing is commonly used in business to communicate and cooperate both within and outside of an organization. To get the most out of the experience, you only need to set up the essential hardware and software.

Advantages

As you might expect, there are numerous advantages for businesses that use live video conferencing services. Let's look at some of the most important and well-known advantages of using video conferencing solutions for communication and collaboration.

Saves time and resources - It is critical for any business to have efficient and effective communication in order to function correctly.Employee productivity is increased – Internal communication has never been easier, faster, or more convenient than it is now, thanks to live video conferencing firms and video conferencing service providers. It has a lot of intangible benefits too – Along with increasing the productivity of the organization in general, live conferencing tools also have a lot of intangible benefits for any organization such as a strong sense of community due to the personal face-to-face conversation instead of only hearing the voice of the person they’re communicating with.

Disadvantages

Video conferencing, like any other technology, has its limitations and drawbacks. Even if most people would properly claim that the benefits of video conferencing solutions outweigh the downsides, it is necessary to examine both sides of the argument in order to have a well-developed perspective on everything.

It lacks the personal touch of face-to-face communication – While most live video conferencing systems get close to replicating the experience of personal face-to-face communication online, there is still a minor difference in the effectiveness of video conferencing and a face-to-face meeting.Even the most advanced systems are susceptible to technological issues — no technology is impermeable to flaws and faults. Even though most live video conferencing companies offer 24-hour assistance for any corporate system they install, problems can still occur, wasting time and resources for the firm.It has a high initial cost – Setting up an enterprise-grade video conferencing solution may be costly, as most solutions necessitate specialized hardware that must be installed, frequently in numerous locations.

Question answered correctly = [tex]\bold{16}[/tex]

Question on the quiz = 80%

100 - 80 = [tex]\bold{20}[/tex]

➢ 20% of x = [tex]\bold{16}[/tex]

➢ 20/100 xx = [tex]\bold{16}[/tex]

➢ x = [tex]\bold{\frac{16x100}{20}}[/tex]= [tex]\bold{80}[/tex]

Answer:

97

Step-by-step explanation:

(x + 73 + 75 + 89 + 91) ÷ 5 = 85

(x + 328) ÷ 5 = 85

x + 328 = 425

x = 425 - 328

x ≥ 97
