2. 2. The Chainsaw Act -​


Answer:

SEC. 1. Title. – This Act shall be known as the “Chainsaw Act of 2002”.

SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. – It is the policy of the State, consistent with the Constitution, to conserve, develop and protect the forest resources under sustainable management. Toward this end, the State shall pursue an aggressive forest protection program geared towards eliminating illegal logging and other forms of forest destruction which are being facilitated with the use of chainsaws. The State shall therefore regulate the ownership, possession, sale, transfer, importation and/or use of chainsaws to prevent them from being used in illegal logging or unauthorized clearing of forests.

18. RA 9163 is also known as the_? a.NSTP act of 2001 b.NSTP Act of 2002 c.NSTP act of 2003 d NSTP act of 2004?​


RA 9163:

Answer:

a. NSTP Act of 2001

Explanation:

Republic Act 9163 is a law regarding the National Service Training Program which is enacted in 2001. It is a program which aims to enhance civic consciousness and defense preparedness in youths through enhancing their patriotism and service ethics while on training. It covers three different programs: (1.) Civic Welfare Training Service (CWTS) (2.) Literary Training Service (LTS) and (3.) Reserve Officers Training Corps (ROTC). Though enacted in 2001, its implementation took place the following year. The main objective of this program is to improve civic consciousness and national defense preparedness among the Filipino youth specifically those that develop their total well - being as agents toward the pursuit of national development.
