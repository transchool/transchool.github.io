18. The price of a gallon of milk in 1970 was $0.76. The price of a gallon of milk today is $2.86. What is the percent of increase of the price of a gallon of milk from 1970 to today?


Answer:

276.32%

Step-by-step explanation:

To determine the percent increase between two values, we use the following formula:

[tex]\text{Percent increase} = \frac{\text{new value - old value}}{\text{old value}} * 100\%[/tex]

Substitute the given values,

[tex]\text{Percent increase} = \frac{2.86 - 0.76}{0.76} * 100\% \\\\\text{Percent increase} = \frac{2.10}{0.76} * 100\%\\\\\text{Percent increase} \approx 2.7632 * 100 \%\\\\\text{Percent increase} \approx 276.32 \%[/tex]

Therefore, the percent increase of the price of milk is approximately 276.32%.
