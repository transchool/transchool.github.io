What was the most interesting part of the story?5. What was the story about?6. Who narrated the story?​


Answer:

1. Where did the story happen?

There are several locations but the main setting was in the garden and in the kitchen.

Other locations were in Bulacan and in the dining area of Liza's house.

2. Who were the characters in the story?

The characters are:

Liza

Meg - Liza's younger sister

Tita Loleng

Liza's Father

Liza's Mother

Sylvia - Liza's father's concubine

Lem - Liza's half brother who died

3. What

Liza, from the story was originally close with his father until she found out that her father has another family.

4. What was the most interesting part of the story?

The most interesting part of the story for me as the reader is that the woman Liza's father had another child apologized to her. It is unusual for me to actually know that if you know you made a mistake towards another person, that person will take responsibility from that action and really apologize for what he has done.

5. What was the story about?

The story is about forgiveness. That in a family no matter how huge the mistake that was done towards you, there should always be room for forgiveness and acceptance, specially if whoever did you that mistake is a family member.

I think this story was also entitled "Sinigang" because similarly with the infamous pinoy dish, the taste of sinigang is sour but is a delicious treat to anyone who tries it. Just like in life, most of the time it may not be a smooth sailing life. We experience problems, but those problems is what makes us special. Our life may feel like the sourness of sinigang but if see our life in a bigger picture - it is beautiful.

6. Who narrated the story?

The story was narrated by Liza.
