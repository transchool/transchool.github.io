1. Mt babuyan claro active or not ​


Answer:

Status of Babuyan Claro

The current status of Babuyan Claro is active. It is one of the 24 active volcanoes of the Philippines, according to the latest list provided by PHIVOLCS.

Explanation:

Babuyan Claro is a stratovolcano located in Babuyan Island. This island contains two volcanoes – Babuyan Claro, and the other, Smith Volcano. The crater on this volcano is measured to be 500-meter wide, and one could find lots of hot springs around the volcano, with some still steaming heavily. Three historical eruptions are attributed to this volcano, but the ones in 1913 and 1831 are unconfirmed. The sole eruption that has been confirmed by scientists is the 1860 eruption.

3. 2 important events of claro recto​


Answer:

Claro Mayo Recto, (born Feb. 8, 1890, Tiaong, Phil.—died Oct. 2, 1960, Rome, Italy), statesman and leader of the “Filipino-first” movement that attacked U.S. “neo-colonialism” in the Philippines.

During the Japanese occupation in World War II, Recto served in the government of José Laurel. After the war, he was elected for two terms, in 1949 and 1955, to the Senate of the, by then, independent Philippines.During the presidency of Ramon Magsaysay (1953–57), he became prominent in the struggle against excessive U.S. influence on the islands.He campaigned for repudiation of the Bell Act, which gave the United States unequal trade advantages; demanded acknowledgment of Philippine ownership of U.S. military bases on the islands; and proposed the Omnibus Nationalization Act to nationalize almost every large economic enterprise, including foreign-owned ones. In 1957 Recto broke away from the Nacionalistas and joined the new Nationalist Citizens’ Party, advocating neutrality in foreign relations and economic independence from U.S. interests. He ran unsuccessfully as its candidate for president in 1957.

4. what in short is nationalism according to claro m. recto?​


Claro M. Recto, a prominent Filipino nationalist, defined nationalism as "the complete devotion and loyalty of a people to their country, their willingness to make sacrifices for its welfare, and their determination to safeguard its sovereignty and integrity." Recto believed that nationalism is an essential element in the development of a strong and independent nation, and that it is necessary for the protection and advancement of the interests of the people of that nation. For Recto, nationalism was not just a political or economic ideology, but a deeply rooted cultural and social phenomenon that embodied the collective aspirations and values of a people.

13. Ang Babuyan Claro ay isang uri ng anyong ​


Answer:

Anyong Lupa

Isang anyong lupa ang Babuyan Claro dahil ito ay isang bulkan na matatagpuan sa isang isla. Hindi ito isang anyong tubig.

Explanation:

Ang bulkang Babuyan Claro ay matatagpuan sa pulo ng Babuyan. Ito ay isang aktibong bulkan ayon sa mga tala ng PHIVOLCS. Makikita sa hilagang-kanlurang bahagi ng Babuyan Claro ang bulkang Smith, na huling pumutok noon pang 1924. Ang mga anyong lupa, kagaya ng Babuyan Claro, ay mailalarawan natin dahil sa taglay nitong itsura. Ang mga bulkan ay namuo dahil sa mga tumigas na lava at tephra, at ang mga ito ay nagiging lupa paglipas ng mahabang panahon. Taliwas ito sa mga anyong tubig kagaya ng mga karagatan, ilog, at lawa.
