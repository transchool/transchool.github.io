3. Two vertical poles 20 m and 30 m high, are 60 m apart. A rope is to be attached from the top of one pole to a point on the ground halfway between the poles, and then to the top of the other pole. How long a rope is needed?

┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄

Explanation:

The description forms two right triangle. Half the distance between the two poles represents its bases. The rope from the top of each poles connecting to the ground represents the hypotenuse of the triangles.

1st Triangle:

Base = 30mLeg = 20mHypotenuse = c

2nd Triangle:

Base = 30mLeg = 30mHypotenuse = c

Formula:

[tex]\Large \tt \green{a^2 + b^2 = c^2}[/tex]

┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄

Find the length of the hypotenuse of the 1st Triangle

[tex]\big \begin{aligned}\green{\sf c^2} &\green{=\sf a^2 + b^2} \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 30^2 + 20^2 \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 900+400 \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 1300 \\\sf \sqrt{c^2}&=\sf \sqrt{1300} \\\sf \purple{c}&\purple{=\sf 10 \sqrt{13}} \end{aligned}[/tex]

┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄┄

Find the length of the hypotenuse of the 2nd Triangle

[tex]\big \begin{aligned}\green{\sf c^2} &\green{=\sf a^2 + b^2} \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 30^2 + 30^2 \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 2 \times 30^2 \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 2 \times 900 \\\sf c^2 &=\sf 1800 \\\sf \sqrt{c^2}&=\sf \sqrt{1800} \\\sf \purple{c}&\purple{=\sf 30 \sqrt{2}} \end{aligned}[/tex]
