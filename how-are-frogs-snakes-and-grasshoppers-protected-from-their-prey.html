3. Which food chain is in the correct order? grasshopper → grass → snake → frog → hawk grasshopper → frog → hawk → snake → grass grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → hawk grass → grasshopper → snake → frog → hawk


The food chain that is in the correct order is: grass → grasshopper → frog → snake → hawk

Remember that no food chain can start from a consumer since there will be no source of energy for the primary consumers. In this case, the producer is the grass, it produces its own food via photosynthesis; the grasshopper is the primary consumer, it consumes the producer; the secondary consumer is the frog which in turn eats the primary consumer; and finally the tertiary consumer is the hawk, which eats the secondary consumer.

Do note though that energy received per level in the food chain and energy pyramid is decreasing as you ascend. This is because some of the energy is lost by the organisms to the environment as heat while some is used by the organism.

Animals can only produce a new individual when two parents are present. This is called

sexual reproduction. Frogs, chickens, cows, and many other animals reproduce by sexual

Sexual reproduction takes place when sex cells from two parent animals (one male and

one female) unite to form a new animal.

Animals have sex organs that produce sex cells or gametes. These gametes contain

information about the new organism. There are two types of sex cells or gametes. The egg

cell for females and sperm cell for males.

called a zygote which develops into an embryo. The embryo undergoes continuous

development until it eventually grows into a new born animal. Fertilization in animals may be

internal or external.

Explanation:

Predators are types of animals that catch, hunt, and kill another animal as their source of food.

On the other hand, those animals that are being caught, killed, and eaten are the Prey.

The interactions between Predator and Prey are part of the Ecosystem.

Living organisms or animals need to survive and get a source of their energy in a form of food. They need this energy to continue the reproduction of their species.

Though there is some Prey that are difficult to catch by the Predators, this is another way to continue their reproductions and produce more species of them.

This makes the Ecocsytem balanced.
