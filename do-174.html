12. the sum of three consecutive numbers is 174. find the numbers.​


Answer:

The numbers are 56, 58, 60

Step-by-step explanation:

Using Algebra

x+(x+2)+(x+4)=174

3x+6=174

3x=174-6

3x=168

x=168/3

x=56 (then 58 and 60)

or

(x-2)+x+(x+2)=174

3x=174

x=174/3

x=58

x-2=56 again

Using Simple Math

The middle integer is the mean of the three integers:

174/3=58

58-2=56 and 58+2=60
