16. List down some ways of caring for orchard under each column. Watering Cultivating Fertilizing


Answer:

Fertilizing

During the first three years, fertilizers are used to promote rapid tree growth. Do not apply fertilizer until the trees produce their first leaf flush. A suggested programme from Australia is indicated below.

Irrigating

Not all orchards in Asia have access to irrigation, however, supplementary watering during the first few years will assist tree establishment.

Pruning

Young trees are pruned to provide a strong structure, minimize wind damage and increase fruit bearing area. 

Pests

The most important pest of young trees is erinose mite. The mite causes the leaf surface to blister, while the underside develops a brown felting.

Weeds

Weeds compete with the trees for water and nutrients. If allowed to grow, considerable damage to the tree's roots can occur when they are removed.

Orchard layout

A well-managed orchard should have a long commercial life. Hence, close attention to orchard layout and land preparation will have their rewards for many years. 

Relationship between yield and tree size

Horticulturists studied the relationship between yield and tree size for a group of ten small trees growing in an orchard in southern Queensland.

 

Development of pruning strategies

Left unchecked, lychees grow into large trees that are difficult to spray, harvest and net.

28. Which energy level listed below would be the energy level filled last? 6p 4f 5d 6s


Among the listed energy levels, 6p will be filled with electrons lastly. Orbitals have different energy levels which is represented by small "n". Energy level starts from 1 , n=1 and it should be filled first. In this case, 6p has an energy level n=6. Although 6s has an energy level that is also n=6, p orbital has a higher energy than s orbital. Therefore in the list, 6p is the highest/farthest energy level and will be filled last.
