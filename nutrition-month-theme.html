Ngayong buwan po ay atin pong ipinagdiriwang ang buwan ng Nutrisyon na may temang “ Ugaliing magtanim, wastong nutrisyon ay aanihin.”  Sa atin pong tema ay naglalayong hikayatin ang mga mamamayan na magtanim ng mga prutas at gulay upang maging mas malusog ang mga mamamayan at maiwasan ang karamdaman.Sapagkat kung mayroong tanim sa bawat bakuran ang bawat pamilya siguradong ligtas ang mga mamamayan sa mga pagkaing may halong mga kemikal na nagiging sanhi ng pagkakasakit ng bawat indidwal. Ano- ano ba ng maaring maging epekto sa ating kalusugan kung hindi tayo kumakain ng sariwang prutas at gulay? Una , magkakaroon tayo ng iba’t-ibang karamdaman na dulot ng mga artipisyal at kemikal na ating kinakain, ikalawa ay maari itong maging sanhi ng maagang kamatayan ang pagkain ng hindi masusustansiyang pagkain, Ikatlo, ang pagkain ng mga pagkaing hindi masustansiya ay maaring magdulot ng panghihina ng katawan na magiging dahilan na hindi maabot ng isang tao ang tugatog ng tagumpay. 9. Slogan for theme of 2017 nutrition month


 “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit - For Life!”, is the theme, quote, or nutrition month slogan 2017  of our nation-wide nutrition awareness month.

This theme of 43rd nutrition month of the country aims to promote healthy diet among Filipinos as well as spread health awareness in the whole archipelago. (If you’re looking for slogan for nutrition month English or slogan about nutrition 2017 in English, this answer might help.)

July 1 to July 31, 2017 is the annual campaign for Philippine’s Nutrition Month pursuant to the Presidential Decree No. 491 - Nutrition Act of the Philippines organized and implemented by the National Nutrition Council (NNC).

With the theme “Healthy Diet, Gawing Habit – for Life! aims to promote healthy diets that would contribute to decrease the incidences of overweight, obesity and non-communicable diseases; to teach the Filipinos how to better distinguish between the healthy foods and unhealthy foods for better nourishment choices; and to encourage the  country’s food industries to produce, make, sell, and offer healthier food options to the consumers. The food industry includes farmers, producers, distributors, and food establishments.

The changes in our food system increases the availability of foods that are highly processed such as refined carbohydrates, with high salt, high fat and are calorie-dense.

These changes also include reduced access to whole foods including fruits and vegetables. Thus, there is an increasing number of Filipinos not consuming healthy diets.

Based on the survey conducted during 2013 of National Nutrition Survey of FNRI-DOST, the population of Filipino households with diets that met energy requirement is only 31.70%.


Here are the following reasons why we, Filipinos should focus on healthy diet:

1. A healthy diet, a healthy lifestyle is a foundation of good health.

2. Breastfeeding and other complementary feeding practices which encompasses healthy diet of babies and young children to promote optimal growth and development and impact on long-term health including reducing risks malnutrition. 

3. Filipinos are apparently not consuming healthy diets. The average food consumption among us is below the recommended amount of energy and nutrient levels. There is growing population of prevalence of overweight / obesity among adults. This actually translates to about eighteen (18) million Filipinos.

10. Essay for Nutrition Month Theme 2017 (English and Tagalog)


ENGLISH
The Nutrition Month theme 2017: “Healthy Diet Gawing Habit for Life”


This 2017, our theme aims to let us understand what we need to do. In this theme “Health Diet Gawing Habit for Life”, we have to promote awareness on malnutrition, obesity, and other related health issues. We need to encourage everyone that we have to consume healthy foods instead of unhealthy ones.


As most of us know, obesity affects not just appearance but disease processes as well. With healthier diet, our bodies can better shield disease-causing viruses and bacteria. Healthy diet is a way of protecting and preventing people from diseases, maintaining and providing our body with essential nutrients that it needs.


Also, the theme makes a call to the farmers, producers and manufacturers to produce healthier, organic foods instead of producing products that are filled with harmful chemicals and preservatives.


Most importantly, the theme emphasizes that real change can only be seen through repetitive actions. This means that in order for us to achieve this healthy goal, we make it a habit of eating healthy and producing healthier foods. Habit means that we have to make it an everyday routine and not just a one-time event.

TAGALOG
Ang tema ng Nutrition Month 2017: "Healthy Diet Making Habit for Life"

Ang tema ay naglalayong ipaalam sa mga tao na maunawaan kung ano ang kailangang gawin. Sa temang ito na "Healthy Diet Gawing Habit for Life", kailangan nating itaguyod ang kamalayan sa malnutrisyon, labis na katabaan, at iba pang kaugnay na mga isyu sa kalusugan. Kailangan nating hikayatin ang lahat na kinakailangang kumonsumo tayo ng malusog na pagkain sa halip na mga hindi malusog.


Sa pagkakaalam ng karamihan, ang labis na katabaan ay nakakaapekto hindi lamang sa hitsura kung hindi pati na rin sa mga proseso ng sakit. Sa malusog na diyeta, ang ating mga katawan ay maaaring mas mahusay na kalasag ng mga virus at bakterya na nagdudulot ng sakit. Ang malusog na diyeta ay isang paraan ng pagprotekta at pagpigil sa mga tao mula sa sakit, pagpapanatili at pagbibigay sa ating katawan ng mahahalagang sustansiya na kailangan nito.


Gayundin, ang tema ay tumawag sa mga magsasaka, producer at mga tagagawa upang makagawa ng malusog, organic na pagkain sa halip ng paggawa ng mga produkto na puno ng mga mapanganib na kemikal at preserbatiba.


Pinakamahalaga sa lahat, ang tema ay nagpapahiwatig na ang tunay na pagbabago ay makikita lamang sa pamamagitan ng mga paulit-ulit na pagkilos. Nangangahulugan ito na upang makamit natin ang malusog na layunin na ito, dapat nating gawing araw-araw ang pagkonsumo ng mga makapagpapalusog na pagkain. Kailangan nating gawin itong pang-araw-araw na gawain at hindi lamang isang pangyayari.
