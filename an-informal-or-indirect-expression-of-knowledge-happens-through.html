3. What is an informal or indirect expression of knowledge?​


What is an informal or indirect expression of knowledge?

 

Informal learning is the education that extends beyond the limits of a typical formal learning environment such as a university, school, or college It is described as learning that occurs in our everyday lives or learning projects undertaken by us to educate ourselves. This learning is dependent on the learner's real life interactions, such as peer groups, relatives, media, or some other factor on his or her environment. This learning platform involves a series of events, such as studying the International Gallery collection, learning to cook in a community kitchen, volunteering for a group, and so on.  In other words, informal learning often entails learning without being aware of the learning process. This may involve collecting knowledge from television, the internet, movies, direct contact with people, or some other informal means. Informal Learning is spontaneous and can occur at any time and in any place. The learner is self-motivated and willing to acquire knowledge about a specific topic, or he or she may feel inspired to become acquainted with a new topic. From the viewpoint of an educator, informal learning is mindful of the need to communicate an ability or experience with the learner in a specific context. For example, if a new employee has no idea how to operate a copy machine, another colleague can automatically demonstrate it in action and how to complete the task. This casual method of acquiring skills provides immediate assistance to new employees and is an excellent example of a spontaneous and informal learning background. The learner develops the ability to build greater capacity for self-directed approaches to long-term learning. Anyone who does it and learns these abilities has a significant edge to others who are constantly dependent and submissive to different authoritative people who instruct them what to learn, how to learn, when to learn, and when to stop learning.
