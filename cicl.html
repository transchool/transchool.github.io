11. what is the standard equation of the cicle with the center at its origin, radius √11​


Answer:

x² + y² = 11

Step-by-step explanation:

Standard Equation of circle

(x-h)² + (y-k)² = r²

(h, k) = center, r = radius

With center at origin (0, 0) and radius √11,

⇒ (x-0)² + (y-0)² = (√11)²

⇒ x² + y² = 11
