Summary of Ang Kapatid Ng Tatlong Marya

This is the story of a man who dreamt to have a better life.  Because of greed, the man cut the Kolesmeloko tree to sell its leaves that are selling like hotcakes. After cutting the tree, the snake went out of it. As a punishment, the snake captured his three daughters. Before his death, he requested his son to find his daughters which are married to the beastly animals. In this trip, he is bound to go to three different kingdoms, reigned by three different royalties.
