Who is the Character in the Story?

The protagonist of the story is the unnamed narrator, a young girl from the Caribbean island of Antigua.

Where is the Setting of the Story?

The story takes place in Antigua.

Point of View of the Story?

The story is told in the first person point of view, from the perspective of the young girl.

Theme of the Story?

The primary theme of the story is that of a young girl's coming of age, as she navigates expectations and advice from her mother.

Mood of the Story?

The mood of the story is one of resignation and acceptance, as the narrator struggles to reconcile her mother's expectations with her own desires.

Conflict of the Story?

The central conflict of the story is between the protagonist's desire to break away from her mother's expectations and the mother's expectations for her daughter to conform to societal norms.
