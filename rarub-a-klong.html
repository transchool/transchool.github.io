Answer:

C. Vinta

Explanation:

This boat is traditionally made by the Badjaos who live in the Sulu archipelago. The sail, called Vinta, consists of several colors and geometric shapes, representing the colourful culture and history of Muslim people. These boats are small and not meant to be used for long ocean travel. They are used for livelihood activities such as fishing and diving for pearls and transportation around the archipelago.
