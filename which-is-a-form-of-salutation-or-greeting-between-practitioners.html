B. It is greater in amount

C. Solvent can be of any form

D. Solvent is liquid in form

ANSWER

Amongst the given choices above the false answer is letter D. Solvent is liquid in form.

This is because solvents are not liquid in form all the time, but it is true that usually when we say solvent it is a liquid, specifically water (universal solvent). Solvent are substance that dissolves a solute, and it can also be solid and in gaseous form.
