28. What cookery method is used for a matured poultry?a. boilingb. fryingc. roastingd. stewing​


Answer:

C. Roasting

Explanation:

Roasting is a common cookery method used for a matured poultry. Roasting involves cooking the poultry in an oven at a high temperature, typically around 400-450°F (204-232°C). This method allows for the poultry to be cooked evenly and for the skin to become crispy and golden brown. Roasting typically results in a moist and flavorful finished product. Other common cookery methods for poultry include baking, grilling, and frying. The specific method used will depend on the desired outcome and the type of poultry being cooked.
