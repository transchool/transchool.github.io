SUMMARY

The Last Leaf is about a young artist woman who caught up on a disease called pneumonia. Her name is Johnsy. She was very ill and the doctor said that if Johnsy will not have anything to cling hope to, she will not have much chance of survival.  

Johnsy even became more hopeless when she encountered a vine outside her window and noticed that the leaves were slowly falling from it. She even told Sue that after the last leaf falls, she will also die.  

Here comes Sue, Johnsy's roommate and co-artist who mentioned what Johnsy is going through to Mr. Behrman, their downstairs neighbor, who is an old artist who also happen to be waiting for his time to paint his own masterpiece.  

Upon hearing Johnsy's sentiment, Mr. Behrman have decided what his masterpiece will be - the last leaf of Johnsy. Later then, Mr. Behrman passed away due to pneumonia as well and they - Sue and Johnsy - learned that Mr. Behrman painted the last leaf which gave Johnsy hope.

ABOUT THE SHORT STORY

It was written and published in 1907 in New York City where the story took place.  

ABOUT THE AUTHOR

The Last Leaf written by famous author William Sydney Porter who goes by his name as O.Henry lived a very adventurous life indeed. He was trained to be a pharmacist at a young age but when he moved to different places, he experienced having different kinds of jobs. He was also once arrested and had to live some of his life in prison. During these years was when he wrote different literature and published them under different pseudonyms, having O.Henry as the most popular one.
