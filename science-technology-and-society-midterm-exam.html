30. Does technology shaped us or is it us, our culture and society, that shaped technology? Whoever answers this one, thank you and may you pass all your exams. Godbless!​


I think both of them are correct.  

We and our culture and society shaped technology AND technology also shaped us.

EXPLANATION:

1. Human shaped technology

Technology or techniques invented by humans that can ultimately be used to facilitate human activities themselves. The history of technology begins with the emergence of the human species on earth, traces of equipment and technology possessed by humans since the beginning of their civilization are found, then reconstructed historically and then analyzed in relation to the development of human civilization. The history of human technology starts from simple things like developing language, making stone tools to very complex things like quantum computers.

The development of an applicative technology can be viewed from the human needs for such as overcoming weather and climate change, dealing with wild animal attacks, facilitating economic activities to war. However, technology can also develop from a theoretical aspect that is only based on human curiosity about natural phenomena that occur around it, such as the discovery of the law of gravity and calculus by Isaac Newton and Leibniz.

Because technology is an applicable matter, technological developments greatly shaped changes in the economic, social, political, and cultural activities of human life, so that the history of technology can also be reconstructed based on these changes.

1. Technology shaped us and our socio-cultural life

This increasingly rapid development or technological progress has an influence in various fields of human life, one example is the social and cultural fields. One of the consequences of technological advances that have a positive impact in the social and cultural fields is the increase in the economic sector.

How to communicate

In the past, many Filipinos used to communicate by means of correspondence. However, technological advances have changed the culture in society in terms of establishing communication. Now, people are accustomed to communicating via e-mail or through other social media. This cultural change has a positive impact on society because it can establish communication without boundaries of space and time.

How to dress

The way people dress today has been heavily influenced by other cultures. Nowadays it can be found easily, in a way of dressing that is more modern and can reach various groups, especially young people. The presence of technology makes us exposed to a lot of information both from within and outside the country.

Lifestyle

Advances in technology make it easier for people to find various information on the internet. Without realizing it, the lifestyle of an individual or group begins to change. Some of these changes are positive and some are negative.

Language style

The style of language also changes as technology advances. Regional languages ​​are rarely used and replaced with Tagalog, as the national language. Because not all Tagalog residents understand other regional languages.
