4. example of solicitation letter this christmas on our community​


Answer:


Dear [Name],

I hope this letter finds you well. As the holiday season approaches, we at the [Community Name] would like to extend a warm invitation to join us in spreading cheer and goodwill throughout our community.

This year, we are organizing a Christmas toy drive to benefit children in need. We are asking for donations of new, unwrapped toys or gift cards that we can distribute to families in our community.

Any contribution, big or small, would be greatly appreciated and would bring joy to a child in need this holiday season. If you are interested in donating, please drop off your contribution at [Location] between the hours of [Hours] on [Date].

Thank you in advance for your generosity and for helping to make this holiday season bright for those in need.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Kilala ang mga Pilipino na mahilig maglaro ng Basketball. Hindi ito mawawala bilang isang palaro sa mga barangay, ito ay tinatawag na Liga kung saan maaaring sumali ang mga kalalakihan upang lumaro. Bilang isang manlalaro, kinakailangang may magkakatulad na uniporme ang bawat miyembro ng grupo upang basihan ng pagkakakilanlan nito. 9. Solicitation letter sample english for dance competition


If you need a fundraising for your upcoming event, big or small, some organizations or groups send solicitation letter to potential donors. Below is an example of a solicitation letter for a dance competition:


SAMPLE SOLICITATION LETTER 


Officer-in-Charge

XYZ Company


Dear Sir/Madam:


We are writing you on behalf of the ABC Talent Committee of Brgy. San Antonio, Paranaque. We are a committee formed to handle events in our Barangay, with members from the most creative talents of the homeowners.


This year, we are organizing a dance competition as part of the yearly event for the feast of St. Anthony every June 13th. We would like to seek assistance from your organization for a donation for the prizes. All collected amounts will be allocated to three (3) winners: 3rd placer, 2nd placer and the champion.


Prize donors will be acknowledged in the event program, and will be recognized in our newsletter and website. 


Your support will surely make this event successful. We would like to thank you, in advance, for your generosity. 



Respectfully,


Your Name

12. Affidavit of financial support stating monthly support provided philippines


And affidavit of financial support is a legal document in the Philippines where someone, generally the father of an illegitimate child will need to execute to document how much financial support he would be giving to the child. In the affidavit, the document should state the amount of the monthly support and how it would be provided (ATM, cash, check).

The law on violence against women and their children RA 9262 considers withholding of allowances or support to a child as a form of economic abuse. The Philippines is rather strict on this one. Hence, a father who is not able to provide for their children, when they have the means to do so, could be liable under the law.
