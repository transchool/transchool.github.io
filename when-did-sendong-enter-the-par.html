14. when did sendong enter the par.​


December 15, 2011

Sendong was a tropical cyclone that hit the Philippines and the area around it in 2011. The cyclone started in the Pacific Ocean and moved into the Philippine Area of Responsibility on December 15, 2011. (PAR). The cyclone was the strongest one of the year, and it killed a number of people.

Tropical Storm "Sendong" (also called "Washi" internationally) hit land in the municipality of Hinatuan in Surigao del Sur on the afternoon of December 16, 2011. This was exactly 10 years ago. Then it went west toward Agusan del Sur and Bukidnon. But it did the most damage in the cities of Cagayan de Oro and Iligan because it hit at night and brought with it raging floodwaters that caught people by surprise since many of them were already asleep late at night. Over a thousand people died, and many more are still missing because they went into the water.

In a report, the NDDRMC said that more than 100,000 people in eight cities and 140 villages in 11 provinces were affected by the deadly disaster.
