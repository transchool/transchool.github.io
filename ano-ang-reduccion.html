17. ano ang reduccion brainly.ph


Answer:

Reductions or in Spanish reducciones, also called 'congregaciones', (Portuguese: redução, plural reduções) were settlements created by Spanish rulers in Spanish America and the Spanish East Indies (the Philippines). The Spanish relocated, forcibly in many cases, indigenous inhabitants of their colonies (los indios) into urban settlements modeled on those in Spain. In Portuguese-speaking Latin America, such reductions were also called aldeias.

Pinapaalis ng mga Espanyol ang mga katutubo na lisanin ang kanilang mga orihinal na tahanan upang sila ay  simulang  turuan ng mga  misyonerong Heswita ukol sa Kristiyanismo.
