14. Crafter’s Warehouse sells 6 yards of solid color fleece for $16.49. They sell 16 feet of patterned fleece for $15.40. Which fleece is the better price?


convert yard to feet
1yard=3ft
6yards*3=18ft
$16.49/18=0.916$/ft -solid color
$15.40/16=0.9625$/ft - patterned
therefore the fleece with solid color has the better price
