2. Write on Liberia's code of ethics for nursing


Answer:

Write on Liberia's code of ethics for nursing

1. Adhere to professional standards of conduct and ethical principles that govern the practice of nursing.

2. Respect the rights of patients and their families, including their right to make decisions about their own care.

3. Respect the autonomy of the patient and act in the patient’s best interest.

4. Maintain confidentiality of patient information and records.

5. Provide safe, competent, and compassionate care.

6. Advocate for the health and safety of patients and communities.

7. Maintain professional relationships with colleagues and other health professionals.

8. Participate in continuing professional development and remain current in the practice of nursing.

9. Demonstrate respect for diversity and cultural differences in the provision of nursing care.

10. Refrain from participating in or condoning any form of discrimination.

29. What is the difference of code of ethics and code of conduct?


Answer:

A code of ethics is broad, giving employees or members a general idea of what types of behavior and decisions are acceptable and encouraged at a business or organization. A code of conduct is more focused. It defines how employees or members should act in specific situations.
