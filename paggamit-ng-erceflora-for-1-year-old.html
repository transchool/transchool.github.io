24. 1. The sum of the ages of Rosa and Zeny is 40.Rosa is 4 years older than Zeny.How old is Rosa? 2.In 15 years, Alma will be four times as old as she was nine tears ago.How old is she now?


Answer:

Step-by-step explanation:

1)  

Present ages:

Zeny's age:  xRosa's age:  x + 4  (4 years older than Zeny)Sum of their ages: 40

Equation:

(x) + (x+4) = 40

2x + 4 = 40

2x/2 = (40-4)/2

x = 36/2

x = 18

Zeny's age: x = 18 years oldRosa's age:  x+4 = (18) + 4 = 22 years old

Rosa is 22 years old.

2)

Alma's present age: x

Alma's age in 15 years:  x + 15

Alma's age 9 years ago:  x - 9

Derive the equation:  

In 15 years  ⇒   (x + 15)Alma will be ⇒   =four times as old as she was nine years ago ⇒  4(x-9)

Equation:

(x + 15) = 4(x - 9)

x + 15 = 4x - 36

4x - x = 15 + 36

3x = 51

3x/3 = 51/3

x = 17

Alma is 17 years old (present age).
