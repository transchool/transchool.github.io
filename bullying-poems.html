15. poem about bullying with present real and unreal conditionalpatulong Naman po

If I had the courage, I would say something

With the same rights as me?

Explanation:

The poem above, as requested, is about bullying. The highlighted lines are in the Present Unreal and Present Real Conditional, respectively.

The Present Unreal uses a verb in the past for the condition and then the verb "would" to accompany the main verb of the clause indicating the result or consequence. Example: If I had a car, I would drive to the beach.

The Present Real Conditional uses verbs in the present for both the condition and the consequence. For example: If I feel bored, I watch my favorite series.

For together, we can make a difference,

And create a world that is kinder and more just.

Spreading hate and causing pain

And show them love instead

For in the end, love will conquer

And bullies will be left for dead

Burdening the young and old

And poverty will no longer live
