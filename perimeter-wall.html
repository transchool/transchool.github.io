20. The perimeter of the floor of the house is 300 m and its height is 6 m. What is the cost of the painting of four walls at the rate of 15 per m²? (Answer must be in $)​


COST OF PAINTING

===========================================

[tex]\large\underline{\sf{Problem:}}[/tex]

The perimeter of the floor of the house is 300 m and its height is 6 m. What is the cost of the painting of four walls at the rate of 15 per m²? (Answer must be in $)

===========================================

[tex]\large\underline{\sf{Answer:}}[/tex]

[tex] \qquad \qquad \qquad \large \boxed{ \bold{ \: \: \$27000 \: \:}} \\ [/tex]

===========================================

[tex]\large\underline{\sf{Solution:}}[/tex]

Given :

Perimeter = 300 mHeight = 6 m

According to the question by using the formula we get,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf\boxed{\bold{Perimeter_{(Rectangle)} =\: 2(L + B)}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf 300 =\: 2(L + B)\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf\bold{2(L + B) =\: 300\: ------\: (Equation\: No\: 1)}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

So, the area of four walls will be :

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf\boxed{\bold{Area_{(Four\: Walls)} =\: 2H(L + B)}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

where,

H = HeightL = LengthB = Breadth

So, by putting those values we get,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf Area_{(Four\: Walls)} =\: H \times 2(L + B)\\\end{gathered}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf Area_{(Four\: Walls)} =\: 6 \times 300\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf\bold{Area_{(Four\: Walls)} =\: 1800\: m^2}\\\end{gathered}[/tex]

Now, we have to find the cost of four walls at the rate of 15 per m² :

Given :

Area of Four Walls = 1800 m²Rate per m² = $15 per m²

According to the question by using the formula we get,

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf Cost\: Of\: Painting =\: \$15\: {\cancel{per\: m^2}} \times 1800\: {\cancel{m^2}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\begin{gathered}\implies \sf\bold{\underline{Cost\: Of\: Painting =\: \$27000}}\\\end{gathered} [/tex]

[tex]\therefore[/tex] The cost of the painting of four walls is $27000 .

===========================================
