21. answer the following questions: 1. What is Reality? Give example. 2. What is fantasy? Example 3. What is a bluff? 4. What is a fact? 5.What is real? 6. What is make-believe?


Answer:

1. Reality means anything that exists. An event that has actually happened or a thing that really exists is said to have "reality."

Examples: We drink coffee every morning.

2. Fantasy means the faculty or activity of imagining things, especially things that are impossible or improbable.

Examples: I can be invisible.

3. Bluff means an attempt to deceive someone into believing that one can or will do something.

Examples: When someone playing poker pretends he has a full house even when he doesn't have a good hand.

4. Fact means a thing that is known or proved to be true.

Examples: Friends are always there for you when you need them.

5. Real means actually existing as a thing or occurring in fact; not imagined or supposed.

Examples: I ate lots of strawberries with my friend yesterday.

6. Make-believe means the action of pretending or imagining that things are better than they really are.

Examples: A castle that you pretend you live in but that really doesn't exist at all
