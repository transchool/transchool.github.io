3. 3. Example of Consultative Formal register used in conversation: o Language of conversations with colleagues, peers, etc.


Example of consultative language:

"Hello, Mr. Smith. How are you this morning?"

Consultative language is a variation of language that is commonly used in ordinary conversation in business transactions, conversations between doctors and patients, schools, meetings, or results-oriented or production-oriented conversations.

So, it can be said that this type of business is the most operational variety of language. The form of this language variety is between the formal variety and the informal variety or the casual variety.

The use of the consultative variety requires a minimum of two participants. The use of language in the consultative variety is not too formal, but by choosing the right words. This variety is used to maintain a neutral relationship so that this variety is often used to communicate with strangers.

This consultative language variety in grammatical elements uses a standard pattern that looks similar to the official language variety. The difference lies only in the purpose of the conversation. This variety also emphasizes the context of asking and giving suggestions, opinions and complaints within the scope of work.

Furthermore, the use of code labels or word features in the form of um-hmm, that's right, I see, nod or smile to indicate that participants understand what is being discussed.

The main function of language is as a means of communication. As a group language, language functions as a means of communication and daily interaction within the group.

13. Teacher and student conversation, client and doctor consultation, employer and employee conversation


Teacher and student conversation:

Student: Good morning teacher!

Teacher: Good morning! How are you today?

Student: I am doing fine! Also, may I ask you a question?

Teacher: Yes, you may.

Student: I can't understand what this topic is. Everything written here is just too difficult to understand!

Teacher: Don't worry! I will explain everything about this topic.

Student: Thank you so much!

Teacher: You're welcome.

Client and doctor consultation:

Client: Doctor, I have had a stomach ache since yesterday.

Doctor: What did you eat yesterday?

Client: Some street food.

Doctor: You may have eaten contaminated food.

Client: What should I do?

Doctor: Drink plenty of water every day. I will also prescribe you some medicine to control diarrhea.

Client: Thank you, doctor.

Employer and employee conversation:

Employee: Good day, sir. May I speak with you?

Employer: Yes, you may.

Employee: I want to talk to you about something.

Employer: Okay, please take a seat. Now, what would you like to talk about?

Employee: I have been working here for the past 5 years and have been loyal to this company ever since, so may I get my salary raised?

Employer: If this is the matter, how much?

Employee: Php 30,550 to Php 45,000 is good for me.

Employer: If that's the case then I will accept. I will add a few responsibilities for your salary raise, is that alright?

Employee: Yes, sir! Thank you very much!
