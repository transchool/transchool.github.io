1 Cubic meter (m3) is equal to 1000000 cubic centimeters (cm3). To convert cubic meters to cubic centimeters, multiply the cubic meter value by 1000000. For example, to convert 10 m3 to cm3, multiply 10 by 1000000, that makes 10000000 cm3 is 10 m3.
