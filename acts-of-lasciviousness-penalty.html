Answer:

the act of making a physical contact with the body of another person for the purpose of obtaining sexual gratification other than, or without intention of, sexual intercourse. ... It is distinguished from Attempted Rape in that there is no intent to have sexual intercourse with the victim.

28. what is the penalty for immoral act? please last na i owe you


Penalties vary enormously. Some (but not all) immoral acts are also illegal. Murder, for example, is both immoral and illegal, and punishments for it (handed out by government) are pretty severe.

Other immoral acts are punished by individuals, or by society. If you break an important promise to a friend, your friend may punish you by telling other people, and hence damaging your reputation. If you get known for doing immoral things within a community, that community may shun you, excluding you from group activities. In some (unfortunate) cases, individuals may also take it upon themselves to use violence in response to immoral acts.
