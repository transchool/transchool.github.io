12. Define Rational Soul, Spiritual Soul, Appetitive Soul


Answer:

Definition of Rational Soul, Spiritual Soul, Appetitive Soul

Explanation:

Rational Soul

: the soul that in the scholastic tradition has independent existence apart from the body and that is the characteristic animating principle of human life as distinguished from animal or vegetable life.

Spiritual Soul

: In many religious, philosophical, and mythological traditions, the soul is the incorporeal essence of a living being. Soul or psyche comprises the mental abilities of a living being: reason, character, feeling, consciousness, memory, perception, thinking, etc.

Appetitive Soul

: This is the part that governs desire. It “partakes of reason insofar as it complies with reason and accepts its leadership” (1102b31). It is partly rational (because it can be trained to follow reason) and partly irrational (because it is not itself a faculty of thought).

18. what is hatching what is cross hatching whay is scribble hatching​


Explanation:

Hatching (hachure in French) is an artistic technique used to create tonal or shading effects by drawing (or painting or scribing) closely spaced parallel lines. ... Hatching is especially important in essentially linear media, such as drawing, and many forms of printmaking, such as engraving, etching and woodcut.

Cross Hatching is like taking parallel hatching to the next level. You create a first layer of parallel lines (in any direction)

Scribble Hatching

Make sets if tiny overlapping circles or squiggles. This creates a rough, loose organic texture. To darken, and more squiggles over the squiggles. Blur your eyes to find areas of uneven value and fill these with additional marks.
