6. What is the horizontal distance from the first to the last riser of a stair flight?

It is the horizontal distance from the first to the last riser of a stair flight.

Answer:

220mm

Explanation:

The going of a flight of stairs is the horizontal distance measured between the face of the first and last riser. The going of an individual step is measured from the face of the preceding riser to the face of following riser. For domestic use an individual going should be a minimum of 220mm.

24. Details and specification of riser diagram ?


Answer:

An electrical riser diagram helps you know the system layout, which could come in handy if you are looking at a large rise building in Chicago. You would know about the electrical wiring that has gone up in the building vertically, in a way that's organized, safe, and orderly manner. Electrical risers often share space together with telecom and internet cables.
