6. what is the genre of the literary text ilongga: madamo ang guya by Alicia Tan Gonzalez​


Answer:

Sang 1991, nakabaton ako sang isa ka Professorial Chair halin sa UP Visayas. Gilayon nga nagsugod ako sa pagsulat sang isa ka hinut-ong nga binalaybay nahanungod sa babaye, nga akon gintawag Ilongga: Madamo nga Guya. Ang una nga duha ka pulo ka binalaybay sa hut-ong nga akon natapos sang 1992 akon gindugangan sang lima pa sa wala pa matapos ang 1994, kaupod sang isa ka liwan nga hut-ong sang binalaybay nga akon gintawag Mga Tingug sang Duta, kon sa diin ang “persona” ukon panan-awan (point of view) sa binalaybay iya sang lalaki. Apat sa hut-ong nga ini ang gin-upod ko sa sining libro.

Ang una nga duha ka pulo ka binalaybay sa Ilongga: Madamo nga Guya ginpagwa na sang UP Miag-ao Experimental Theatre bilang poetry in motion ukon poetry performance sa Iloilo, Bacolod, San Jose, Roxas, kag Kalibo sang mga tuig 1996-1997. Nahimo ini sa pagsakdag sang National Commission for Culture and the Arts (NCCA).

8. Make a short reflection about the Winds of April by Nestor Vicente Madali Gonzalez.​


Answer:

she can absorb Ang reflec it

[PLEASE BRAINLIEST ME]

Sometimes the winds are so turbulent that he can't move forward with his dreams. Even though the water around him is choppy, he manages to stay afloat and pursue his dreams, even when things seem difficult. He waits for the wind to calm down and embark on new opportunities, while also holding on to his old dreams. Like Gonzalez's other works, much of the story focuses on the countryside. The rustic landscape and the colorful locals mirror the changing attitudes of the Filipinos during the Commonwealth era, when they were still seeking independence from America. It is no surprise that Gonzalez's works include references to towns and rural people in the provinces of Romblon and Mindoro, since he was born and raised in these areas. This is evident in his novel A Season of Grace, in which the town of Pangasinan appears, and in Look, Stranger on This Island Now, in which the protagonist is from the island of Mindoro. 29. Mrs Gonzalez bought a pine tree that measures 3.50 yards tall. How tall is the pine tree in inches Gonzalez


One yard is three feet. One foot is 12 inches. That means 12*3=36 is how many inches are in a yard. Then you do 3*36= 108. Then, you add one half of 36 for the 0.5 part of 3.5, which is 108+18=126. Therefore, Mrs.Gonzalez's pine tree is 126 inches.
