Problem 2:In rhombus SAMT, what is the measure of m∠ASM, if m∠SAT = 35°?

A. 35°

B. 55°

C. 70°

D. 110°

Solution:

m∠SAT = 35°

m∠TAM = 35°

35 + 35 + m∠AMT = 180

m∠AMT = 180 - 70

m∠AST = 110°

m∠ASM = m∠AST /2

m∠ASM = 55°
