13. A bus left a bus station at 5:00pm and travels at the rate of 50kph. Another bus on the same route left the bus station 1 hour later and overtakes the first bus at 7:00pm. What is the rate of the second bus? with solution po please :)


Answer:

The 1st bus left the station and traveled for two hours, and the speed rate of the bus is 50kph.

Let S1 be the speed of the first bus, and S2 be the speed of 2nd bus.

Let's solve the distance traveled by the 1st bus:

Distance = Speed * time

Distance = S1 * Time = 50kph * 2h = 100kph

The second bus overtook the first bus at the time that the first bus traveled 100 km.

Let's solve the speed of the second bus:

2nd bus = distance/travel of time

              = 100 km/1 hour

Thus, the rate of the second bus is 100km per hour (100km/h)

28. Two hours after a bus left a station for Baguio at a speed of 40 kph, another bus left the same station traveling at a speed of 60 kph. How many hours will it take the second bus to overtake the first bus? Full solution


Answer:

4 hours

Step-by-step explanation:

What is being asked?  

How many hours will it take the second bus to overtake the first bus?

Given:

1st Bus :  

2 hours  

40 Kph

2nd Bus :  

60 Kph

Solutions:  

To get the total hours needed for the 2nd bus to overtake the 1st bus:

Know first the starting kilometer of each bus

For the 1st bus = 40kph x2hrs=80km for the 2hrs earlier.

2nd bus starting kilometer = 0 km

after 1 hour: 1st bus = 80km(2hrs earlier)+ 40km for the 1st hour= 120km

While, the 2nd bus = 0 + 60km = 60km

after 2 hours: 1st bus = 120km+40km = 160km

While, the 2nd bus = 60km+60km = 120km  

after 3 hours: 1st bus = 160km+ 40km = 200km

While, the 2nd bus = 120km+ 60km = 180km

after 4 hours: 1st bus = 200km+ 40km = 240km

While, the 2nd bus = 180km+ 60km = 240km

Therefore, after 4 hours the 2nd bus can now overtake the 1st bus.

Let:
x - traveling hours of bus

Note:
1st bus is traveling at a speed of 40kph and 2hours earlier than 2nd bus (40(x + 2))
2nd bus is traveling at a speed of 60kph (60x)


60x > 40(x + 2)
60x > 40x + 80
60x - 40x > 80
20x > 80
x > 4

After 4hours, the 2nd bus can overtake to the first bus.
