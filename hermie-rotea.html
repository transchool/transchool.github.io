How much is the discount? 3) Tina bought a blouse for P469.75 and a skirt for P524.50. How much change did she get from P1000 bill? 4) Hermie receives his monthly salary worth P20 000. He spends 30 percent of it for bills, 1/8 of it for food and 25% of it for gadgets. How much money does he have left? 5) Allen works as a student assistant for 3 hours a day for P45.50 and hour. How much does she earn from Monday to Saturday? Please help meee :"(


1. Let x= liters of gasoline the tank can hold
Equation: 0.40x + 21.6 = x
x - 0.40x = 21.6
0.60x = 21.6
x = 21.6/0.6
x = 36 liters

2. Sale price = 850 - (850 x 0.15)
= 850 -127.5
=722.50
The discount is P127.50.

3. Let x= change
Equation: 1000 - (469.75 + 524.50) = x
x = 1000 - 994.25
x = 5.75

4. Let x = money left
Equation:
x = 20000 - [ (20000 x 0.3) + (20000 x 1/8) + (20000 x 0.25)]
x = 20000 - [ 6000 + 2500 + 5000]
x = 20000 - 13500
x = 6500

5. Let x = Allen’s earnings from Mon-Sat
Equation: x = (45.50 x 3) x 6
x = 136.50 x 6
x = 819
