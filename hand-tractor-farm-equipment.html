Bolo - a large single-edged knifeHand tractor - is a small two-wheel tractor powered by a small gas engine, usually 6-10 horsepower.Rake - is a broom for outside use; a horticultural implement consisting of a toothed bar fixed transversely to a handle, or tines fixed to a handle, and used to collect leaves, hay, grass, etc.Rotavator - is an agricultural equipment used for secondary tillage.
