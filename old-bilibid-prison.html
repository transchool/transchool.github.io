30. Old Bilibid served as penitentiary or prison during the Spanish regime.a. apartmentb. housec. prisond. restaurant​


Answer:

C. Prison

Explanation:

The Old Bilibid Prison, then known as Carcel y Presidio Correccional (Spanish, "Correctional Jail and Military Prison") occupied a rectangular piece of land that was part of the Mayhalique Estate in the heart of Manila. The old prison was established by the Spanish colonial government on June 25, 1865 via royal decree.
