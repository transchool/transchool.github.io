10. Accessories of mimaropa


Answer:

What are the products in MIMAROPA?

What are the products in MIMAROPA?MIMAROPA is one of the Philippine's top producers of rice, banana, coconut, mango, cashew, papaya and cassava, which are included among the country's top export products.

What are the natural resources in MIMAROPA?

What are the natural resources in MIMAROPA?Major agricultural products include rice, corn, coconut, tobacco, garlic, onions, mango, banana, melons, and peanuts. Fisheries and aqua-marine products include salt, milkfish, prawn, tuna, octopus, and seaweeds. The island of Mindoro is internationally recognized as an area for bio-diversity conservation.
