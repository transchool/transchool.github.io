3. ano Ang writ of Amparo ​


Answer:

The petition for a Writ of Amparo is a remedy available to any person whose right to life, liberty and security is violated or threatened with violation by an unlawful act or omission of a public official or employee, or of a private individual or entity, and covers extralegal killings and enforced disappearances

Ang petisyon para sa isang Writ of Amparo ay isang remedyo na magagamit ng sinumang tao na ang karapatan sa buhay, kalayaan at seguridad ay nilabag o binantaan ng paglabag ng isang labag sa batas na gawa o pagtanggal ng isang pampublikong opisyal o empleyado, o ng isang pribadong indibidwal o entity, at sumasaklaw sa mga extralegal na pagpatay at sapilitang pagkawala o pagbabanta nito

26. amparo invested a certain amount at 10% simple interest per year . after 2 years, the interest she received amounted to P3,000. how much she invest?​


Answer:

[tex]15000[/tex]

Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

Interest = P3,000Rate = 10% or 0.1Time = 2 yearsPrincipal = ?

Formula: I = PRt

I = interestP = principal R = ratet = time

Solution:

I = 3000 = P(10%) (2)

[tex]\begin{gathered} \sf{} I=3000=P(0.1)(2) \\ \\ \sf{} I=3000=0.2P \\ \\ \sf{} I=3000=0.2P/0.2 \\ \\ \sf{} 15000=P\end{gathered}[/tex]

Therefore, she invest 15000.
