21. The average weight of the top 5 fish caught at a fishing tournament was 12.3 pounds. Some of the weight of the fish are shown in the tabl wayne s. x= calra p. 12.8 Deb N. 12.6 Vinctente R. 11.8 Armin


It was given that average weight of the top 5 caught at a fishing tournament was 12.3 pounds and their respective weights were: 12.8, 12.6, 11.8 and 9.7.  

The entry lacks one fish since it is mentioned that there were 5 fishes in the tournament.  

Therefore we will let the unknown as "x"                                                                    

12.3 lbs= (12.8 + 12.6 +11.8+9.7+x)/5                                                              

x= 14.6 lbs, we then recall the given weights.  

From all five, 14.6 lbs weighs heaviest.  

ANSWER: 14.6 lbs is the heaviest.
