4. Similarities between arrest with warrantand warranthers arrest​


Answer:

A warrant is a written order that is signed by a judge. The warrant authorizes the police to conduct searches, seizures, and arrests. A judge may only issue a search warrant when there is probable cause that the police will find evidence of a crime during a search. A judge may only issue an arrest warrant when there is reason to believe the person listed in the warrant is engaging in, or has engaged in, criminal activity.

15. The difference of arrestwith warrantand arrest withoutWarrant​


Answer:

In the Philippines, as provided in Rule 113, Section 5 of the 2000 Revised Rules of Criminal Procedure,[3] a peace officer or a private person may, without a warrant, arrest a person:

When, in his presence, the person to be arrested has committed, is actually committing, or is attempting to commit an offense (in flagrante delicto arrest);

When an offense has just been committed and he has probable cause to believe based on personal knowledge of facts or circumstances that the person to be arrested has committed it (hot pursuit arrest); and

When the person to be arrested is a prisoner who has escaped from a penal establishment or place where he is serving final judgment or is temporarily confined while his case is pending, or has escaped while being transferred from one confinement to another.

Also as provided within the Revised Rules are other instances of lawful arrests without warrant:

If a person lawfully arrested escapes or is rescued, any person may immediately pursue or retake him without a warrant at any time and in any place within the Philippines

For the purpose of surrendering the accused, the bondsmen may arrest him or, upon written authority endorsed on a certified copy of the undertaking, cause him to be arrested by a police officer or any other person of suitable age and discretion

An accused released on bail may be rearrested without the necessity of a warrant if he attempts to depart from the Philippines without permission of the court where the case is pending

21. reaksyon tungkol kay buknoy at tikboy


Answer:

Reaksyon tungkol kay Buknoy at Tikboy

Ang sinasabi ng unang argumento at salungat ay na walang kasiyahan sa bagsak na grado, pero wala ring kasiyahan sa tagumpay na hindi pinagpaguran. Sang-ayon ako sa pareho, kaya walang kahihinatnan ang pandaraya. Kung kailangang mamili ng isang bagay na walang kasiyahan, yung bagsak na grado na lamang ang pipiliin ko, para kahit papaano, malinis ang konsensiya ko.

Sa ikalawang argumento naman at salungat naman, sinasabi na karamihan naman ay nandaraya, at tinatanong kung katuwiran ba iyon. Tama ang salungat, hindi ibig sabihin na dahil ginagawa ng karamihan ay tama na ang isang bagay. Dapat ay matuto tayong mag-isip para sa sarili natin kung tama o mali ba ang isang bagay.

Sa ikatlo naman, sinasabi ng argumento na maliit ng bagay ang pangongopya at walang masasaktan. Sabi naman ng salungat, marahil binibigyang katwiran niya lamang ang takot niya. Ang reaksyon ko rito ay na ikinatutuwa ko na alam niya sa sarili niya na nangangatwiran lamang siya. Ibig sabihin, alam niya namang mali ang pangongopya, nagdadahilan lang siya para magmukhang tama na gawin iyon. Ako, madalas ko ring gawin iyon.

Sa ikaapat at huling argumento, sinasabi nila na para sa ibang tao, mas mahalaga ang tagumpay kaysa sa prinsipyo kaya magulo ang buhay. Sang-ayon rin ako rito. Halimbawa na ang korupsyon at krimen sa ating bansa. Ibig rin sabihin, tayo mismo sa sarili natin, dapat lagi nating inuuna ang prinsipyo kaysa sa tagumpay.
