1.If you want to actually achieve your goals this year, then consider the following:

Be specific.

When setting goals, be specific in what you want to achieve. Vagueness will only cause you to feel overwhelmed, and you will just give up.

Make your goals measureable.

In order to know if you achieved the goal, it must be measurable. For example, if you want to lose weight, don't simply write down "lose weight" as a goal. How much weight do you want to lose? Or don't just write "spend more time with family." How much time do you want to spend with your family every night?

Are they your goals?

Only you can set your own goals. If your spouse, co-worker or friend sets a goal for you, you're not going to achieve it. Taking ownership will give you more incentive to meet your goal.

Set a time limit.

Setting a time frame will help you set realistic goals. For example, if you want to save more money, list how much money a month you want to put into your savings account.

Put them in writing.

Putting your goals in writing will make you much more likely to achieve them. Write down your goals and review them often. This will give you motivation to make them a reality.

When we blame others, we lose control and become victims of the event or those involved in it.Gestalt Therapy emphasizes the importance of the patient taking responsibility. Indeed, it is one of the fundamental principles of this therapy, which also emphasizes acknowledgment and the immediate present.2. DEALING WITH IMPASSEGestalt therapy will create interventions that expose how the person is emotionally stuck as well as provide experiences for emotional experience. When the impasse is broken, the client can explore new avenues for self-sufficiency.3. AWARENESS OF THE PRESENT MOMENTGestalt therapy helps people focus on the present moment. This enables people to discover what immediate thoughts, feelings, or behaviors make them anxious. Problems with mental health. Gestalt therapy can help people suffering from behavioral disorders such as bipolar disorder.Gestalt therapists and their clients use creative and experiential methods to increase self-awareness and solve a variety of problems. Rather than discussing past experiences, clients are encouraged to live them in the present moment and accept responsibility for how they feel.4. UNFINISHED BUSINESSOne of the primary goals of Gestalt Therapy is to assist people in completing unfinished business. According to Gestalt Therapy, people cannot be considered separate from their environment or from interpersonal relationships.
