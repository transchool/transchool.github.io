2. under what ribbon group does insert smart art fall


The ribbon that SmartArt belongs to is called Illustrations. Your knowledge and thoughts are represented visually through a SmartArt image. Select SmartArt from the Illustrations group of Insert's Illustrations instructions. A dialogue box will display.

Select the desired SmartArt graphic by clicking on a category on the left. Easy integration of the SmartArt graphic into the current slide. You build one by selecting a layout that matches your message. Some patterns such as organization charts and Venn diagrams depict specific data types, while others enhance the aesthetic of a bulleted list.

Combined with other capabilities such as themes, SmartArt graphics enable you to generate professional-quality illustrations with a few mouse clicks. When selecting a layout for your SmartArt visual, consider the message you wish to express and whether you want the information to appear in a specific manner. Whenever you create a SmartArt graphic, you are requested to select a kind, such as a Process, Hierarchy, or Relationship. A type is akin to a category of SmartArt graphics, and each type has numerous layouts.

Because you can quickly and easily alter layouts, experiment with many designs until you discover the one that effectively shows your message.

How should you convert a picture to a SmartArt?

Select the desired text for use.By selecting Home > Convert, you can convert to SmartArt.Then, choose Picture from Additional SmartArt Graphics.By selecting OK, the desired SmartArt will have been selected.
