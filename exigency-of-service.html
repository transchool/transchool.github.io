Answer:

Section 6 of Article II of the R.A. "Where the exigencies of the service require the transfer of a teacher from one station to another, such transfer may be effected by the schools division superintendent who shall previously notify the teacher concerned of the transfer and the reason(s) therefor," according to 4670, also known as "The Magna Carta for Public School Teachers."
