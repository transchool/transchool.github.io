Transition Signals

Transition signals are words or phrases that connect ideas and add cohesion to writing. They indicate the relationships between sentences and between paragraphs. Thru these, the readers can easily understand the text. The message is easily grasp by the reader or the listener of the text. Examples of transition signals are those words used in comparison and contrast.
