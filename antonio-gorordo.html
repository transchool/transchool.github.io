23. The total mass of Eric and Antonio is 79.5 kg. If Eric loses 3.5 kg and Antonio's mass remains unchanged, they will have the same mass. What is Antonio's mass?


Eric's mass: x
Antonio's mass: y

x + y = 79.5 
x = 79.5 - y

If Eric loses 3.5 kg, and Antonio's unchanged, they will have the same mass.

x - 3.5 = y

x = 79.5 - (x - 3.5)
x = 79.5 - x + 3.5
2x/2 = 83/2
x = 41.5 kg   ⇒  Eric's mass

Find Antonio's mass:
x - 3.5 = y
41.5 - 3.5 = y
y = 38 kg.   ⇒  Antonio's mass

Answer:  Antonio's mass is 38 kg.

Checking:

41.5 + 38 = 79.5
79.5 = 79.5   (True)

41.5 - 3.5 = 38
38 = 38  (True)
