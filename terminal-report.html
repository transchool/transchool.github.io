1. What is Terminating and Non-terminating decimals? (Non-sense/Copied= Report)


Answer:

Decimal quotients in which the decimal parts is non-repetitive and definite are called terminating decimals. Quotients in which the digits in a decimal repeat and are never ending are called non-terminating decimals, Non-terminating decimals are further divided into two categories; repeating and non-repeating decimals.

Examples: 1.5 ÷ 0.46= 3.26069... (non-terminating/repeating)

                 8.134 ÷ 3.5= 2.324 (terminal)
