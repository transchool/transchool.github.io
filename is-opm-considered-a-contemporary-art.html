Contemporary art is the art of today, produced in the second half of the 20th century or in the 21st century. 21. What made OPM become a strong force in Philippine Contemporary Arts?​


Answer:

OPM (Original Pilipino Music) has become a strong force in Philippine contemporary arts due to its ability to combine traditional Filipino music with modern influences. OPM has become extremely popular in the Philippines, and its influence can be seen in many forms, including pop, rock, jazz, hip-hop, and more. OPM has also become an important platform for Filipino artists to express themselves and share their stories, which has led to the growth of a vibrant music and arts scene in the country. The success of OPM has also been attributed to its ability to bring together different generations and cultures, as it is a genre which speaks to everyone.


22. what is philippine contemporary music and opm​


Answer:

Contemporary music in the Philippines usually refers to compositions that have adopted ideas and elements from twentieth century art music in the West, as well as the latest trends and musical styles in the entertainment industry. This brief introduction covers only the works written by the art music composers.

Original Filipino music, now more commonly termed original Pinoy music or OPM, originally referred only to Philippine pop songs, particularly ballads, such as those popular after the collapse of its predecessor, the Manila sound of the late 1970s. Currently, OPM is used as a catch-all term for popular music composed and performed by Filipino musicians and singers.
