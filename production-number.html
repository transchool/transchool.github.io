29. The product of two numbers is 8933. If each of the two numbers is doubled the product of these larger numbers is_.


Answer:

35,732

Step-by-step explanation:

8,933 is a prime number.  Its factors are 1 and 8,933.

Find the product if each factor is doubled:

2(1) = 2

2(8,933) = 17,866

The product of the doubled factors:

2 × 17,866 = 35,732

Here, we can deduce that given any two factors of a number and when the same factors are doubled and multiplied, the product is four times the number.

Proof:

2(1) × 2(8,933) = 4 (8,933)

2 × 17,866 = 35,732

35,732 = 35,732  (True)
