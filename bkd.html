5. Diameters AB and CD of circle K intersect such that m∠BKD = 100°. The measure of arc AC is


Answer:

100

Step-by-step explanation:

Since they are diameters, then we can safely assume that arc AB measures 180 degrees, and same goes for arc CD. and since they meet at K, with m∠BKD being equal to 100 degrees, then the m∠AKC is also 100 degrees because they are vertical angles, and we know that these kinds of angles are congruent. and so, since AKC is a central angle, the measure of the arc is also 100 degrees.
