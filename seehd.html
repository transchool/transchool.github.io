4. Round seeds and yellow seed color are dominant to wrinkled seeds and green seed color. What is the probability of having offspring that have wrinkled seeds and yellow seed color for the cross RRYY ´ RrYy?


Answer: 4/16 RRYY; 4/16 RRYy; 4/16 RrYY; 4/16 RrYy

Explanation: Since this problem involves 2 genes, this is a dihybrid test cross. In solving a dihybrid test cross, you may use 2 ways. Its either, (1) 16x16 Punnet Square and (2) Forkline Method. But I used forkline method to this question:

1. Get the test cross

RR x Rr = 2 RR, 2 Rr

YY x Yy = 2 YY, 2 Yy

2. Proceed with the forkline method

2 RR - 2 YY - 4/16 RRYY

- 2 Yy - 4/16 RRYy

2 Rr - 2 YY - 4/16 RrYY

- 2 Yy - 4/16 RrYy

0% is the probability of having an offspring which have wrinkled seed and yellow seed color.
