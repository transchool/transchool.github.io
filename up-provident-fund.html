23. a php 1000000-trust fund was set up and to be used by an 8 years old nephew when he goes to college. ibn 8 years how much will the fund be if the investment rate is 7.5% compound quarterly?


Answer:

The total amount accrued, principal plus interest, with compound interest on a principal of $1,000,000.00 at a rate of 7.5% per year compounded 1 times per year over 8 years is PHP1,783,477.83.

Step-by-step explanation:

First, convert R as a percent to r as a decimal

r = R/100

r = 7.5/100

r = 0.075 rate per year,

Then solve the equation for A

A = P(1 + r/n)nt

A = 1,000,000.00(1 + 0.075/1)(1)(8)

A = 1,000,000.00(1 + 0.075)(8)

A = $1,783,477.83
