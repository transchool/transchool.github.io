12. ballet presentation of lisa macuja elizalde


Answer:

Lisa Teresita Pacheco Macuja-Elizalde (born October 3, 1964) is a Filipino prima ballerina.[1][2] In 1984, she became the first foreign soloist to ever join the Kirov Ballet. In the Philippines, she is the Artistic Director and CEO of Ballet Manila and was the Vice-Chairman of the Philippine UNESCO National Commission. She was also the Commissioner of the National Commission on the Role of Filipino Women. Macuja-Elizalde is also Directress and faculty member of the Lisa Macuja School of Ballet – a training center for ballet professionals who are steeped in the Russian Vaganova method.

24. 10 years ago, Jane was 4 times as old as Bianca. Now she is twice as old as Bianca. Find their present ages. Show your solution.


Age Problem:

Bianca is 15 years old while Jane is 30 years old.

Solutions:

Given that Jane is 4 times as old as Bianca, represent their ages.

x - Bianca's age

4x - Jane's age

Since this is a representation of their ages 10 years ago then,

4x + 10 = 2(x + 10)

Solve for x.

4x + 10 = 2(x + 10)

4x + 10 = 2x + 20

4x - 2x = 20 - 10

2x = 10

2x/2 = 10/2

x = 5

Therefore, Bianca is only 5 years old 10 years ago.

Find Jane's age using 4x.

4(5) = 20

Thus, Jane's age 10 years ago is 20.

Find their current ages.

x + 10 - Bianca's age

5 + 10 = 15

Therefore, Bianca is now 15 years old.

Find Jane's age.

4x + 10 = 4(5) + 10 = 20 + 10 = 30

Thus, Jane is now 30 years old.
