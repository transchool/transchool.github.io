30. conceptual definition of chi-square?


Answer:

The chi-square (x2) is a test of significance for categorical variables. ... The logic behind the chi-square is to calculate the distance between the observed frequencies within the contingency table and the condition of statistical independence (i.e. the hypothesis of no association or "null hypothesis").
