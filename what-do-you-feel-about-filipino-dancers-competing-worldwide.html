22. What do you think about combining our traditional crafts and indigenous materials with worldwide trends in color,texture,design,and even function?


Answer

It could be a good idea for a Philippine Ethnic and Indigenous Art Movement in the future. Fortunately, this idea has already been done years ago by Filipino Interior Designers like Ito Kish, Liliana Manahan, Dem Bitantes, Vito Selma, and Kenneth Cobonpue. Their designs are well known in the European and Middle-eastern markets for its Ethnicity in texture and color. Most of their famous furniture designs are made out of indigenous materials like Rattan, Narra and Santol Wood, Abaka, Coconut, Bamboo, and so on.

However, the real problem is that not all Filipinos appreciate the Ethnicity and Indigenous traits of Philippine art designs. This is because most Filipinos are influenced by foreign cultures. Thus, the awareness and acceptance of society to Ethnicity and Indigenousness still have a long way to go. Our Ethnic/Indigenous craftsmen also needs help not only from the local government but also from the national government.
