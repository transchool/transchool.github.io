21. difference between medley and individual relay​


Answer:

In the relay medley, the order goes backstroke, breaststroke, butterfly then freestyle. In the individual medley, the order goes butterfly, backstroke, breaststroke then freestyle. ... From there, the relay goes in order of the relative speed of each stroke
