What is the moral lesson of the good prince Bantugan?

The moral lesson of the story are: he who takes advantage of other man’s suffering is aweakling; and we should not envy other people. When one is already in agony and is suffering, we must reach out to them and do our very bestto help them; not take advantage of their situation and use it as a weapon.

Who is Bantugan the prince?
