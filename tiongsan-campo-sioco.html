Paulo C. Campos (July 27, 1921 – June 2, 2007) was a Filipino physician and educator noted for his promotion of wider community health care and his achievements in the field of nuclear medicine for which he was dubbed as "The Father of Nuclear Medicine in the Philippines".

28. Mr. Sioco paid $249,750 corresponding to the principal and interest of his one year loan. What was the interest rate applied to this if the originsl amount he borrowed was $225,000?


Just follow this formula:
 I=PRT   (I-interest, P-principal, R-rate, T=time)
I=Total amount-principal

Answer:
I=$249,750 - $225,000
I=$24,750
I=PRT

$24, 750= $225, 000 x r x 1
$24, 750= $225, 000 x r
$24, 750 / $225, 000 =r
0.11 = r
(Change to percent)
11% = r
Check:
$24, 750= $225, 000 x 11% x 1
$24, 750= $225, 000 x.11
$24, 750=$24, 750, CORRECT!!!
Therefore, the interest rate is 11 % per annum. (or 11% per 1 year)

29. siya ay isang maestro de campo ng Binalatongan.


Answer:

Andres Malong

Explanation:

Si Andres Malong ay ang maestro de campo ng Binalatongan, ngayon ay San Carlos City, Pangasinan noong 1660s. Tinulungan niya ang maraming Kastila sa pamamahala sa iba't ibang bayan sa Pangasinan, at dahil dito, natuto at sinanay na gumamit ng dahas at kalupitan. Inaasahan niyang maging Hari ng lalawigan, gayunpaman, isinantabi ang planong ito nang sumiklab ang digmaan, sa pamumuno ni Francisco Maniago, sa Pampanga. Sinimulan ni Malong ang kanyang kampanya sa isang maliit na barangay na tinatawag na Malunguey, ngunit nabigo. Sa pagkakaroon ng parehong kondisyon tulad ng sa Pampanga, pinangunahan niya ang mga tao sa Pangasinan na humawak ng armas laban sa mga Kastila. Kumalat ito na parang apoy sa Pangasinan. Dahil sa kanyang tagumpay, ipinroklama niya ang kanyang sarili na Hari ng Pangasinan.
