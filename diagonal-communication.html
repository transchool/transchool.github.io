3. a community billboard has sides 5 meters and 12 meters. what is the length of its diagonal​


Answer:

13 meters or 13m

Step-by-step explanation:

You can answer this by using the pythagorean theorem which is [a² + b² = c²]

let c be the diagonal:

12² + 5² = c²

144 + 25 = c²

169 = c²

Now, in order to get the final answer, since we need the c and not the c², we need to get its square root(√). So the next step would be to square root(√) both sides of the equation:

√169 = √c²

[13 = c] or [c = 13]

Answer:

Organism

-In biology, an organism is any organic, living system that functions as an individual entity. All organisms are composed of cells.

Community

-A community is a group of people who share something in common

Ecosystem

-An ecosystem is a geographic area where plants, animals, and other organisms, as well as weather and landscape, work together to form a bubble of life.

Biotic

-A biotic factor is a living organism that shapes its environment. In a freshwater ecosystem, examples might include aquatic plants, fish, amphibians, and algae.

Abiotic

-An abiotic factor is a non-living part of an ecosystem that shapes its environment.

Habitat biome

-A habitat describes the physical environment a species lives in whereas a biome is compromised of multiple habitats with similar features.

Biosphere

-The biosphere is made up of the parts of Earth where life exists—all ecosystems.

Population

-A population is a distinct group of individuals, whether that group comprises a nation or a group of people with a common characteristic.
