30. whisper, mumble, grumble, mutter


Answer: They are alike because they’re a way of communication

You can talk by whispering, mumbling, grumbling, and muttering.

Grumble is slightly different

Whisper, mumble, grumble, mutter

Of all the choices above, the word grumble has a slightly different meaning.

The words whisper, mumble and mutter, all have very similar meaning, which are "to speak very quietly so that it is hard for other people to hear what you talking about."

The word grumble on the other hand refers to the act of complaining quietly about a particular subject or to speak in a sad and unhappy way.
