12. all incoming freshmen a major university are given a mathematics diagnosis exam. The scores normally distributed with a mean of 120 and a standard deviation of 25. If a student scores less than a certain score, he or she will have undergo an intervention program. Find the cutoff score at which 64% of the students would have to undergo the intervention program


Answer:

The sample mean is 18.6 and the sample standard deviation is 0.68

Step-by-step explanation:

1. In any math problem, it is very important to identify what the given information are.

Based on what we have:

The population mean (represented by the Greek letter μ) is 18.6.

The  population standard deviation (represented by the Greek letter σ) is 5.9.

The size of the sample/sample size of test taker from Federico Rural High School is 76.

2.Let's answer the question.

a. Sample mean

For the mean of the sample mean of the 76 students, the mean is equal to the population mean.

That is, μ (population mean) = μ (sample mean)

Since μ = 18.6, then μ = 18.6

b. Sample Standard deviation

To solve for the standard deviation (σ), use the formula σ = σ/

σ = 5.9/√76 = 0.68

The sample mean and sample standard deviation of the 76 students who took the National Test from Federico Rural High School are 18.6 and 0.68, respectively.

25. Identical squares are cut from each corner of an 8 inch by 11.5 inch rectangular piece of carsboard.the sides are folded up to make a box with no top if the volume of the resulting box is 63.75 cubic inches how long is the edge of each square that is cutoff


Let:

c = the corner of the piece to be cut out (edge)

new length = 11.5 - c

new width = 8 - c

height = c

Volume = l * w * h

(8-c)(11.5-c)(c) = 63.75

(92 - 19.5c + c^2)(c) = 63.75

(92c - 19.5c^2 + c^3) = 63.75=>

92c - 19.5c^2 + c^3 - 63.75 = 0

c1 = 12.59992

c2 = 0.83409

c3 = 6.06599

nearest ANSWER: 0.83409 inch

Checking: (8 - 0.83409)(11.5 - 0.83409)(0.83409) = 63.75 cubic inches
