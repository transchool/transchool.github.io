25. There are two ships that are sailing form General Santos City. Ship A is sailing east,and ship B is sailing 43° south of east. after an hour, ship A has travelled 115 km and ship B has travelled 98 km how far are the two ships? ​


Given:

θ = 43° south of east

OA = 115 km

OB = 98 km

time = 1 hour

Formula:

Use cosine law

[tex] {AB}^{2} = {OA}^{2} + {OB}^{2} - 2(OA)(OB) \cos(θ) [/tex]

Solution:

[tex] {AB}^{2} = {(115)}^{2} + {(98)}^{2} - 2(115)(98) \cos(43) [/tex]

[tex] {AB}^{2} = 6344.29[/tex]

[tex]AB = \sqrt{6344.29} [/tex]

Final answer:

[tex]AB = 79.65 \: km[/tex]
