It formed in the Pacific Ocean, around 945 kilometers southeast of Guam on December 13, 2011, and entered the Philippine Area of Responsibility (PAR) on December 15

20 December – Tropical Storm Sendong may have left the Philippine area of responsibility but the massive flooding reported throughout Region X, especially in cities of Iligan and Cagayan de Oro, left 957 dead, 1582 injured, 49 missing and affected 63,079 families.
