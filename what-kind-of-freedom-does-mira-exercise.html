16. What enables the person to exercise limits in his/her freedom


The thing that enables a person to exercise limits on his /her freedom is the desire to avoid the negative effects that might be obtained from his/her freedom.

Freedom is certainly something that everyone wants. With freedom, humans have the full right to do something or make decisions according to their wishes. However, apart from providing positive things, freedom can also have a negative impact. For example, when a student is too free, he may have too much fun and forget to study and his grades drop.

Therefore, the awareness that freedom can have negative effects and the desire to avoid these negative things enables a person to exercise limits on his freedom. For example by applying discipline or making limits on how far he can do something with his freedom.

30. what are the three kinds of freedom ?what is the most essential ?​


Answer:

Freedom can be of three types, and those three types have to be understood well. The first is freedom from, the second is freedom for, and the third is just freedom—neither from nor for. The first, freedom from, is a reaction. The second, freedom for, is revolution. The third, just freedom, is rebellion. It is present-oriented. The first is political, the second is poetic, the third is mystic, religious.
