28. what test will you undergo if you want to be informed about your general health status?


Answer:

The test that you will undergo if you want to be informed about your general health status is a known as Comprehensive Executive Check up. Depending on the hospital that will perform the tests and demographics, Comprehensive Executive Check up usually includes but not limited to:

• A physical examination  

• Urine test

• Biometrics (height, weight, blood pressure and pulse rate)

• Eyesight test

• Extensive blood test

• Lung-function test

• Audiogram (hearing test)

• Exertion ECG

• ECG at rest

• Ultrasonic heart exam

• Ultrasonic exam of the entire abdominal organs

• Ear area pressure test

• Concluding consultation

Explanation:

These tests can further be expanded to suit each patient's needs for specialists such as gynecologists, internists, radiologists, neurologists, cardiologists,  and psychologists.

Regular Health check-up plays a big role in the early detection of health issues. Identifying problems early increases your chances of receiving effective treatment. Different factors, such as your age, lifestyle choices, present health, and family history determines how often you go back for check-ups.
