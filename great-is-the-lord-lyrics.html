The intention of the query was only to test Jesus how knowledgeable he is about the Scriptures.

Jesus gave him a clear answer: the greatest among many things that man could do is to love. All the wars, all the blood, all the sorrow and anguish would be erased with this continuous attitude and action. 29. Ebony and ivory,live together in perfect harmony side by side on my piano,keyboard,oh lord why wr dont we?can you determine the underlying meaning of the lyrics of the song


Answer:

At the simplest level, the song is about the ebony (black) and ivory (white) keys on a piano, but also deals with integration and racial harmony on a deeper level. The title was inspired by McCartney hearing Spike Milligan say "black notes, white notes, and you need to play the two to make harmony, folks!"
