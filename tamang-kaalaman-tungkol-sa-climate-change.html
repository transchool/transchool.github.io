3. Tamang kaalaman tungkol sa climate change


Climate change is a change in global or regional weather caused by increasing levels of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere since the Industrial Revolution, due to the use of fossil fuels.

Detail Explanation

Climate change is a change in weather that occurs globally or regionally caused by increasing levels of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

The causes of climate change include:

1. Energy production

Electrical and thermal energy is produced by burning fossil fuels, resulting in emissions of carbon dioxide and nitrous oxide, which are greenhouse gases that cause climate change.

2. Deforestation

Greenhouse gas emissions arise due to deforestation, because the felled trees will release the carbon stored in them. Because forests absorb carbon dioxide, their logging also results in reduced absorption of greenhouse gas emissions.

3. Use of transportation

Fossil fuels as a vehicle energy source cause climate change due to carbon dioxide gas emissions.

The impacts of climate change are:

Declining water qualityReduced quantity of waterHabitat changeExtinction of speciesThe decline in the quality and quantity of forestsIncreasing disease outbreaksThe ice at the poles is melting, so sea levels rise and submerge some coastal areas and small islands
