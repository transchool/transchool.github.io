Step-by-step explanation:

In this problem, we are given a simple age problem. We don't even need to use a variable for this since we can solve it using simple division.

If her mother is 62 years old, and Rowena's age is half that, we just need to divide this number by 2.

During the End of the Month Sale, Rowena bought a skirt that was on sale for 40% off. The original price of the skirt is ₱450.00. How much did Rowena pay?with solution po​


Answer:

STEP 1;

Identify what is being ask.

x=Total of Rowena Pay

Step 2;

Identify what is given.

25% employee discount

40% off

450.00 price of skirt

Step 3;

Formulate a Function

If there will be 25% off when she are purchasing at store,then it represented by 0.25x (need to convert).

The total sale off the skirt is should be added to 0.25x to get the total price of Rowena pay.

Thus,the linear equation is x=0.65x

In Function Form it is

f(x)=0.65x

Step 4;

Solve for what is being ask.

Substitute x=450 in the function;

f(x)=0.65x

f(450)= 0.65(450)

f(x)=292.5

So the total amount of Rowena paid is ₱292.5.
