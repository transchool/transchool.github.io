30. Complete the following sentences 1 We may have individual differences but 2 What I appreciate most with our differences in the family is 3. We celebrate our similarities by


Answer:

We may have individual differences but that does not mean we cannot unite and work as one. Our individual differences are what made us unique. If someone lacks an area of specialization, another one will contribute because he or she has that something to provide. What I appreciate most with our differences in the family is being able to do responsibilities based on our capabilities. I have the capacity to do some things but there are also areas I cannot. On the other hand, we have different lines of expertise and that is what I truly appreciate because, despite all the differences we have, we still continue to unite and work as one. We celebrate our similarities by inviting family members to share ideas and experiences. We should encourage everyone to share their hobbies, interests, dreams and etc. through reunions, siesta time, and special occasions to create a sense of unity and understanding.
