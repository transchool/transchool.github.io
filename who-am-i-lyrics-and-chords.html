11. Chord ID intersects chord AM in the interior of a circle at C. If segment IC is 4cm and segment CD is 6cm, find segment CM if segment AC is 3cm.


Answer:

8

Step-by-step explanation:

Let CM = x

Substitute,

IC * CD = AC * CM

4*6 = 3*x

24 = 3x

24/3 = 3x/3

8 = x

15. what is an angle whose vertex is on a circle and whos sides contain chords of the circle


Answer:

Inscribed angle

Step-by-step explanation:

An angle whose vertex lies on a circle and whose sides intercept the circle (the sides contain chords of the circle) is called an inscribed angle. The measure of an inscribed angle is half the measure of the arc it intercepts.
