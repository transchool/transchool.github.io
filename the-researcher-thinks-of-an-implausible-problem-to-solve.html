16. What refers to the incidence where an implausible concept or character is brought into the story in order to resolve the conflict and to bring about a pleasing solution? ​


Answer:

Deus ex Machina

Explanation:

Deus ex Machina is the incidence where an implausible concept or character is brought into the story in order to resolve the conflict and to bring about a pleasing solution. It is translated as 'God in the Machine' since the turn of events is comparable to a works of a god- impossible, incomprehensible and miraculous.

It is said that deus ex machina is a characteristic of bad writing since resolution is made through strange concepts that are out of place with the theme of the story. It is recommended to stick with the theme to give justice to how the story has gone so far.
