15. Pinoy ako explaination​


Answer:

Pinoy Ako (eng. I'm a Filipino) is the official theme song of Pinoy Big Brother, which is sung by the Filipino band Orange and Lemons.

The song has been used since the show's inception in 2005 and it has been part of Filipino pop culture.

This song is also the basis for much of the musical scoring used in the show.

The theme song is often used for Civilian Seasons. Celebrity Seasons and Teen Seasons have their own theme songs; "Sikat ang Pinoy" (eng. Filipinos are Famous) for Celebrity Edition and "Kabataang Pinoy" (eng. Filipino Youth) for the Teen Edition.
