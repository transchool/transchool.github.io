11. is the earth flat? and not an oblate spheroid?


The earth is round and it it a oblate spheroid
No, the earth is not flat. Since the Earth is flattened at the poles and bulges at the Equator, geodesy represents the figure of the Earth as an oblate spheroid. The oblate spheroid, or oblate ellipsoid, is an ellipsoid of revolution obtained by rotating an ellipse about its shorter axis.
