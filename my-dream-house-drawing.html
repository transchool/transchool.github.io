oftenevery yearsometimesonce a monthevery nightTime ExpressionsThey perform the same function as the adverb of frequency. These are words or phrases that tells when an action happened. This are sometimes located in the beginning or the end of a sentence.

Explanation:

Learning Task 1:

Identify the present time expressions used in the sentences below

1. Alex often draws his dream house.

2. Nina and Lino join this contest every year.

3. My tooth sometimes hurts whenever I drink cold beverages.

often,every year, sometimes,once a month,and every night are examples of present time expressions,used the present continuous with time expressions.

Examples of Present Time Expression:

every daytodayat the moment

Sentence:

My mom is working at home today.Worker are busy at the moment.I try to exercise every day.

Past time expressions are used to say when something happened in the past.

Examples of Past Time Expression:

yesterdaylast weeklast night

Sentence:

I finished my homework yesterday.We went shopping last week.I didn't sleep well last night.

Future time expressions are used to speak about something in the future.

Examples of Future Time Expression:

TommorowSoonnext week

Sentence:

I'm going to finish my homework tommorow.They'll sleep soonWe have new modules next week.
