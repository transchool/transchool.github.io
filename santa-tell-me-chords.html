Answer:

1. Radius

2. Chord

3. Diameter

4. Chord

5. Radius

Step-by-step explanation:

A radius is a line segment in which the points are the center of the circle and any point on the circle.

A diameter is a line segment in which the points are on the circle. It passes through the center point.

A chord is a line segment in which the points are on the circle. It doesn't pass through the center point.

30. Assume chords BE and CD are equal in length. What can we tell about the degree measure of Arcs BE and CD respectively?


Step-by-step explanation:

If chords BE and CD are equal in length, it means that they are congruent. This means that they have the same length and measure the same angle in the circle. Therefore, if Arc BE has a degree measure of x, then Arc CD would also have a degree measure of x.
