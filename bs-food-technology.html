4. In parallelogram BEST, diagonals BS and ET bisect each other at O.B1. If ES = 10cm, how long is BT?2. If BE = 13cm, how long is TS?3. If EO = 6cm and SO = 7cm, what is the length of ET? BS?4. If ET + BS = 18cm and SO = 5cm, find ET and BS.​


Answer with Step-by-step explanation:

(Final answers are underlined in bold fonts.)

1.)  ES = 10 cm

ES ≅ BT (Opposite sides of parallelogram are congruent.)

BT = 10 cm

2) BE = 13 cm

BE ≅ TS  (Opposite sides of parallelogram are congruent.)

TS = 13 cm

3)  Diagonals of parallelogram bisect each other.  Each diagonal divides the other into two congruent segments.

EO = 6 cm

EO + OT = ET;  EO ≅ OT

ET = 2 (EO)

ET = 2(6 cm)

ET = 12 cm.

SO = 7 cm

SO + OB = BS;  BO ≅ SO

BS = 2 (SO)

BS = 2(7 cm)

BS = 14 cm

4.) Diagonals of parallelogram bisect each other.  Each diagonal divides the other into two congruent segments.

ET + BS = 18 cm

SO = 5 cm

BS = SO + OB;   SO ≅ OB

BS = 2(SO)

BS = 2(5 cm)

BS = 10 cm

ET = 18 cm - BS

ET = 18 cm - 10 cm

ET = 8 cm.

30. Discuss the nature of BS in Forestry​


Answer:

The Bachelor of Science in Forestry (BSF) is a four-year degree program that helps students gain a thorough understanding of the forest ecosystem and how it can be properly conserved and managed. The course combines ecology, biology, economics, and social science to educate students on how to manage forest ecosystems and apply knowledge of forestry science to help solve natural resource, environmental and energy problems. The BS in Forestry program teaches students essential skills in the development of management plans and options for forested landscapes and implementing them while maintaining ecosystem integrity.
