1. good note taking during lectures

Note taking would require sharp attention to details and comprehension.  It is necessary to jot down only the important details of the discussion.  This will help in your analysis and retention of information.

2. use of index cards

When memorization is required, it will be essential to keep your notes in an index cards.  

3. scheduled reading  

Time management is always helpful in effective learning strategy.  Creating a study habit on a scheduled basis will help you focused while studying and monitor your study progress.

4. confidence and perseverance

One has to develop self-confidence and trust in order to establish motivation while studying.
