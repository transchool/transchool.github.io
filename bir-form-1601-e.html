16. 3. Discuss your prior knowledge of the following agency/institution:a. SSSb. GSISc. HDMFd. PhilHealthe. BIR​


Answer:

a. SSS or Social Security System

Social security refers to the protection that a society offers to people and households in order to secure access to health care and financial security.

b. GSIS or Government Servive Insurance System

is tasked with insuring the government's properties, assets, and interests against any insurable risk.

c. HDMF or Home Development Mutual Fund also known as Pag-IBIG Fund

They help their members by providing low-interest financing for their housing requirements.

d. PhilHealth or Philippine Health Insurance Corporation

Give financial aid and access to affordable health care for Filipinos

e. BIR or Bureau of Internal Revenue

It is responsible for assessing and collecting all national internal revenue taxes, fees, and charges, as well as enforcing any forfeitures, penalties, and fines connected therewith.

22. Ano ang sanhi at bunga sa paglaban sa cordillera noong 1601


Ang labanang naganap noong taong 1601 sa pagitan ng mga Katutubo ng Cordillera (Igorot) at ng mga Espanyol ay nagsimula noong nagtangkang pasukin at sakupin ng mga Espanyol ang kanilang lugar. Dahil dito, pinigilan ng mga Igorot ang pakay ng mga Espanyol, at ang labanang ito ay nagdulot ng maraming kamatayan sa hanay ng mga Espanyol na siya namang naging dahil kung bakit di tuluyang nasakop ang Cordillera.


23. The relationship between the BIR and a business?​


Question:

The relationship between the BIR and a business?​

Explanation:

As the taxing authority in the Philippines, the BIR regulates finance, taxation, and monetary policy in the country and collects internal revenue taxes, fees, and charges. It also enforces all forfeitures, penalties, and fines to individuals/organizations that fail to file their taxes.
