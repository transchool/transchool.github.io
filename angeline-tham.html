27. voice type of angeline quinto


Voice type of angeline quinto Angeline quinto is Mezzo-soprano type.

The mezzo-sopranos vocal range lies between the  soprano and the forms of contralto voices.  In general, mezzo-sopranos have a  deeper, darker sound than sopranos. The mezzo-soprano voice resonates at a  greater range than a contralto voice.  The words Dugazon and Galli-Marié, after the names of  popular singers, are often used to refer to light mezzo-sopranos.

Angeline Quinto is one of  today's must-hear, and must-see young artists.  Her continued success can be attributed to her charming appeal which makes her easily  likable as well as her own desire to develop her singing in all aspects.  These factors have contributed to  its growth and maturity in music.
