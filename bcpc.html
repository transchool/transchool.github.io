26. what is bcc in an email?


BCC Definition in Email

BCC in email stands for blind carbon copy. It is useful in sending emails to someone without the other recipient or recipients knowing that the email was also sent to another person. It is generally used in sending emails to bulk or multiple recipients and also protecting each one of them.

Benefits of Using BCC The privacy of the email address of the recipient in BCC is protected. The email address of the recipient in BBC will not received any forwarded email The person in BBC would be protected from unnecessary replies The BBC field also decreases the likelihood of spam
