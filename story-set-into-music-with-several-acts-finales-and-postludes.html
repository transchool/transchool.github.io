1. story set into music with several acts finales and postludes


Answer:

Opera

Explanation:

Opera is a story set into music with several acts, finales, and postludes. Music is a major element in operas- the plot and story is expressed through the music. Performers in opera are talented and well-trained actors, singers, and instrumentalists. Along with the music, choreography and blocking is an element that also colors the scenes of an opera.
