Ako ay pilipino lyrics by george canseco

George Canseco was the Philippine composer of various popular  Filipino songs from April 23, 1934 to November 19, 2004. Canseco graduated from the University of the East  in the Philippines, where he studied Liberal Arts.  After finishing his studies, he was a journalist for the Philippines Herald and the Associated Press.  He also openly employed  in Manila as a scriptwriter.  Canseco was named to create the national tribute hymn, "Ako ay Pilipino," by Imelda Marcos, the former first lady of Philippines.  He died from cancer on 19 November 2004, in Manila, Philippines.
