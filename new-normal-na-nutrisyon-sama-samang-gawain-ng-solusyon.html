Ang pagkain ng mga masusustansyang gulay ay kailangan ng ating katawan upang maging malusog, malakas at malayo sa iba’t ibang uri ng sakit. Katulad na lamang ngayong pandemya, samu’t-saring mga sakit ang naglipana sa ating lipunan at upang makaiwas sa mga ito ay kinakailangan ng katawan natin ng masusustansyang pagkain upang mapalakas ang ating katawan at labanan ang iba’t ibang uri ng sakit. Ang mga gulay ay mayayaman sa iba’t ibang uri ng bitamina na kinakailangan ng ating katawan tulad na lamang ng Vitamin C na nakakatulong upang maiwasan natin ang mga sakit tulad ng sipon, ubo lagnat at iba pa. Samantalang ang Vitamin B naman ay nakakatulong upang mas mapatalas ang ating pag-iisip at Vitamin A na nakakatulong upang maging malinaw ang ating mga paningin. Marami pang ibang uri ng bitamina ang makukuha natin sa pagkain ng mga gulay kaya kahit ayaw mo man itong kainin ay wala kang magagawa dahil kailangan mo itong kainin upang makamit ang malusog na pangangatawan.

25. write an essay about our nutrition month theme (not less than 250 words)THEME: "NEW NORMAL NA NUTRITION,SAMA-SAMANG GAWAN NG SOLUSYON"​


It's important to observe and celebrate Nutrition Month. Every country commemorates nutrition month yearly. Following nutrition month, we observe children parading and teachers educating students about the value of maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

"NEW NORMAL NA NUTRITION, SAMA-SAMANG GAWAN NG SOLUSYON", Why does this matter? Why do we need to understand its significance? Is it important? It is, indeed! This month's nutrition month's tagline, goal, and objective all revolve around it. It implies that in this new normal, we should all work together and prioritize our health in order to stop COVID-19 from becoming strong. A new disease is on the rise, so we need to be ready and take care of ourselves. The topic makes it abundantly evident why it's crucial to take part in Nutrition Month events. Why this theme when there are so many others available? We are having face-to-face courses and individuals are regaining their employment since our COVID cases are progressively declining, which is obviously a sign that the new normal has arrived. Furthermore, it is crucial for young individuals to understand the value of health throughout nutrition month. Being healthy includes not just having a good bodily condition but also having a good intellectual, social, emotional, and spiritual state of being.

In conclusion, we should always adhere to and believe that the theme is a crucial component of an event since it explains why the event is being held. This subject genuinely teaches us that despite the diseases and health issues that are part of the new normal, we can all find solutions to them. Once again, health is wealth!
