1. convert to the indicated unit of measure​


In the given problem, apply your knowledge in algebra and basic mathematics specially in converting different units of lenght and other measurements.

Solution and Answer

1. 708 mm = ____ m

1 m = 1000 mm

708 × 1 m/1000m

= 708/1000 m

= 0.708 m (answer)

2. 15.6 km = ____ dam

1 km = 100 dam

15.6 km × 100dam/1km

= 1560 dam (answer)

3. 108 cm³ = ____ milliliters

1 cm³ = 1 milliliters

108 cm³ × 1 milliliters/1cm³

= 108 milliliters (answer)

4. 8 tablespoon = ____ milliliters

1 tablespoon = 14.787 milliliters

8 tablespoon × 14.787 milliliters/1 tablespoon

= 118.296 milliliters (answer)

5. 16.5 L = ____ gallon

1 l = 0.264 gallon

16.5 L × 0.264 gal/1L

= 4.356 gallon (answer)

6. 3569 seconds = ____ days

1 day = 86400 seconds

3569 × 1 day/86400 seconds

= 0.041 day (answer)

7. 4 score = ___ months

1 score = 20 years, 1 year = 12 month

4 score × 20 year/1 score × 12 month/1 year

= 960 mo. (answer)

8. 299 K = ____ °C

K - 273.15 = °C

299 K - 273.15

= 25.85 °C (Answer)

9. 200 °F = ___ °C

(32°F − 32) × 5/9 = 0°C

(200°F - 32) × 5/9

= 93.33 °C (answer)

10. 10866 mg = ____ kg

1 kg = 1000000 mg

10866 mg × 1 kg/1000000mg

= 0.010866 kg
