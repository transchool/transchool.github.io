Here are some things to check before printing on long bond paper in epson l360.

2. Make sure the printer is connected to your PC, laptop, or any device you are going to use, which is compatible to the printer.
3. Check the inks of the printer.
.
Here's the procedure.
1. Open the document you are going to print.
