18. is sanctuary (by joji) a rejection song? confuse lang po​


According to the words in verse 2, this song actually suggests that Joji is singing for someone he loves or that the song is highlighting someone they both adore "Not anyone, you're the one More than fun. you're the Sanctuary". This could imply that Joji is singing for a special someone, depending on how the listeners interpret it and explain it.

According to the listener's perspective, the song may be dedicated to someone they love or to someone they rejected but still had feelings for.

George Kusunoki Miller, also known as Panggung Joji or Filthy Frank on YouTube, was born on September 18, 1992, and is an accomplished musician, songwriter, record producer, webmaster, and comedian. Originally from Kobe, Japan, this song is also known by the titles Slow Dancing in the Dark and Glimpse of Us.

Before being known as Joji, he was known as Pink Guy through his works Pink Guy (2014) and Pink Season (2017). He later became known as Joji through In Tongues (2017), Ballads 1 (2017), and Nectar (2020).

The song Sanctuary is an alternative/Indie song sung and composed by Joji. This 3-minute song was first released on the 88rising YouTube channel on June 14, 2019.

This song is also the first single song after Joji released his debut album titled BALLADS 1 which was released in 2018. Sanctuary itself is incorporated into one of the tracks on the Nectar album which Joji released in 2020.
