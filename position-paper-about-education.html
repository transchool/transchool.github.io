Younger generations overloaded with home tasks and numerous assignments have already raised this issue many times.  Yet, homework remains mandatory. Is it fair or should it be cancelled?  I believe that homework should not be viewed as a mandatory part of education for several reasons.  First, children spend seven hours every single workday at school. So, the whole idea of dedicating time to self- education is lost here.  Finally, knowing that after classes you would still have to work at home makes a kid less concentrated at school. However, in other cases, they should have time after school for other activities.
