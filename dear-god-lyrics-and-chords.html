20. Good for you lyrics dear evan hansen meaning


The song is about someone who died. Someone that had a blast with her own life. Even when she suffered before she bidded good bye, the narrator or singer was thankful that she's finally free. But along the way while moving on, the painful and the happiest memories kept on creeping in the singer's heart. He knows that he must be happy for her but the pain of losing her is simply unbearable. For his heart was left broken and empty ever since she was gone to the loving arms of God in heaven. In the end, the singer was hoping and praying that someday the pain will subside and the memories that he kept on reminiscing will finally bring joy.
