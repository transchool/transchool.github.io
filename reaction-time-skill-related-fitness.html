21. Explain each skill-related fitness component:AgilitySpeedPowerBalanceReaction TimeCoordination


Answer:

Speed - this measures the capacity of the body to move from one point to anotherfollowing a straight line.

Agility - is the ability of the body to shift direction from one place to another.

Balance - is the capacity of the body to maintain upright posture even when moving.

Coordination - when all the body parts are harmoniously working together tosuccessfully execute a movement. It is the ability to use the senses with the bodyparts to perform motor tasks smoothly and accurately.

Power - is the ability to transfer a certain amount of force at a rapid pace.

Reaction Time - the ability of a person to respond quickly to stimulus.
