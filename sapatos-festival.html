1. paano ipinagdiriwang ang sapatos festival?


Answer:

Sapatos Festival is a three-month-long festival that usually begins in September. It showcases the creativity, craftsmanship, style, and quality of shoes, a product Marikina is known for. The festival features parades, cultural presentations, trade fairs featuring shoes made in the city, visits to the shoe museum, among other fun activities.

Among the highlights of the festival is the Tour de Takong, where participants are required to wear stilettos at least three inches in height instead of the usual running shoes. Since the race started in 2011, it has attracted a multitude of participants from all over the country and is one of the highly-anticipated events in the city.

14. Marikina celebrates Sapatos Festival. In a certain booth, there are 10 new shoe designs to be displayed. In how many ways can these shoes be arranged if there are only 7 spaces available in a row?


Answer:

The number of ways to arrange 10 shoes in 7 spaces is equal to the number of combinations of 10 items taken 7 at a time, which can be calculated using the formula:

C(10,7) = 10! / (7! * 3!) = 120.

Therefore, there are 120 different ways to arrange the 10 shoes in 7 spaces.

Problem:

Marikina celebrates Sapatos Festival. In a certain booth, there are 10 new shoe designs to be displayed. In how many ways can these shoes be arranged if there are only 7 spaces available in a row?

Asked:In how many ways can these shoes be arranged if there are only 7 spaces available in a row?

Given:

Identify the given information:

10 = n (new shoe design)7 = r (space available row)

Formula:

A permutation is an arrangement of all or some of the objects in a set. Where, orders and matters.

Formula for PERMUTATION

P(n, r) = n!/(n - r)!

The number of permutation of n objects taken r at a time.

Where:

n = total number of objects r = objects taken for arrangementn > r (always n are greater than r)

Solution:

The arrangement of shoes in a rack is in linear form. If the number permutation of 10 objects taken 7 at a time. Solve the permutation using formula.

Formula in Linear Permutation

P(n, r) = n!/(n - r)!

Substitute the value of n and r

P(10, 7) = 10!/(10 - 7)!

Simplify (10 - 7)!

P(10, 7) = 10!/3!

Expand 10!

P(10, 7) = 10×9×8×7×6×5×4×3!/3!

Cancel 3! in a numerator and denominator

P(10, 7) = 10 × 9 × 8 × 7 × 6 × 5 × 4

Simplify

P(10, 7) = 604,800

Answer:

∴ Therefore, there are 604,800 ways in arranging 10 new shoe designs if there are 7 spaces.
