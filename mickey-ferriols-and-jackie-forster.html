13. mickey and kyle of masses 35 kg and 55kag respectively,are standing on a very slippery icy surface. kyle pushes mickey to the left with a force of 75N. find the acceleration of mickey


Answer:

Mickey's acceleration is 2.14 m/s²

Explanation:

Here, we are going to use the concept of Newton's second law of motion where the applied force has an equal opposite force where the weight of the object causes it to accelerate.

The formula to use is

F = ma

where

F     is the force, the unit is in Newtons (N)

m    is the mass, the unit is in kg

a     is the acceleration, the unit is in m/s²

Given information

m (Mickey) = 35 kg

m (Kyle) = 55 kg

F = 75 N

a = ?

Solving the problem

Let us solve for Mickey's acceleration using the formula

F = ma

Take note in Newton's second law of motion, the force applied is equivalent to the force exerted on the opposite side, therefore, Kyle's force will produce the same force on Mickey, therefore, Mickey will also have 75N force.

Substitute the given:

75N = (35 kg) a

Divide both sides of the equation by 35 kg

a = 75N / 35 kg

a = 2.14 m/s²

Therefore, Mickey's acceleration is 2.14 m/s²
