21. What 3 rules do the grammar of the english and filipino have in common?​


Answer:

The first grammar rule that English and Filipino have in common is the capitalization rule.  Proper nouns, acronyms, and titles must be capitalized.

Another grammar rule is the punctuation rule. Both English and Filipino sentences should use the appropriate punctuation mark based on the type of sentence.

Lastly, English and Filipino grammar have tenses of verbs. They use different forms of verbs based on their tenses. The three common tenses of the verbs they share are present, past, and future.
