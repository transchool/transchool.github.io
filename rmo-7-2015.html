Answer:

ExplaTumuturo dito ang Silay, para sa kalapit na bulkan, tingnan ang Silay (bulkan).

Ang Lungsod ng Silay ay isang ikatlong klaseng lungsod sa lalawigan ng Negros Occidental, Pilipinas. Ayon sa senso ng 2015, ito ay may populasyon na 126,930 sa may 5,557 na kabahayan. Bahagi ito ng Pook Metropolitan na tinatawag na Metro Bacolod, na kinabibilangan ng mga lungsod ng Bacolod at Talisay.[3] Sa lungsod matatagpuan ang bagong Paliparang Pandaigdig ng Bacolod-Silay; na pumalit sa Paliparang Domestiko ng Lungsod ng Bacolod.

Madalas tawaging "Paris ng Negros" ang Silay[4] dahil sa malawak nitong kalipunan ng mga napapanataling mga sinaunang kabahayan. Higit sa tatlumpo ng mga kabahayan na ito ay naitalagang mga makasaysayang pook ng Pambansang Komisyong Pangkasaysayan ng Pilipinas na bahagi ng Pambansang Palantaang Pangkasaysayan ng Silay. 19. in circle M,RE and IO are diameters and mIE =68°.find the following measures of an angle or an arc?1.mRI2.m<RMO3.mOEI4.mREO5.m<OME​


✒️CIRCLE

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{ANSWER}:} [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \large \: \rm 1) \; m\overset{\frown}{RI} = 112\degree [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \large \: \rm 2) \; m\angle RMO = 68\degree [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \large \: \rm 3) \; m\overset{\frown}{OEI} = 180\degree [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \large \: \rm 4) \; m\overset{\frown}{REO} = 292\degree [/tex]

[tex] \qquad \large \: \rm 5) \; m\angle OME = 112\degree [/tex]

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

[tex] \large\underline{\mathbb{SOLUTION}:} [/tex]

Number 1:

Segment RE is a diameter. Therefore,

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{RIE} = 180\degree [/tex]

Partition Postulate.

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{RI} + m\overset{\frown}{IE} = m\overset{\frown}{RIE} [/tex]

Substitution.

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{RI} + 68\degree = 180\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{RI} = 180\degree - 68\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{RI} = 112\degree [/tex]

Therefore, the measure of arc RI is 112°

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

Number 2:

Angles RMO and IME are vertical.

[tex] m\angle RMO = m\angle IME [/tex]

The measure of an angle is as same as its intercepted arc.

[tex] m\angle IME = m\overset{\frown}{IE} [/tex]

[tex] m\angle IME = 68\degree [/tex]

Substitution.

[tex] m\angle RMO = 68\degree [/tex]

Therefore, the measure of angle RMO is 68°

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

Number 3:

Segment IO is a diameter. Therefore,

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{OEI} = 180\degree [/tex]

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

Number 4:

Partition Postulate.

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{REO} + m\overset{\frown}{RO} = 360\degree [/tex]

Substitution from previous solutions.

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{REO} + m\angle RMO = 360\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{REO} + 68\degree = 360\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{REO} = 360\degree - 68\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\overset{\frown}{REO} = 292\degree [/tex]

Therefore, the measure of arc REO is 292°

[tex]••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••[/tex]

Number 5:

Segment IO is a diameter. Therefore,

[tex] m\angle OME + m\angle IME = 180\degree [/tex]

Substitution from previous solutions.

[tex] m\angle OME + 68\degree = 180\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\angle OME = 180\degree - 68\degree [/tex]

[tex] m\angle OME = 112\degree [/tex]

Therefore, the measure of angle OME is 112°
