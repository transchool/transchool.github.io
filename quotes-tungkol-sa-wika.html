12. quotes tungkol sa simbang gabi​


Tayo ay magsisimbang gabi, hindi simbang tabi.

Simbang gabi means, to attend nine days of worship in honor of Christmas celebration. It usually starts at the 16th of December and ends at the 24th day. Simbang tabi is attending the mass on those dates but your heart and feelings are not on the worship but rather, getting attention or making other agendas. This is a normal saying to filipino people to remind everyone the real meaning of simbang gabi.
