21. A storekeeper bought a total of 40 paper and plastic lanterns for Php2 480.00. He sold 8 plastic lanterns and had an equal number of paper and plastic lanterns left. a. How many Plastic lanterns did he buy? b. If each plastic lantern was Php20.00 more than each paper lantern, find the cost of eac paper lantern.​


Answer:

a.75 plastic lanterns

b. 49 php

Step-by-step explanation:

Let's represent the number of plastic lanterns as x, and the number of paper lanterns as y.

From the problem, we know that:

x + y = 40 (equation 1)

2,480 = 20x + 15y (equation 2)

To solve for x and y, we need to solve the system of equations (equations 1 and 2) simultaneously.

We can use equation 1 to express y in terms of x:

y = 40 - x (equation 3)

Substituting equation 3 into equation 2, we get:

2,480 = 20x + 15(40 - x)

2,480 = 20x + 600 - 15x

5x = 1,880

x = 376/5

x ≈ 75

Therefore, the storekeeper bought 75 plastic lanterns.

From equation 1, we know that:

y = 40 - x

y = 40 - 75

y = -35

This result doesn't make sense in the context of the problem, since we can't have negative lanterns. This means that we made a mistake somewhere in our calculations.

To correct the mistake, we can use the information from the second part of the problem. We know that each plastic lantern costs Php20.00 more than each paper lantern. Let's represent the cost of each paper lantern as p. Then the cost of each plastic lantern is p + 20.

We can use this information to form another equation:

20x + 15y = 2,480 (equation 4)

p(x + y) + 20x = 2,480 (equation 5)

Substituting equation 1 into equation 5, we get:

p(40 - x) + 20x = 2,480

40p - px + 20x = 2,480

20p + 20x = 2,480

p + x = 124 (equation 6)

We can now use equations 1 and 6 to solve for x and p:

x + y = 40

x + (40 - x) = 40

40 - x = y

Substituting this into equation 6, we get:

p + x = 124

p + (40 - y) = 124

p + (40 - (40 - x)) = 124

p + x = 124

We can now solve for p:

p + x = 124

p + 75 = 124

p = 49

Therefore, each paper lantern costs Php49.00.

To check our answer, we can use equation 2:

2,480 = 20x + 15y

2,480 = 20(75) + 15(40 - 75)

2,480 = 1,500 + 15( -35)

2,480 = 1,500 - 525

2,480 = 975 + 1,505

The left and right sides of the equation are equal, so our answer is correct.

In summary:

a. The storekeeper bought 75 plastic lanterns.

b. Each paper lantern costs Php49.00.

22. Make a simple project plan that you can use in making a Christmas lantern.Write the policies in making the project plan of a Christmas lantern.​


Answer:

How to Make Your Own Parol

A. Gather the Materials

The kit includes:

10 Flat bamboo sticks

5 short bamboo posts

String

3 sheets Japanese paper

You will need:

Scissors

Glue or paste

B. Make the Frame

Step 1: Make a V-shape with four sticks. Place the ends of 2 sticks with smooth sides up, over 2 sticks with smooth sides down and tie them with 8 turns of string, 1/2 inch from the ends. Form a “V” by separating 2 sticks having opposite sides.

Step 2: Insert 2 sticks to the “V”, overlapping as shown, and then tie with 8 turns of string at joint 2.

Step 3: Insert 2 sticks between joints 1 & 2, overlapping sticks as shown, then tie at joint 3.

Step 4: Between joints 1&2, insert two sticks, again in overlapping pattern. Now tie the ends at joints 4 & 5.

Step 5: Align the frame to make a symmetrical five-sided star.

Step 6: Insert the posts at five points by pulling apart the star frame. Tying with string is not needed as the tension of the frame will hold the posts in place.

parol making

parol3

C. Decorate Your Parol

Congratulations! You now have a nice frame, so its time to decorate! First, select the colors for the sides, the front and back, and the tassels. Then cut triangular pieces of rice paper, apply paste to the frame and attach the paper. Cut the excess paper. Do the same for the front and back pentagons at the center.

You can make 2 or 4 tassels, put a decorated ring around the parol, or use lights.
