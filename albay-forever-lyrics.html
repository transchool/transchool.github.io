7. Marikina and albay is around 480 km apart. The Rodriguez family left marikina for Albay at 3:15 am..If the average speed of the car they were riding was 64km/hour,what time did the Rodriguez family reach albay?​


Answer:

10:45 am

Step-by-step explanation:

Use distance = speed x time

Given na yung speed and distance

To get time, divide distance by speed

480 km / 64 km/hr = 7.5 hrs (or 7 hrs and 30mins)

Add 7.5 hrs (or 7hrs 30mins) to 03:15am kaya naging 10:45am

13. Interesting facts of Albay​


Answer:

legaspi

Explanation:

Legazpi, officially the City of Legazpi, is a 1st class component city and capital of the province of Albay, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 196,639. Legazpi is the regional center and largest city of the Bicol Region, in terms of population.

Explanation:

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano is the most popular scenic spot in Albay, Bicol and the most active volcano in the Philippines. It is still spewing steam as of this day. I did not mean to scare you. You don’t have to worry. It is closely monitored by our government agency PHILVOLCS (Philippine Institute of Volcanology and Seismology). In order to see its perfectly symmetrical shape like a cone, you have to see it early in the morning. Otherwise, clouds gather at the top.

Cagsawa Ruins

What remains of a structure you see in the following photo is the Cagsawa church which was built in 1587. It was buried during Mayon’s most explosive history on February 1, 1814. According to historical accounts, it was the most tragic event as hundreds of inhabitants of the town of Cagsawa were buried in it. They sought refuge in the church because of the abrupt nature of the eruption. Stream of lava and accompanying ash proved too much and swallowed up the church.

Daraga Church

Nuestra Señora de la Porteria Parish Church, popularly known as Daraga church was built by Franciscans in 1772. What’s interesting is the facade and its walls are made out of volcanic rocks. Its facade was coated with white lime in order to protect it from deterioration.

Where to eat

There are many restaurants that serve popular dishes of Bicol but 1st Colonial Grill proves to be the best. Bicol is famous for their spicy dishes cooked in coconut milk.
