29. What will happen to the verb if it is used in the past tense?(-d ored will be added to the base fort)​


Answer:

We add +ed to the end of a verb. Although this does depends on what type of verb we are converting into..

Explanation:

When a verb ends as a vowel + "y," we include "-ed". Example; "betray + ed = betrayed". If you encounter words like "say" and you need to convert it to past tense, NEVER add -ed for the verb.
