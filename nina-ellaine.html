10. 1)Ellaine has a rectangular gift wrapper that measure 30 cm by 25 cm.What isthe possible measure of a square each side which will not have aleftover?​


The possible measure of a square each side is 5 cm by 5 cm.

Step-by-step explanation:

Word Problem: Greatest Common Factor

The problem above is asking for the possible measure of a square each side that can be cut from a rectangular gift wrapper that measures 30 cm by 25 cm with no leftover. To find the answer, you have to find the greatest common factor of 30 and 25.

There are three ways to find the greatest common factor of the numbers: prime factorization, continuous division and listing method. Let us use the listing method since it is the easiest among the three.

G C F:

30 - 1, 2, 3, 5, 6, 10, 15, 30

25 - 1, 5, 25

Their greatest common factor is 5.

Therefore, the measure of each side is 5 cm.
