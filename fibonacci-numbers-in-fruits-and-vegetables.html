5. What is Fibonacci numbers


Answer:

Fibonacci numbers, commonly denoted Fn form a sequence, called the Fibonacci sequence, such that each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1.

Step-by-step explanation:

Fibonacci numbers are strongly related to the golden ratio: Binet's formula expresses the nth Fibonacci number in terms of n and the golden ratio, and implies that the ratio of two consecutive Fibonacci numbers tends to the golden ratio as n increases.

14. 1. Binet’s Formula states that the nth Fibonacci number is .(a) Use Binet’s Formula to find the 29th and 30th Fibonacci numbers.(b) Use the results of part (a) to find the 31st Fibonacci number.


Binet's Formula

Given in 1943 by Jacques Philippe Marie Binet. An explicit formula used to find the nth term of the Fibonacci sequence. The formula is f_n = [tex]\frac{1}{\sqrt{5} }[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ - [[tex]\frac{1 - \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ.  n is the number of terms in the Fibonacci sequence. This is derived from the general form of quadratic equation.

Solutions:

a. Given: Binet's Formula: f_n = [tex]\frac{1}{\sqrt{5} }[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ - [[tex]\frac{1 - \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ

               n = 29

f_n = [tex]\frac{1}{\sqrt{5} }[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ - [[tex]\frac{1 - \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]ⁿ

Find the 29th term of the Fibonacci sequence.

f₂₉ = [tex]\frac{1}{2.2360679775}[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + 2.2360670775}{2}[/tex]]²⁹ - [[tex]\frac{1 - 2.2360679775}{2}[/tex]]

f₂₉ = .447213595[[tex]\frac{3.2360679775}{2}[/tex]]²⁹ - [[tex]\frac{-1.2360679775}{2}[/tex]]²⁹

f₂₉ = 514,229

Find the 30th term of the Fibonacci sequence.

f₃₀ = [tex]\frac{1}{\sqrt{5} }[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]³⁰ - [[tex]\frac{1 - \sqrt{5} }{2}[/tex]]³⁰

f₃₀ = [tex]\frac{1}{2.2360679775}[/tex] [[tex]\frac{1 + 2.2360670775}{2}[/tex]]³⁰ - [[tex]\frac{1 - 2.2360679775}{2}[/tex]]³⁰
