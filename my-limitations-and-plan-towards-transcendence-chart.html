This is of course accommodated by having a set of habits that require some sort of consistency, so that we can say confidently that we have transcended. As for limitations, we should also be aware of our limits, that is because we would not want to exert our physical and mental beings.

Good habit includes waking up early in the morning and simply going to bed at 10pm. Some people underestimate this habit, however, I believe that this is easy and within everyone's limit. There can be other habits as well such as eating healthy, meditating, etc.Write in a journal about your daily/weekly/monthly progress. Writing a milestone is extremely important if we want to notice our progress. What happens to most people is that they quit after being satisfied.
