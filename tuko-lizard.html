Vertebrates are animals with backbones. They can be subdivided into five major groups: fishes, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals. Fishes are animals that generally have fins, gills, scales that allow them to survive in the aquatic environment. Most fish have a bony skeleton. However, sharks and rays have a skeleton made of cartilage, a strong and flexible tissue. Amphibians are cold-blooded animals that generally live in aquatic environments when young specifically when reproducing and in land when adults. Examples of amphibians are frogs, salmanders, and newt. Reptiles are also cold-blooded animals that lay eggs. Their body covering is hard and dry scales. Examples are alligators, snakes, lizards, and turtles. Birds are animals are warm-blooded animals and lay eggs. They also have feathers as their body covering. They have wings but not all of them can fly. Examples of birds are eagles, hawks, ostrich, flamingos, and ducks. Mammals are the most complex kind of vertebrate. Mothers have mammary glands that produce milk for their young. They have fur or hair as their body covering.  Examples of mammals are monkeys, bats, anteaters, dolphins, and human.

16. Kahulugan Kambal tuko


Answer:

Ang kahulugan ng kambal tuko ay pinagsama at ayaw mawalay sa isa't isa.

Explanation:

Ang kahulugan ng kambal tuko ay pinagsama at ayaw mawalay sa isa't isa.

Ang kambal tuko ay isang tambalang salita na ang ibig sabihin ay mahigpit ang kapit at ayaw bumitaw sa isa't isa. GInagamit ito kapag ang dalawang tao ay hindi mapaghiwalay ng pagkakataon o ng kahit na anong pangyayari.

Ang mga sumusunod ay mga pangungusap na kung saan ginamit ang salitang kambal tuko.

Para kayong kambal tuko, kung nasaan ang isa ay nandoon din ang isa.

Ang magkasintahan na Mario at Rosa ay parang kambal tuko.

Ang mag-asawa ay tila kambal tuko sa paghamon sa problema.

Ang magkaibigan na Ana at Ester ay kambal tuko dahil sabay palaging umuuwi galing eskwela.

Kung sila ay kambal tuko, sigurado na sila ay magtatagal dahil tiyak na kung nasaan ang isa, susunod din ang isa.
