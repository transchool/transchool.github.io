19. my weakness when i was elementary ​


Weakness

All of us might have it; it can find you challenging since you want to correct and improve your weaknesses. And these are the downsides that we have because we are not all perfect. But we can get through with it in time, so take it to step by step.

My weakness when I was an elementary studentFear of failure

I always wanted what my parents to see was the best of me. In terms of academics, when I was an elementary student, I pursued myself to be on the list of honors. And that leads me to fear failure, which is that I need to strive more as hard as possible even though it exceeds my limitations as a kid. But this weakness makes me more stressed, wherein if I fail, I would see myself as a worthless child. My parents wanted me the best at school with high expectations; nevertheless, it resulted in some weakness in my life that gave me anxiety and stress in my mind.

Nowadays, we might struggle to cope with our weaknesses in life. We do our best so that we can get through with it. And we see those weaknesses as a strength if we overcome them step by step. It might be hard on the first part, but it is all worth it in the end. If you face all those weaknesses you have in life, you may see yourself as a growing person and go for the best you can to get rid of them. Sometimes, it might not let you go, but it lessens that before.

Remember:

Do not worry if you have weakness in life, and it is all normal. And do not see it as a negative part ourselves but a challenge to overcome to improve more. Do not let yourself be down on this weakness, and focus on the strengths you have now.

27. similarities in all stages of my self in elementary


My similarities in all stages of my self in elementary are loving to learn and explore new things.

One of the first things you can do to learn a new thing/skill is to think about how to use it in its simplest form. For example, if you want to learn to play the piano, make your initial goals simpler by applying simple chord progressions and melodies, such as pop songs.

This allows you to start working on understandable concepts that you can execute instead of reading a lot of theories that might not be applicable.

Here are some of the benefits you get by learning new things:

Sparking Fresh Ideas and Creativity

By trying new things, the brain will indirectly connect neuron cells to get ideas. After getting a new idea then you can develop it into creativity. You will learn from the new ideas that you find so that one day you can find brilliant creativity.

Know Yourself Better and Find Your Passion Within

By doing new things you will better understand what really interests you and your passion. In addition, you will also understand what your real strengths and talents are. If you already know all that, then you just need to hone it so that it gets better and can produce achievements someday.

Increase Ability (Skill)

Be brave to try new things that you haven't done so far so you can find out what your strengths and talents are. The more often it is honed, the better your talents and strengths will be, so it is possible to achieve achievements from these abilities.

Overcoming Fear

Many people actually want and are interested in doing something different than usual, it's just that fear overpowers them. Ignore all fears by daring to do what you love. Through new things, you can gradually let go of the fear that haunts you.

Adding Life Experience

Experience will teach what is good and what is not, what is allowed and what is forbidden. You will not gain experience if you do not dare to try new things.
