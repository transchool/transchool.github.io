Si Private William W. Grayson ang sundalong bumaril sa isang kawal na Pilipino gabi noong Pebrero 4, 1899 sa Santa Mesa, Maynila na naging hudyat ng digmaan sa pagitan ng mga Pilipino at Amerikano, kaya ang tamang sagot ay letrang B.

Noong Pebrero 4, 1899, bandang alas nwebe ng gabi, Si Private William W. Dahil dito, nagkaroon ng putukan sa pagitan ng mga Pilipino at mga Amerikano, na kinilala bilang Labanan sa Maynila noong 1899. Lalo pang lumaki ang gulong ito at naging isang ganap na digmaan.

29. Luca and William are playing a video game and they have scored a total of 20,000 points. Luca scored 4,000 more points than William. If you let l= the number of points that Luca scored, and w= the number of points that William scored, then the problem can be represented by the system: l+w=20,000 and l=w+4,000 Graph the system. How many points did each boy score? a. William = 8,000 and Luca = 12,000 b. William = 12,000 and Luca = 8,000 c. William = 10,000 and Luca = 10,000 d. William = 4,000 and Luca = 16,000


L + W = 20,000

L = W + 4, 000

Use the substitution method

L + W = 20,000

(W + 4000) + W = 20,000

2W + 4000 = 20,000

2W = 20,000 - 4000

2W = 16,000

W = 8,000 William's score

L = W + 4000

L = 8,000 + 4000

L = 12,000 Luca's score

Answer:

A. William = 8,000 and Luca = 12,000
