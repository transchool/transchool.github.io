13. Prayer for Christmas party​


My prayer for the Christmas party is:

God the Father in heaven, we praise You and thank You for Your words that became flesh by being present in our midst. He became a weak human so that we who are fragile and mortal are anointed by eternal divine power.

FURTHER PRAYERS:

Almighty Father,

Let alone rejoice for the vindication of His birth. Let the sick and weak rejoice because their Saviour is born. Let the captives rejoice because their Redeemer is born. Let slaves rejoice because their Master is born. because Jesus Christ was born.

God, our Father who is in heaven. On this holy Christmas day, I remember all Your goodness, in my life throughout this year. Thank you, Lord, for Your guidance and care, Your endless love from eternity to eternity. Your love is extraordinary, O Father, so that You have given Your only-born Son into this world to atone for my sins.

Throughout this year I have experienced so many ups and downs, but one thing I believe in, namely Your inclusion is always with me.  You are a good Father, who always gives good and perfect things to me. Thank you Father. I pray and give thanks only in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Hallelujah, Amen.

28. suggest closing prayer for christmas party. ​


This is an example of Christmas Prayer.

God the Father in heaven, we praise You and also thank You for Your words that became flesh by being present in our midst. He became a weak human so that we who are fragile and mortal are anointed by eternal divine power.

With His birth in this world, You who cannot be seen now appear as human like us, and Your light of salvation shines in our midst, dispelling the darkness that overwhelms us.

Lord Jesus Christ, we ask You to help all of us who are involved in the preparation for worship and Christmas celebrations so that we may cooperate well with one another, and prepare everything as well as possible. The most important thing is that we always remember that this is all for the glory of Your name, to please You and not for our pride or satisfaction.

Lord Jesus Christ, how happy we are because soon we will celebrate your birthday. In many of Your churches, Lord, you are starting to see various activities in order to prepare for worship and Christmas celebrations. Our hearts rejoice and with enthusiasm we also take part in the ministry of preparing the anniversary of Your birth.

Pour out Your grace so that we, who are now celebrating the mystery of the incarnation, have the courage to be peacemakers for others, and in this way we also become means of Your incarnation among them. By the intercession of Christ, Our Lord, now and forever.
