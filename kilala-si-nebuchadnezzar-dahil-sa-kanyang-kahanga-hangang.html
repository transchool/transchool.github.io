4. kilala si nebuchadnezzar dahil sa kanyang kahanga-hanga:​


Answer:

Kilala si Nebuchadnezzar dahil sa kanyang kahanga-hangang Hanging Gardens. Si Nebuchadnezzar II ay ang hari ng Babylon noong 605 BC – 562 BC.

Siya ay itinampok sa Aklat ni Daniel o Book of Daniel dahil siya ang nakawasak sa sinasabing temple ni Solomon. Subalit mas kilala siya dahil sa itinayo niyang kahanga-hangang hanging gardens na tinawag na Hanging Gardens of Babylon.

Ito ay itinayo niya upang pasiyahin ang kanyang asawang si Amytis ng Media. Nais ng asawa niyang makatunghay ng mga puno at iba’t ibang halaman sa kanyang baying pinagmulan kung kaya’t naitayo ito. Subalit ang mga halamang nakasabit ay nasira dahil sa pagyanig ng lupa o lindol.

Answer:

Explanation:

The hanging gardens of babylon were one of the SEVEN WONDERS OF THE ANCIENT WOLD listed by HELLENIC culture. They were described as a remarkable feat of engineering with an ascending series of tiered,shrubs,and vines,resembling a large green mountain constructed of mud bricks. It was said to have been built in the ancient city of BABYLON,near present-day HILLAH, BABIL province, in IRAQ .The Hanging Gardens' name is derived from the Greek word kremastós (overhanging),which has a broader meaning than the modern English word "hanging" and refers to trees being planted on a raised structure such as a terrace
