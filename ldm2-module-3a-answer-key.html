14. what is the answer of (3a-b)²​


Answer:

9a² - 6ab + b²

Step-by-step explanation:

Definition of Terms :

FOIL Method - When multiplying binomials, get the product of the First two terms, Outer terms, Inner terms and Last terms :

(a + b)(c + d)

First terms are a and c.Outer terms are a and d.Inner terms are b and c.Last terms are b and d.

If multiply the binomials you will get a product of :

(a + b)(c + d) = ac + ad + bc + bd

Solution :

Expand the Expression

(3a - b)² = (3a - b)(3a - b)

Apply FOIL Method

F = 3a • 3a = 9a²

O = 3a • -b = -3ab

I = -b • 3a = -3ab

L = -b • -b = b²

Combine Terms

9a² - 3ab - 3ab + b²

9a² - 6ab + b²

Therefore, the answer is :9a² - 6ab + b²

You can also use this equation but Only for Squaring Binomials.

(a + b)² = a² + 2ab + b²

(3a - b)² = (3a)² + 2(3a)(-b) + (-b)²

= 9a² - 6ab + b²
