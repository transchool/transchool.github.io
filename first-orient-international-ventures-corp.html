12. was there a difference in how filipino artist first ventured into the world of digital art?if yes explain briefly​


Answer:

True

Explanation:

Filipino artists were influenced by the technology trend in art. At first, it was more in commercial and media sphere.

From the 1960s to the 1990s, Filipinos computer-generated works were primarily geared towards illustrating for international viewings and publishing such as comic books.

Filipino illustrators earned quite a reputation for their talents and were highly in demand in the field of technology-based art. These kind of artists became sought after as animators and graphic and motion artists for some of the major film production companies in U.S.A. and some other countries, too.

30. why do companies have to study their environment first before venturing into new businesses?​


Answer:

From getting to know your target audience to exploring potential competitors, market research gives businesses a competitive edge, allowing them to thrive in new environments.

Explanation:

Before you begin making any big steps in business, you need to ensure there’s an audience for you to sell to. Without customers, there are no sales. Market research will determine if you have a potential audience and where they’re hiding.

If no one’s interested in the product or service you’re offering, you’ll need to reconsider if starting a new business in that direction is the best idea. On the other hand, if there is a promising audience, it’s important you make the right decisions regarding where and how to sell to these customers.
