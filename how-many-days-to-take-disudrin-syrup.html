7. apple syrup and water in the ratio 2:3..1800ml of such fruit juice. how many ml of apple syrup?​


Answer:

720 mL of apple syrup

Step-by-step explanation:

2:3 = 1800

2x + 3x = 1800

5x = 1800

5x/5 = 1800/5

x = 360

Substitute x as 360

2x

2(360)

= 720

There is 720 mL of apple syrup in the fruit juice.

3x

3(360)

= 1080

There is 1080 mL of water in the fruit juice.

Checking:

2:3 = 1800

2x + 3x = 1800

2(360) + 3(360) = 1800

720 + 1080 = 1800

1800 = 1800
