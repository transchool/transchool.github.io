13. In a barangay, the number of volunteers increased from 40 to 50. What was the percent increase in the number of volunteers? ​


Problem:

In a barangay, the number of volunteers increased from 40 to 50. What was the percent increase in the number of volunteers?

Solution:

[tex]op = 40[/tex]

[tex]np = 50[/tex]

[tex] \red{pi = \frac{50 - 40}{40} \times 100}[/tex]

[tex]pi = \frac{10}{40} \times 100[/tex]

[tex]pi = \frac{10}{4} \times 10[/tex]

[tex]pi = \frac{100}{4} [/tex]

[tex] \purple{pi = \boxed{25\%}}[/tex]

Answer:

25% was the percent increase in the number pf volunteers

Additional Knowledge:To find the percent of increase:

[tex] \blue{pi = \frac{np - op}{op} \times 100}[/tex]

To find the percent of decrease:

[tex] \blue{pd = \frac{op - np}{op} \times 100}[/tex]
