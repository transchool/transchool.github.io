The type of annuity used by Eli in the illustration above is Simple annuity.

Understanding Annuity

Annuity is an installment payment or receipt of a fixed value if paid or received within a certain period of time. Annuities are intended to make it easier for customers to pay the installment amount for each period because the amount of the payment is fixed. Generally, annuities are used in calculating interest on loans and interest on long-term investments.

More broadly, the term annuity is also used in insurance products. In the world of insurance, it can be understood as a pension benefit that is paid every month. An annuity is a contract between an employee and an insurance company in which each employee makes different installments and in return receives regular payments after a specified period expires.

Meanwhile, in the investment world, annuities are used for future financial planning to provide a steady stream of income during retirement. These funds will be paid regularly by employees while they are still working and the amount will definitely increase every year until retirement.

In the world of banking, annuities are used to calculate the amount of interest or investment. The goal is to make it easier for customers to pay and get a return on their investment.
