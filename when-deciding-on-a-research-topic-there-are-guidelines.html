6. When deciding on a research topic, there are guidelines that we must follow. these is _____,____,______,______ and _____. These guidelines must be considered because___________.​


When deciding on a research topic, there are guidelines that we must follow. 

These are:

have benefits for otherswell controlled by the researcher,topic must be interestingdiscussion of the topic should be adequate,and determination of interesting and good topics

These guidelines must be considered because: It allows for more focus and helps in identifying the important areas of the possibilities for exploration and analysis of the areas.

EXPLANATION:

There is basically no secret or special method for writing research topics. For professional researchers, I believe they also have their own way of determining research topics. Meanwhile for beginners, some are still confused about how to write down research topics. So, when creating a research topic, pay attention to several points that can be taken into consideration in determining the topic.

1. The topic raised has benefits for other people

The topics raised have value for other people (society) and deserve to be discussed. If the topic is less interesting and has no impact on society, it is advisable to look for another topic.

2. The topic must be well mastered by the researcher

The way to write down a research topic that is no less important is to make sure you master the topic. Topics that are not mastered will affect the performance and motivation to complete the research.

3. The topic must be interesting

Interestingly, in this case, there are common threads, conflicts or emotions that can be dissected. Of course based on existing data and facts.

4. Adequate Topic Discussion

If you have difficulty determining the topic and you don't understand the topic taken, then you can do this by reading references so that you have adequate knowledge. Mastery of good material, of course, will affect you in conveying the message.

5. Define interesting and good topics

Some quick tips, as follows:

Topics can be taken from old topics, meaning not new topicsNot too technical because it eliminates the scientific impressionDo not take topics that are controversial in nature, because if forced to keep taking them, data collection can cause problems.
