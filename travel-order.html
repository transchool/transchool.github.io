19. which shows the correct order of food traveling through the digestive system after it is as swallowed​


Answer:

Esophagus ⇒ Stomach ⇒ Small intestines ⇒ Large intestines (Colon) ⇒ Rectum ⇒ Anus  

Explanation:

The digestive system consists of the digestive tract and other organs that help with digestion. The four vital functions of the digestive system are motility, digestion, absorption and secretion. Digestion involves the breakdown of food into smaller components so that nutrients can be absorbed into the body for important things like energy, growth and cell repair.  

The digestive system is mainly composed of the mouth, the esophagus, the stomach, the small intestine and the large intestine (or also called the colon). It also includes accessory digestive organs like the liver, pancreas and gallbladder.
