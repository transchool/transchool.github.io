What is a Yell in a sports competition?              

Yell- is a loud shout with a chant. It is most performed in a sports day to motivate sports teams and to entertain the audience. It is an activity in which the members cheer to promote the team they represent.

What is a team?

A team is a group of players working together to win for their team in a competitive game or sport.

What is a Sports Day?

A sports day are events in school in which students participate in sporting activities with the aim of winning trophies, medals and other prizes.

Important of Sports Day

Sports Day is important because it helps to develop students mental and physical toughness. It helps to develop also their sense of friendliness and being a good sport
