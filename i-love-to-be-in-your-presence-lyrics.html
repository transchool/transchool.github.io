18. How can I let the marginalized members of our society experience God’s loving presence in their conditions?​


1. Provide access to spiritual resources: Offer resources such as Bibles, devotional books, and Christian literature to those who may not have access.

2. Create a safe space: Invite marginalized members of your community to church services, meetings, and other gatherings. Make sure they feel welcome and accepted.

3. Show compassion and understanding: Listen to their stories and experiences without judgment. Show them kindness and respect.

4. Serve with love: Find ways to serve those in need. Offer practical assistance such as food, clothing, or transportation.

5. Pray with them: Pray for their physical, emotional, and spiritual needs. Ask God to give them hope and healing.

6. Share God’s message: Share the Good News of Jesus Christ with those who may not have heard it. Emphasize God’s love, mercy, and grace.
