Act 1: Introduction

The protagonist is a shy and nervous individual who has been harboring feelings for their crush.

We see the protagonist sitting in their room, rehearsing what they want to say to their crush.

Act 2: Rising Action

The protagonist spends the day psyching themselves up for the confession and trying to find the perfect moment to do it.

They have a few close calls, but ultimately chicken out each time.

Act 3: Climax

The protagonist finally gets the courage to confess to their crush.

They stumble over their words at first, but eventually manage to articulate their feelings.

Act 4: Falling Action

The crush is surprised but receptive to the confession, and they have a heart-to-heart conversation about where to go from here.

The protagonist is relieved and grateful to have gotten their feelings off their chest.

Act 5: Resolution

The protagonist and their crush agree to take things slow and see where their feelings lead them.

The protagonist walks away from the conversation with a newfound sense of confidence and hope for the future.
