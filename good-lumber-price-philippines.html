The would-be importer cannot get ufficient credit from dometic ource to pay for the hipment but init that the finihed lumber can quickly be reold in the Philippine for a profit. Outline the tep the exporter hould take to export to the Philippine effectively.


ANSWER:

To export finished lumber to the Philippines effectively, the exporter based in California should follow these steps:

• Research the market in the Philippines to determine the demand for the finished lumber and the potential profit from selling it there.

• Develop a plan for exporting the lumber to the Philippines, including the transportation method, estimated shipping time, and potential challenges or risks.

• Contact potential buyers in the Philippines to negotiate the sale of the lumber and establish a payment plan that is acceptable to both parties.

• Coordinate with a freight forwarder or shipping company to arrange for the transportation of the lumber from California to the Philippines.

• Prepare the necessary documentation and paperwork for exporting the lumber, including any export licenses, certificates of origin, or other required documents.

• Ship the lumber to the Philippines and track its progress to ensure that it arrives safely and on time.

• Follow up with the buyer in the Philippines to ensure that the sale of the lumber is completed and the payment is received as agreed.

By following these steps, the exporter can effectively export finished lumber to the Philippines and generate a profit from the sale.
