» Reflection – is when light bounces off an object.

» Plane mirror – is a mirror with a flat reflection surface.

» Specular Reflection – is a reflection of light on smooth surface such as mirrors on calm body of water.

» Diffused Reflection – is a reflection of light on rough surface such as clothing, paper, wavy water and the asphalt roadway.
