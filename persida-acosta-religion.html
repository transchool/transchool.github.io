23. Mr. Acosta baked 120 cookies. He placed 1 ¼ dozen cookies in small boxes. How many Boxes did he use?15. What operation to be used?_________________16. Write the mathematical sentence?​​


15. Division operation

16. Mathematical sentence:

120 cookies ÷ 1 ¼ dozen cookies = N

To solve this, convert 1 ¼ dozen to its equivalent number of cookies.

1 dozen = 12 cookies

¼ dozen = ¼ x 12 = 3 cookies

So, 1 ¼ dozen = 1 x 12 + 3 = 15 cookies

Now we can solve the number of boxes:

120 cookies ÷ 15 cookies/box = 8 boxes

Therefore, Mr. Acosta used 8 small boxes.
