In a literary work, it contains the meaning of the background story. Usually this story setting is the main thing in a literary work to describe how the situation is in the literary work. Understanding the setting of the story contains how the atmosphere is in the events of a literary work, where they are located, and when they occur.

The setting of the story is also an intrinsic element in a literary work that cannot be left behind when writing or creating a literary work. An article cannot be called a literary work or a story if it does not contain the meaning of the background story in it. About Character of story

Every story must have elements of characters in it. Characters are not only limited to the roles played by humans.

In stories, characters can be animals, objects, or any character that represents the course of a story.

Characters can be in the form of personal pronouns, such as I, you, he, they, and so on. The use of pronouns of this kind clarifies the point of view of the story.

Characters are the actors in the story, while characterizations are traits that are attached to the characters themselves, depictions, or descriptions of the story characters.
