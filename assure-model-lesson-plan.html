20. How does planning prior to laying out appliques assure the quality of the finished article?


Answer:

Planning prior to laying out appliques helps assure the quality of the finished article because it allows the person creating the appliques to consider factors such as the size and placement of the appliques, the type of fabric and thread to be used, and the overall design of the finished product. By carefully considering these factors before beginning the appliqué process, the person can ensure that the finished article will have a professional, cohesive appearance and that the appliques will be securely attached to the fabric. Planning also allows for any potential problems or challenges to be identified and addressed before they become issues during the actual appliqué process, which can help to prevent errors or mistakes that could compromise the quality of the finished product.
