BCC in email stands for blind carbon copy. It is useful in sending emails to someone without the other recipient or recipients knowing that the email was also sent to another person. It is generally used in sending emails to bulk or multiple recipients and also protecting each one of them.

If repeated random samples of a given size n are taken from a population of values for a quantitative variable, where the population mean is μ (mu) and the population standard deviation is σ (sigma) then the mean of all sample means (x-bars) is population mean μ (mu).

The Sampling Distribution of the Sample Mean is the distribution of all possible sample means of a given sample size.
