20. ano ang ifugao?saan matatagpuan ang ifugao?​


Answer:

Ifugao (Ilocano: Probinsia ti Ifugao; Tagalog: Lalawigan ng Ifugao) is a landlocked province of the Philippines in the Cordillera Administrative Region in Luzon. Its capital is Lagawe and it borders Benguet to the west, Mountain Province to the north, Isabela to the east, and Nueva Vizcaya to the south.

30. What dance among the ifugaos imitates the high flying birds?


The dance is called Lumagen or Tachok, which is a Kalingan festival dance.  

The Ifugao tribe in Kalinga, specifically the maidens, perform this dance during events of happy celebrations such as births, weddings, and peace pacts. The music accompanying the dance comprises of gongs or gangsa. The dance has a moving characteristic of high flying birds.
