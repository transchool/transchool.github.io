22. Write an essay about DepEd order No. 49 series of 2022 (teachers not being allowed to socialize with their students unless they are relatives) agree or disagree. 2 paragraphs only write on a one whole sheet of paper and then use at least 3 opinion marking signals. ​


I personally disagree with DepED Order No. 49 series of 2022 which says that teachers are not allowed to socialize with their students unless they are relatives because I think socializing is a right for everyone, including between teachers and students. Although it is said that the purpose of this rule is to prevent bias and unwanted things from happening between students and teachers, in my opinion, there is no need for this kind of rule.

        Schools can provide clear direction and regulation regarding boundaries and good ways of socializing between pupils and teachers. From my point of view this is a better action than blocking their socialization because apart from the bias that may occur, socialization between students and teachers outside of school activities is not entirely bad because it allows them to become close and makes their relationship less rigid and creates an atmosphere learning to be fun.

The opinion markers used in the text above are: I think, in my opinion, from my point of view.
