What is intuition?

Intuition is one thing that is often used by business people in making decisions that are combined with existing insights and data. These three combinations are, in fact, able to produce more profitable decisions.

How to use intuition to make business decisions?

An intuition expert named Daniel Kahneman, Ph. D, said that there are two systems of thinking, namely fast-thinking systems and slow-thinking systems, which are influenced by various data.

Generally, most of us prefer to use data rather than intuition. Daniel argues that intuition is our knowledge of the right answer and things without doing any further analysis.

Intuition is often underestimated and not used as a method of making the most important decisions. In fact, intuition often gives the right answer when applied to an environment that is orderly, stable, and easy to predict.

Intuition can be used before deciding whether or not to use data before making a decision, especially when looking at trends and also segmentation from various classes.
