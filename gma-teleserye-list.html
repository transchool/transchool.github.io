30. In a survey of 500 househokfs, 60% prefer abs-cbn over gma. How many households prefer gma? Need solution! ​


200 households prefer GMA

Step-by-step explanation:

1. Find the 60% of 500:

500 x 0.60 = 300 (prefers ABS-CBN)

2. Subtract the answer from total number of households:

500 - 300 = 200 (prefers GMA)
