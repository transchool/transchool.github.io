2. What is the decalogue kartilya ng katipunan by emilio jacinto


Answer:

The Kartilya ng Katipunan (English: Primer of the Katipunan[1]) served as the guidebook for new members of the organization, which laid out the group's rules and principles. The first edition of the Kartilya was written by Emilio Jacinto. Andrés Bonifacio later wrote a revised Decalogue. The Decalogue, originally titled Katungkulang Gagawin ng mga Z. Ll. B. (Duties of the Sons of the People), was never published because Bonifacio believed that Jacinto's Kartilya was superior to what he had made.

Explanation:

Answer:

Emilio Jacinto (December 15, 1875 – April 16, 1899) was a Filipino General during the Philippine Revolution. He was one of the highest-ranking officers in the Philippine Revolution and was one of the highest-ranking officers of the revolutionary society Kataas-taasan, Kagalang-galang na Katipunan ng mga Anak ng Bayan, or simply and more popularly called Katipunan, being a member of its Supreme Council. He was elected Secretary of State for the Haring Bayang Katagalugan, a revolutionary government established during the outbreak of hostilities. He is popularly known in Philippine history textbooks as the Brains of the Katipunan while some contend he should be rightfully recognized as the "Brains of the Revolution" (Filipino: Utak ng Himagsikan, a title that is usually given to Apolinario Mabini). Jacinto was present in the so-called Cry of Pugad Lawin (or Cry of Balintawak) with Andrés Bonifacio, the Supremo (Supreme President) of the Katipunan, and others of its members which signaled the start of the Revolution against the Spanish colonial government in the islands.

23. What is the contribution and relevance of Emilio Jacinto’s Kartilya ng Katipunan in understanding the grand narrative of Philippine History?


The document's contribution to understanding the major historical narrative in the Philippines The secret society's norms and values are laid out in the Kartilya ng Katipunan, which served as the moral and intellectual compass for the Katipuneros.

Further explanation:

The most well-known Katipunan text is Kartilya, which is also the only longer Katipunan print before August 1896 that is presently known to exist. This can be regarded as the Katipunan code of conduct, which outlines fourteen guidelines for the proper conduct for katipuneros. For the Katipunan members, it serves as their main instruction. It primarily addresses one's obligations to God, his or her country, family, friends, the Katipunan, and oneself.

As well as a warning of punishment for the treacherous and disobedient, it also speaks of dignity, generosity, and self-sacrifice. It includes a code of behavior that was created by our heroes who battled to free the Philippines from Spanish colonial rule. The importance of Emilio Jacinto's Kartilla ng Katipunan in understanding the grand arc of Philippine history is that it encourages Filipinos to sincerely love their country and take stock of their own lives. It teaches us to have sincere compassion for one another and that regardless of our wealth or level of education, you and I are true brethren and equals.

One might examine the value defended in the document as congruent with the developing rational and liberal concepts in the eighteen and nineteenth centuries. The goal of this is to remove a colonial regime. Kartilya was educational for the members' growth as individuals in their own right as well as for the Katipunan's behavior toward other people. The Katipunan's pivotal role in the revolution and the development of Philippine history is of paramount importance.

24. Ano ang kahalagahan ng Kartilya ng Katipunan ni Emilio Jacinto sa panahon ng kauna-unahang armadong rebolusyon sa Pilipinas​


Answer:

Ang Kartilya ng Katipunan ay isinulat ni Emilio Jacinto na tinaguriang Utak ng Katipunan. Dito nakasulat ang mga bagay na kinakailangang alalahanin ng mga bagong miyembro. Ito ang nagsisilbing gabay sa mga miyembro ng nasabing KKK o Kataas-taasan Kagalang-galangan Katipunan ng mga Anak ng Bayan. Ito ay mainam na instrument upang magkaroon ng gabay ang mga kasapi o miyembro nito sapagakat kung wala nito ay maaaring magkaroon ng kalituhan sa kung ano nga ba ang dapat and hindi dapat gawin ng bawat kasapi. Ito ay hindi lamang gabay bagkus ay isang paraan upang maipakita na ang KKK ay isang organisadong grupo.
