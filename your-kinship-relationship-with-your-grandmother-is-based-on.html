30. Cite the kinship relations that exist in your family circle by means of affinity relationship​.


Answer:

In my family circle, there are several kinship relations that exist through affinity relationships. My parents are married to each other, which means that my mother is the mother-in-law of my father and my father is the father-in-law of my mother. My siblings and I are the children of my parents, so we are their offspring. My grandparents are the parents of my parents, so they are our grandparents. I also have uncles and aunts on both my mother's and father's side, as well as cousins who are the children of my uncles and aunts. All of these kinship relations exist through affinity relationships, as they are all connected through marriage or blood ties.
