29. Kate gets into the elevator on the third floor of a shopping mall. She goes up 6 floors to reach the food court. After an hour, she went down 2 floors to buy books. At which floor is she now?​


Problem Involving Integers

Integers are the set of whole numbers and their opposites. Integers are used in real world situations. Positive integers are used to denote the height above the sea level. Negative integers are used to denote the height below the sea level. The integers are often represented as equally spaced points on a number line.

Answer:

7th floor

Solutions:

2. Given: 3rd floor - current floor where Kate is

              6 floors - Kate goes up

              2 floors - Kate goes down

3rd floor + 6 floors = 9th floor

9th floor - 2 floors = 7th floor

Therefore, Kate is currently on the 7th floor.
