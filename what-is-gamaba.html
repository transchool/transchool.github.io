18. what is the significance of GAMABA?


SIGNIFICANCE OF GAMABA

A GAMABA or Gawad sa Manlilikha ng Bayan is the award given to all  traditional folk artists to give honor to their and support to their talents and to assure that  their skills and crafts are preserved. GAMABA is  was institutionalized through Republic Act No. 7355. GAMABA motivates traditional folk artists to share their skills to others so that there will be future predecessor of their skills. GAMABA conducts the quest for the best customary craftsmen of the land, embraces a program that will guarantee the exchange of their abilities to other people, and attempts measures to advance a veritable enthusiasm for and ingrain pride among our kin about the virtuoso of the Manlilikha ng Bayan.

GAMABA ENVISIONEDAs envisioned under R.A. 7355, "Manlilikha ng Bayan" will mean a resident occupied with any customary workmanship extraordinarily Filipino whose particular abilities have arrived at a significant degree of specialized and creative greatness and have been given to and generally rehearsed by the current age in his/her local area with a similar level of specialized and imaginative skill.
