24. the ten digit of a three digit number is one less than its one digit.if the number is a palindrome(read the same forward and backward),what is the smallest number that satisfies such condition?


Answer:

101


Step-by-step explanation:

Define x:

Let the digit in the hundreds place be x

The digit in the ones place is also x (since it is a palindrome)

The digit in the tens place = x - 1


By logical induction:

The smallest digit in the 3-digit number is in the tens place

The smallest possible digit in the tens place must be zero

⇒ x - 1 = 0

⇒ x = 1


Therefore, the number is 101


Answer: 101
