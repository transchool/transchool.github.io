2. "Love has no gender – compassion has no religion – character has no race" explain


Love has no gender: You can still love a person despite of their sexuality/gender.

Compassion has no religion: Being religious doesn't automatically make you compassionate/a good person. People can still be compassionate despite having different beliefs on religion.

Character has no race: Race doesn't affect your character/personality. There shouldn't be any stereotypes of people's personality based on their races.
