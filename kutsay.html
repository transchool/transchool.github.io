24. What are history of futsal?


Answer:

Futsal (also known as fútsal or footsal) is a football game played on a hard court, smaller than a football pitch, and mainly indoors. It has similarities to five-a-side football

Origins. "Futsal" started in 1930 when Juan Carlos Ceriani, a teacher in Montevideo, Uruguay, created a version of indoor football for recreation in YMCAs. This new sport was originally developed for playing on basketball courts and a rule book was published in September 1933

29. discuss the important events happened in the history of futsal and explain briefly the nature on how to play the game futsal​


Answer:

The origin of Futsal (Five-a-Side Soccer) can be traced back to Montevideo, Uruguay, in 1930 when Juan Carlos Ceriani devised a five-a-side version of soccer for youth competion in YMCAs. The game is played on basketball-sized courts, both indoors and out without the use of sidewalls.
