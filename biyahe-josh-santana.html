29. Josh received a package measuring 5 meters length and 10 meters width, what is the area of Josh's package?​


[tex] \purple{ \huge{ \overline{ \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad}}}[/tex]

[tex]{\bold{Question}}[/tex]:Josh received a package measuring 5 meters length and 10 meters width, what is the area of Josh's package?

Step-by-step explanation:

What is asked? The area of Josh's package.

The formula that we are using is A = L × W

[tex] \large{ \blue{ \mathfrak{Solution}}}:[/tex]

[tex] \tt{{A = L × W}} \\ \tt{ {A = (5m)(10m)}} \\ \underline{\boxed{\tt{ \purple{A = 50m² }}}}[/tex]

Therefore, the area measures 50 meters sq.
