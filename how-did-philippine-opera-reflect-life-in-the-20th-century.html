24. how did the philippine opera, ballet and musical plays reflect life in the 20th century?


Philippine Opera, Ballet and Musical Plays

By portraying relative matters, scenarios, and old classics to give tribute to some of the greatest forms of art as perceived by the country.

The Philippine Opera started in the 19th century where there was a rise to musical and performing upgrades. Musical instruments, opera singers and such became prominent. Through portraying various scenarios and performance relating much more on the tragedy side such as:

Romance Deceptive elements Sensitivity and limits of a human Feud/ Blood Feud

It became a well-known pattern of performances in the Philippine Opera. An example of the said old-classics played at the theatre is the Noli Me tangere by Dr. Jose Rizal. We are all too familiar with the two masterpieces of Rizal the El Filibusterismo and the Noli Me Tangere as it was included and taught to us in our school days.  

It contained:

Drama (Constant struggle of the main character) Conflict from the world/ country (From the tyrant government) Romance (Maria Clara and Ibarra) Deceptive elements ( From Crisostomo Ibarra to Simoun)

The Philippine Opera, ballet and musical plays are quite different from the other known theatrical performances as it is much more focused on portraying matters that will appeal much to the Filipino people and these are to give some conflict that needs resolving from relating times.

Most likely it reflects our history and reminds us about our old culture. Since in the 21st century we rely much more on western products of entertainment, we somehow are forgetting the joy of a real live performance rather than from televisions.

It also reflects the simple ways one can have a conflicting manner when living a normal life of a Filipino and the simplest to complicated ways of resolving it using your own efforts. The simple rules and terms, where not many can be as productive as the other one. It doesn’t differ much to our “advanced living” in the 21st century.
