10. The name of the main character in Bucaneg's famous epic.


The correct answer is Lam-ang

The protagonist of the epicSon of Don Juan and Namungan Panganiban

About the epic

The Biag ni Lam-Ang epic illustrates the values and attributes of the Filipinos. The fact that the features, attitudes and values shown in this phenomenal epic are all reflections of our ancestors, especially the Ilocans, who were the source of this fictional piece during the pre-Spanish period. This portrays unwavering courage, justice, affection, passion, marriage, journey, and even friendship. The vastness and optimism of the story was packed with idealistic imagination and fantasy.

About the Author

Pedro Bucaneg

As a poet of the Philippines. Blind has been the author of the Ilocano epic Biag ni Lam-ang (Life of Lam-ang) since birth. He is considered the "Father of Ilocano Literature." A street inside the Philippine Cultural Center (CCP) complex in Pasay City, Philippines is named after him. His nickname is lent to Bukanegan, the Balagtasan equivalent of Ilocano.

Synopsis of Biag ni Lam-Ang

The protagonist of the epic,  in the town of Nalbuan. He is respected and worshiped by the people because of his extraordinary powers. He's got a normal conception. He could speak just after he was born. The phenomenal development of Lam Ang was so awesome. When he was nine months old, he was already mature, both physically and mentally. He also had a very loud voice that he could make a clear claim to the town of Nalbuan. He had an indescribable spirit to conquer the many Igorots alone. Lam Ang's courage was shown to the residents of Calanutian and Nalbuan as he tried to get the shellfish, The fact that it was a difficult task, because many tried to get the precious shellfish, but they failed to do it. Besides all his extraordinary abilities, he also had amazing powers. When it comes to his love life, he tried to do anything for his love, Ines Cannuyan, to prove that he was worthy to marry her. Unfortunately, he was killed by berkakan, an angry shark, but he was brought to life with the help of his wonderful animal friends.

In today’s time we can also see the traits that Lam-ang possesses the love for  family , the selflessness of a person towards his/her family. Our ancestors who are still protecting their cultures and their lands.

De los Reyes and his followers celebrated the Philippine’s first organized demonstration on May 1 in Tondo, Manila’s Plaza Moriones. UOD’s fight for the rights of Filipino workers landed De los Reyes in jail. He was marked as a subversive by the Americans.
