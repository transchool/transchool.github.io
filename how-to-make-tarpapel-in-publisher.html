2. Ano ang ibig sabihin ng tarpapel​


Answer:

Ang tarpapel ay kombinasyon ng dalawang salitang “tarpaulin” at “papel (papel)”. Ito ay isang mas murang alternatibo sa tarpaulin printing. Ang paggamit ng tarpapel ay ipinapayong hindi lamang dahil makakatipid ka, ngunit nakakatulong din sa ating kapaligiran sa pamamagitan ng pagliit sa paggamit ng mga materyales na maaaring maging sanhi ng pagkasira nito. Kaya naman, kung balak mong gumamit ng tarpaulin sa maikling panahon, mas mabuting gumamit ng tarpapel sa halip.

Record the product key provided on the download page for future reference.

Click the "Download Now" button to save the Publisher file to your computer.

Double-click the file to launch the installation.

Type the product key when the installation prompts you. After the installation is complete, you have 60 days to use the trial of Microsoft Publisher on your computer.

Yes, it is getting harder to publish in prestigious journals if you haven’t already

By Viviane CallierDec. 10, 2018 , 3:00 PM

Can you publish in Nature if you haven’t already published in Nature? It may sound like a riddle—but according to a new study, it is in fact getting harder to publish in prestigious multidisciplinary journals such as Nature, Science, and the Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences if you haven’t previously done so. So-called “chaperoned” researchers who first publish in these journals as nonsenior authors have a leg up when it comes to publishing in these journals as principal investigators (PIs), the study found—and the trend has gotten stronger in recent years. In Nature, for example, the share of papers authored by chaperoned senior authors grew from 16% to 22% between 1990 and 2012, while new senior authors dropped from 39% to 31%.
