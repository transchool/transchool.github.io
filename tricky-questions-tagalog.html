TAGALOG RIDDLES

Tagalog Riddles or "Bugtong" are usually difficult, vague and misleading questions that requires great challenge even for extremely intelligent people to decipher and unviel the clever answers.

1.) Tanong: Isang prinsesa, punong-puno ng mata.

Sagot: Pinya

2.) Tanong: Dalawa kong kahon, buksan walang ugong.

Sagot: Ang iyong mga mata.

3.) Tanong: Aling ibon dito sa mundo ang lumilipad at sumususo ang anak?

Sagot: Kabag o paniki ng prutas.

4.) Tanong: Maikling landasin, di maubos lakarin.

Sagot: Anino

5.) Tanong: Nang hatakin ko ang baging, nagkagulo ang mga matsing.

Sagot: Kampana.

5 BEST TRICKY QUESTIONS WITH ANSWERS

Tricky Questions are questions that are usually vague, difficult and usually provides a huge challenge even for intelligent people. These questions requires critical thinking skills, wide understanding, and in depth thinking in order to unveil and get the correct answer.

Answer: The baby, since he is a little Bigger.

2.) Question: What do you call a woman who knows where her husband is all the time?

Answer: A widow

Answer: Because there is one more peso in 1979 pesos than in 1978 pesos.

4.) Questions: How many legs does an crocodile have if you count his tail as a leg?

Answer: Four, because calling the crocodile's tale a leg does not make it one.

13. How do airplanes stay inthe air? (Tricky science question)


[tex]\large \boxed {\gg Answer : }[/tex]

How do airplanes stay in the air? A plane's engines are designed to move it forward at high speed. That makes airflow rapidly over the wings, which throw the air down toward the ground, generating an upward force called lift that overcomes the plane's weight and holds it in the sky. The wings force the air downward and that pushes the plane upward.
