Explanation: A black hole is a singularity into which material flows. The universe is a singularity out of which material has flowed. A black hole is surrounded by an event horizon, a surface inside which we cannot see. The universe is surrounded by a cosmological horizon, a surface outside of which we cannot see. (A crucial difference, though, is that the event horizon is fixed whereas the cosmological horizon varies from observer to observer.)

28. There are 700 toothpicks in a​ regular-sized box. If a jumbo box is made by doubling all the dimensions of the​ regular-sized box, how many toothpicks will the jumbo box​ hold? The jumbo box holds nothing toothpicks.


Step-by-step explanation:

Referring to the problem, there are 700 toothpicks inside the regular box. This means that the volume occupied by the 700 toothpicks would be the volume of the box. Further, it says that a jumbo box is made by doubling the dimensions of the regular box. Say we have x length, y width, and z height of the regular box as its dimensions. Doubling the dimensions would give us 2x length, 2y width, and 2z height as the jumbo box' dimensions

Solving the volume of the regular box, we get xyz, since volume is just length x width x height

Thus, the volume of the jumbo box is (2x)(2y)(2z) = 8xyz

If a volume xyz can contain 700 toothpicks, then the volume 8xyz would contain

(8)(700 toothpicks) = 5600 toothpicks
