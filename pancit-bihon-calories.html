5. how to cook delicious pancit bihon anwer


PANCIT BIHON RECIPE

This is a recipe for Pancit Bihon

Course: noodles Cuisine: filipino recipe Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 40 minutes Total Time: 55 minutes Servings: 8

INGREDIENTS:

1 lb pancit bihon Rice Noodles

1/2 lb. pork cut into small thin slices

1/2 lb. chicken cooked, deboned, and cut into thin slices

1/8 lb. pea pods or snow pea

1 cup carrot

1/2 small cabbage chopped

1 cup celery leaves chopped finely

1 medium sized onion chopped

1/2 tbsp garlic minced

1 pc chicken cube

5 tbsp soy sauce

3 to 4 cups water

INSTRUCTIONS:

In a large pot, Saute the garlic and onionAdd the pork and chicken then let cook for 2 minutesAdd the chicken cube and water then simmer for 15 minutesPut in the carrots, pea pod, cabbage, and celery leaves and simmer for a few minutesRemove all the ingredients in the pot except for the liquid and set them asideIn the pot with the liquid in, add the soy sauce and mix wellAdd the pancit bihon (makes sure to first soak it in water for about 10 minutes) and mix well. Cook until liquid evaporates completelyPut-in the vegetables and meat that were previously cooked and simmer for a minute or twoServe hot. Share and enjoy!

6. What is the procedure in cooking pancit bihon ? ​


Answer:

Ingredients

1 lb Pancit Bihon Rice Noodles

1/2 lb. pork cut into small thin slices

1/2 lb. chicken cooked, deboned, and cut into thin slices

1/8 lb. pea pods or snow pea

1 cup carrot

1/2 small cabbage chopped

1 cup celery leaves chopped finely

1 medium sized onion chopped

1/2 tbsp garlic minced

1 pc chicken cube

5 tbsp soy sauce

3 to 4 cups water

Instructions

In a large pot, Sauté the garlic and onion

Add the pork and chicken then let cook for 2 minutes

Add the chicken cube and water then simmer for 15 minutes

Put in the carrots, pea pod, cabbage, and celery leaves and simmer for a few minutes

Remove all the ingredients in the pot except for the liquid and set them aside

In the pot with the liquid in, add the soy sauce and mix well

Add the pancit bihon (makes sure to first soak it in water for about 10 minutes) and mix well. Cook until liquid evaporates completely

Put-in the vegetables and meat that were previously cooked and simmer for a minute or two

Serve hot. Share and enjoy!

11. pancit bihon Item cost php 250 peso markup: 15% find the percentage Markup? find the selling price?​


The percentage markup is 15%.

To find the selling price, you can use the following formula:

Selling Price = Item Cost + (Item Cost x Markup Percentage)

In this case, the selling price would be:

250 + (250 x 15%) = 250 + 37.5 = 287.5

So the selling price of the pancit bihon is 287.5 pesos.

17. A newly opened restaurant in marikina city gives a free iced tea to every 10th customer and a free pancit bihon to every 12th customer. Which customer will get both the iced tea and the free pancit bihon? How will you solve the problem? A. By finding the prime factors B. By finding the GCF C. By finding the common factors D. By finding the LCM​


Answer:

D. By finding the LCM

Step-by-step explanation:

Find the LCM of 10 and 12

10=10,20,30,40,50,60

12=12,24,36,48,60

Their LCM is 60. The 60th customer will get both the iced tea and the free pancit bihon.
