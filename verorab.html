1. 1. What are common to verbrates? _____________ 2. How will you describe verbrates? _____________


Answer:

1. The major groups of vertebrates include fishes, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals.

2. A vertebrate is an animal with a spinal cord surrounded by cartilage or bone. The word comes from vertebrae, the bones that make up the spine. Animals that are not vertebrates are called invertebrates. Vertebrates include birds, fish, amphibians, reptiles, and mammals.
