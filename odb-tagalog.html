6. ABCE is a rectangle and the sum of ABD, BAD and ADB is 180. Find ADB


ABD+BAD+ADB=180
BAD= 60°
ABD=?
ADB=?

To find ABD,
since that ABC is a right angle so
ABD+CBD=90°
ABD+50°=90°
ABD=90°-50°
ABD= 40°

So let's go back to our general formula which is ABD+BAD+ADB=180°
40°+60°+ADB=180°
100°+ADB=180°
ADB=180°-100°
ADB=80°
So therefore, ADB=80°
