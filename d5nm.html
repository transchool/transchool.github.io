6. the x-ray used by your dentist has a wavelenght of 15nm. what are its frequency and energy?​


Problem:

The x-ray used by your dentist has a wavelenght of 15nm. what are its frequency and energy?​

We will assume in my computation that x-ray is traveling in a vacuum with the speed of  3 × 10⁸  m/s

Note:

15 nanometer = .000000015  or 1.5 × 10-⁸

Given data:

Wavelength ( λ ) =  15 nm

Speed  (c)  =   3 × 10⁸  m/s

Frequency (f) = unknown

Solution:

The formula in finding the frequency is f = c / λ

f = 3 × 10⁸  m/s / 1.5 × 10-⁸ m

 = 2 × 10¹⁶ Hz

Frequency is equal to 2 × 10¹⁶ Hz

To solve for power, the formula is E=hf where h = 6.63 × 10 -³⁴

E = 6.63 × 10 -³⁴ joules /s   ×     2 × 10¹⁶ /s

   =  1.326  × 10 - ¹⁹  joules

Energy is equal to 1.326  × 10 - ¹⁹  joules

11. Find the height of an equilateral trisngle if its are is 35 cm and perimeter of 15 cm? A.20cm B.14cm C11.7cm D5cm


Answer:

B. 14

Step-by-step explanation:

Perimeter of a equilateral triangle = S + S +S

Since perimeter is given as 15cm we can substitute that to the formula above to solve for S :

15 = S + S + S

15 = 3S

divide both sides by 3 then we can get S = 5

Now to solve for the height use the formula for

Area of a triangle = (1/2)(b)(h)

since the area is given as 35 cm and we have found the base or S as 5 cm substitute the values to find the height

[tex]35 = \frac{1}{2} (5)(h)[/tex]

[tex]h = \frac{35}{( \frac{1}{2} )(5) } [/tex]

[tex]h = 14[/tex]
