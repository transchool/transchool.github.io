3. romina mondragon is 0.12 m shorter than daniela mondragon. if daniela is 1.55 m tall, what operation is appropriate to use in getting romina's height? a. addition b. subraction c. multiplication d. division


PROBLEM:

Romina Mondragon is 0.12m shorter than Daniela Mondragon. If Daniela is 1.55m tall, what operation is appropriate to use in getting Romina's height?

a. addition

b. subraction

c. multiplication

d. division

ANSWER:

B. SUBTRACTION

[tex]•[/tex] To get Romina's height, we'll subtract the gap between them to the height of Daniela since Romina is shorter than Daniela.

[tex]1.55m - 0.12m = 1.43m[/tex]

Hence, Romina's height is 1.43m.

Answer:

Rewrite the expression as:

x - 450 = 12 500

Change the sign of 450 and move the terms.

x = 12 500 + 450

x = 12 950

= Marga is 12 950 feet above Renz and Renz is 12 950 feet below Marga.
