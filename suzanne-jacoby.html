25. Roxy walks 8 000 cm while Suzanne walks 90 m in going to school. Who walks farther in going to school? By how far?


We convert 8000 cm to m, note that 100 cm=1 m
[tex]8000cm* \frac{1m}{100cm} =80m[/tex]

Comparing the two values now with the same units we would know that Suzanne walks farther by 90m-80m=10m.First, convert:
[tex]90 m(\frac{100 cm}{1 m}=9,000 m[/tex]

The answers:
Suzanne walks farther to school than Roxy.

To find how far Suzanne traveled than Roxy, subtract:
9,000 cm-8,000 cm=1,000 cm
