If she will only pick one name for tomorrow's recitation, what will be the probability of picking a male pupil? Picking a female pupil?​


Answer:

3/8 Probability of picking a male pupil

5/8 Probability of picking a female pupil

Step-by-step explanation:

There are a total of 40 pupils in Teacher Noemi's class.

There are

- 15C1 or 15 ways to pick 1 from the boys(male)

- 25C1 or 25 ways to pick 1 from the girls(female)

So the probability of getting a male pupil is

15/40 or 3/8

and the probability of getting a female pupil is

25/40 or 5/8
