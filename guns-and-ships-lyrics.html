9. a toy gun cost 10 pesos more than a doll. The total cost of 3 toy guns and 4 dolls was 163 pesos. Find the average cost of a toy gun.


Step-by-step explanation:

Given:

10-difference in the price of two items(a toy gun and a doll)

3-quantity of toy guns

4-quantity of dolls

163-total cost of the products

163÷30(3 toy guns which cost 10 pesos more than the 4 dolls)=133

133÷7(sum of 3 toy guns and 4 dolls) =19

A doll costs 19 pesos each, and the toy gun is 10 pesos more than a doll, so 19+10=29.

A toy gun costs 29 pesos.

What is the M3 Grease Gun?

The M3 submachine gun (officially the United States Submachine Gun) was an American submachine gun that was designed by George Hyde, Frederick Sampson and René Studler in 1942 and produced by the General Motors Guide Lamp Division from 1943 to 1945. It was developed for the US Army during World War II as a cheaper alternative to the expensive Thompson submachine gun. The weapon was commonly termed the grease gun (or similar nicknames) due to its passing resemblance to the actual tool.

24. what will happen the gun powder after firing the gun​


Gunpowder in the cartridge case starts to burn, causing it to change from a solid material to a gas. This change creates pressure within the cartridge, which in turn forces the bullet down the barrel and down range. Pressure building behind the bullet is released when the bullet exits the muzzle of the firearm.
