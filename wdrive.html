8. A race car driver can drive one lap in 8.3 minutes. How long will it take the race car driver to drive 4 laps?​


Answer:

QUESTION:

A race car driver can drive one lap in 8.3 minutes. How long will it take the race car driver to drive 4 laps?

GIVEN:

8.3 minutes

4 laps

SOLUTION:

8.3 × 4

ANSWER:

33.2 minutes

Multiply the time for each lap by the number of laps.

Multiply as you would multiply whole numbers.

It is generally not allowed for a driver to drive on the transition lines that separate the lanes of traffic on a road. Transition lines, also known as lane markings, are used to indicate the separation between lanes of traffic and to guide drivers as they change lanes or make turns.

It is important for drivers to stay within their designated lanes and to avoid driving on or across the transition lines, as this can cause confusion and increase the risk of a collision. In some cases, driving on or across the transition lines may also be considered a traffic violation, which can result in fines and points on your driver's license.

To avoid driving on the transition lines, it is important to pay attention to the road and to maintain a safe distance from other vehicles. It is also a good idea to use your turn signals when changing lanes or making turns, to alert other drivers of your intentions.
