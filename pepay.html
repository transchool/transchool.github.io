had submitted her application letter. In this case, Pepay and the company have Interpersonal communication.

Interpersonal communication

Interpersonal communication occurs when we communicate with another individual in a one-on-one context, which is typically casual and unstructured. This type of communication often involves two parties, though it may involve more.

They are more Dyadic Communication to small group communication. Dyadic Communication refers to the type of interpersonal communication in which two communicators function as sender and receiver.

From the illustration, they have: The sender: PepayThe receiver: The company

And after Pepay submits her application, she's gonna have types of dyadic communication:

ConversationsDialoguesInterviews

Participants in a dyad must possess the following qualities in order to perform their duties and make appropriate decisions:

Effective communication abilities;Understanding how to initiate and conclude a conversation;Being able to react respectfully to a statement;Observing a relationship of give-and-take; andBeing aware of and attentive to the presence of other parties.
