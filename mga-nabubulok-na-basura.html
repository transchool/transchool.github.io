11. halimbawa ng nabubulok at di nabubulok na basura


Answer:

Ang mga halimba ng nabubulok at di nabubulok na basura ay nakalista sa explanation part.

Explanation:

Mga halimba ng nabubulok na basura:

Balat ng prutasButo ng manokTinik ng isdaBahaw na kaninPinagbalatan ng gulayPanis na pagkain

Ang mga nabubulok na basura ay karaniwang nang gagaling sa ating mga tahanan, at sa pamilihan o palengke. Kapag ang mga basurang ito ay naiwan ng matagal na panahon, karaniwan silang nagbibigay ng mabahong amoy o "bulok" na amoy. Maaari silang maging pataba sa lupa kung isa ay ibabaon sa ilalim ng lupa sa loob ng ilang linggo dahil sila ay nag dedegrade.

Mga halimbawa ng di nabubulok na basura:

PlasticPapelMetalLata (tin can)DyaryoBote

Ang mga di nabubulok na basura ay hindi nag dedegrade kagaya ng mga nabubulok na basura. Kapag sila'y naipon sa kanal, maaari silang magdulot ng pagbara nila at magiging sanhi ng pagbaha. Ang mga di nabubulok na basura ay maaaring gamitin muli o i-recycle. Madalas ding binebenta ang mga ito sa junk shop.

23. bakit kailangang paghiwalayin ang mga nabubulok sa hindi nabubulok na mga basura?​


Importance of Waste Segregation

Fruits, herbs, bulbs, grasses, trees, and wood or pencil shavings are examples of biodegradable waste. Paper, plastics, bottles, metal, tetra packs, and aluminum foil are among the non-biodegradable wastes.

What is the significance of waste segregation?

If you do not adequately segregate the waste, it will end up mixed up in landfills, much as it is mixed up in your bin. It would be ideal if they did not contain any potentially toxic gases. These wastes, though, may decompose and can contaminate the environment. As a result, leakage and gas will be released, potentially harming the atmosphere. What if it's full of greenhouse gas? This is very harmful because it would have an effect on climate change and will result in drought. As a result, we must be cautious when sorting waste.  

It is important that we separate our waste in order to protect everyone's wellbeing. We must not solely concentrate on our welfare in this situation. If these wastes are sufficiently separated, rag pickers and garbage collectors will not be exposed to the possibility of cuts or bruises. If such individuals cut their finger, they can become infected. As a result, there would be a risk to one's life. Once the waste is separated, rag collectors and trash collectors can have a better time recycling it. They have no trouble with waste that would be recycled or incinerated.

What role does it play in our community?

One of the most significant reasons for the advancement of sustainable pro-ecological growth is effective waste segregation, recycling, and removal of plastic packaging. Furthermore, it is important to improve recycling quality and reduce hazardous waste discharged into the atmosphere.
