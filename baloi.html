1. If you were one of the Sama Dilaut weavers,what re your inspiration to continue bthe optimistic culture of baloy weaving specially the baloy designs and motif?​


Answer:

My inspiration would be simple — to preserve the cultural identity of the Sama Dilauts. Baloy weaving has been one of the trademarks of this race and it would be an honor to keep those trademarks alive until the next generations. Apart from it is also a good source of livelihood for the natives, baloy weaving is clearly a legacy we could offer for the next generation. To continue making baloy motifs and design is also like introducing a piece of our origin that takes a significant part in our history.
