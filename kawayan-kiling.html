11. B. Salve each problem The price of a kile of gelunggone increased from F73.00 to P85. 60 per kile find the percent of increase​


To find the percent increase, you can use the following formula:

((New Price - Old Price) / Old Price) x 100 = Percent Increase

In this case:

((85.60 - 73.00) / 73.00) x 100 = 17.01% increase

So the percent increase in the price of a kilo of gelunggone is 17.01%.
