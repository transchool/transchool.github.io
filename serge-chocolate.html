Kaya ang ganap na kalayaan ay iginawad lamang sa Pilipinas noong Hulyo 1946.

11. serge traveled a distance of 265 miles he drove at 40 mi/hr during the first part of the trip and at 35 mi/hr during the remaining part . if he made the trip in 7 hours, how long did he travel at 40 mi/hr? with explanation please


Answer:

160 miles

Step-by-step explanation:

given:

distance = 265 miles

rate_1 = 40 mi/hr (1st part of the trip)

rate_2 = 35 mi/hr (2nd part of the trip)

time = 7 hrs

formula:

distance = rate x time

analyzation:

distance_1 = x

distance_2 = y = 265 - x

time_1 = a

time_2 = b = 7 - a

solution:

distance_1 = rate_1 x time_1

x = 40a

distance_2 = rate_2 x time_2

y = 35b

265 - x = 35(7 - a)

265 - x = 245 - 35a

where x = 40a
