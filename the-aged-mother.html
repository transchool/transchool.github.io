5. The sum of the age of a mother and a daughter is 52 years. In five years, three times the daughters age will be equal to the mother's age. Find their present age.​


Step-by-step explanation:

Present age of mother = m

Present age of daughter = d

Sum of the ages

M + D = 52 >>>>>(1)

In five years means we have add 5 in their present ages

M + 5 = d + 5

Then three times the daughters age will be equal to the mother's age

M + 5 = 3(d+5)

M + 5 = 3d + 15

M = 3d + 15 - 5

M = 3d + 10 >>>>> (2)

Put the value of m from (2) to (1)

M + D = 52 >>>>>(1)

(3d+10) + D = 52

3d + 10 + D = 52

4d + 10 = 52

4d = 52-10

4d = 42

4d/4 = 42/4

d = 10.5

Put the value of d in (1)

M + D = 52 >>>>>(1)

M + 10.5 = 52

M = 52-10.5

M = 41.5

Answer:

Present age of mother = 41 and half years

Present age of daughter = 10 and half years

16. vince is twice his sisters age who is 8 years old their mother's age is twice the sum of their ages how old is their mother​


Let's call Vince's age as "V".

His sister's age is 8, so the sum of their ages is 8 + V.

Their mother's age is twice the sum of their ages, so
2 * (8 + V) = 2 * 8 + 2 * V = 16 + 2V.

Since Vince is twice his sister's age,
2 * 8 = V, so V = 16.

Their mother's age is
2 * (8 + 16) = 2 * 24 = 48.

So, their mother is 48 years old.
