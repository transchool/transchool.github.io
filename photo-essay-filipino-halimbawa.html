27. what is a photo essay? what is the steps in creatting a photo essay?​


Answer:

what is a photo essay?

photo essay is a collection or series of pictures or images that are arranged in a specific order to tell the progression of events, emotions and comcepts. The photos must work together to convey the message, theme, and emotions of the essay. Each individual photo must contribute to the overall story.

steps in making a photo essay

think about a specific theme or topic you would likem Make sure that your chosen topic is related or interesting to tell a story choose your subject matter. Check if it is suitable or related to your chosen topic or theme.plan your shoot. List down all the ideas for photo composion and choose the best one that suited to your topic or theme. You can also create an outline of all the you need to consider in taking photograps.start shooting. Make sure you have a large variety of photos to choose from. In taking photograps, you need to apply the different principles and elements of art to come up with good pictures.choose all your best shots. Make sure that your photo are connected visually to each other.finnaly, add captions to each picture. Captions are brief explanations about your photos.
