1. What is rangoli? - Rangoli is an Indian art or hindu form in which designs are produced on the floor or tables with materials including powdered lime stone, red ochre, dried rice flour, coloured sand, quartz powder, flower petals, and colored pebbles. It is a common occurrence in Hindu families, although the colors are favored during festivals and other significant occasions due to the time commitment. Rangoli is a Hindu celebration in India that is celebrated during Diwali, Tihar, Onam, Pongal, Sankranthi, and other Hindu holidays.
