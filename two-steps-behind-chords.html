Answer:

These are called slash chords. For instance, C/F indicates that an C major triad should be played with an added F in the bass. In some genres of modern jazz, two chords with a slash between them may indicate an advanced chord type called a polychord, which is the playing of two chords simultaneously.


21. Theorem on two intersecting chord equation


Answer:

The intersecting chords theorem or just the chord theorem is a statement in elementary geometry that describes a relation of the four line segments created by two intersecting chords within a circle. It states that the products of the lengths of the line segments on each chord are equal.

29. 4. Chord-Distance to Centre TheoremTwo congruent chords in a circle are equidistant from the centre of the circle.​


Answer:

In the same circle, or in congruent circles, two chords are congruent if and only if they are equidistant from the center. “q → p” If two chords are equidistant from the center of a circle in the same circle or congruent circles, then the chords are congruent.


30. 3. two chords that are not diameters​


Answer:

If in a circle two chords (which are not diameters) cut one another, then they do not bisect one another. In the words of Euclid: If in a circle two straight lines cut one another which are not through the centre, they do not bisect one another.
