SAMPLE APPLICATION LETTER of DESO TECHNICAL SUPPORT STAFF FOR COMELEC​

Mr. Juan Dela Cruz

Office of the Election Officer

Commission on Election

Sta. Mesa Manila

Dear Mr. Dela Cruz,

Greetings!

I am Juana Santos, 23 years old. I have read your job hiring post from the internet. I am interested to be one of your DepEd Supervising Official (DESO) Technical Support Staff.

I am a degree-holder of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. I worked with Telecommunications Company for two years. I am a hardworking individual and very much willing to be trained.

Attached is a copy of my resume and Duly Accomplished Personal Data Sheet (PDS) as per your request.

Hoping to working with you soon.

Sincerely,

Juana Santos

Explanation:

During the job application process, an application letter, often known as a "cover letter," is provided with your Curriculum Vitae or resume. A letter gives your application more personality by detailing your background and enthusiasm in the position, whereas a resume emphasizes your professional skills and expertise.

A cover letter is a one-page document that you send to a potential employer to show your interest in a job opening. The cover letter for a job application explains who you are as a professional and as a person. The letter should emphasize your accomplishments and skills in order to catch the attention of the hiring manager or recruiter who is assessing applications. This letter, when written effectively, explains why the reader should invite you in for an interview and highlights the important qualifications that qualify you for the position.

A job application letter can help you stand out from the crowd and impress a potential employer. You may also wish to demonstrate your familiarity with the organization in your cover letter. You can discuss how your professional aims and goals connect with the company's objectives. It's critical to highlight characteristics of your personality in your job application letter.

29. Application letter sample for job


An application letter is also called cover letter, being your first introduction it is of great importance and should represent you in a best way, giving your appropriate picture. The first paragraph of the application letter should be short and to the point, explaining what is the reason of this application, if you are applying for a particular job. Start your application letter with a brief introduction of your personal and academic qualification, then mention your professional qualifications and create a link of all this with the required job post.

•introduce yourself.

•mention the job (or kind of job) you're applying for (or looking for)

•show that your skills and experience match the skills and experience needed to do the job.

•encourage the reader to read your resume.

• finish with a call to action (for example, asking for an interview or a meeting).
