3. ang target games ay¿?​


Example: Tumbang preso

Answer:

A TARGET GAMES is a games that involves sending objects toward a subject. In target games, a player either throws, slides, or strikes an object with the goal of hitting the "bull's eye" or the center of a shooting target. Examples of target games are golf, archery, bowling, dart.

Explanation:

Playing target games develops an individual's aim and accuracy, and promotes teamwork among the players.
