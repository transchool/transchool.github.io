19. What condition was requested by chunhyang's mother before she consented to the marriage offered by Yi Mong Yong?


What condition was requested by Ch'unhyang's mother before she consented to the marriage offered by Yi Mong Yong?

"If you give us a secret marriage contract, writing your pledge not to desert her, we shall be contented."

Ch'unhyang's mother was glad to welcome Yi Mong Yong's desire to marry her daughter. But because Yi Mong Yong is a son of a nobleman and Ch'unhyang is a daughter of a kisaeng, there cannot be a formal marriage. Instead, she asked for him to marry her daughter privately but she made sure that despite the secrecy of their marriage, Yi Mong Yong will stay true with his love towards Ch'unhyang and so she asked him to sign a pledge and a promise.
