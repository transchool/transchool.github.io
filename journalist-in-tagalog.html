The similarities of Informative, Journalistic and Literary Writing is their purpose which is:

1.)to inform

2.)to raise awareness

3.)to educate

Other than that, they also have the same goals like:

1.)to write clear, concise and colorful writing

2.)to do so write simply but not dull and

3.)to use descriptive words only when they have impact

4.)to maintain balance in each issue(pro and con views)

5.)to strive for a thorough representation of all

This simply put, means that journalism must be socially responsible, serve the people with devotion, and educate them while avoiding sensationalism, distortion and manipulation of facts in the news reports, and not compromise with ethical standards of journalism for profit.
