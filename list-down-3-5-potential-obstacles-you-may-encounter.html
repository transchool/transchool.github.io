Lack of Skill Exposure

The ratio of Senior High School student trainees and tasks available is limited at work immersion. Also, the task given to immersion students is very basic that they will not allow them to learn the skill more.

Attendance

Understand communication style

It is important for you to understand the communication style of the people in your work environment, but sometimes this is not easy. So it takes time to understand someone's communication style. Basically there are 4 communication styles that you should know, which are as follows:

Passive

People with this style usually look ignorant of something. They usually often fail to express feelings or needs.

Aggressive

It's easy enough to see the intent of someone who is communicating aggressively. However, they often issue orders, ask questions harshly and fail to listen to others.

Passive-Aggressive

The passive-aggressive communication style is quite difficult to understand. Because usually people with this communication style appear passive on the surface, but inside they are very active.

Assertive

People with this communication style are criteria for co-workers that you can work well with. Communicating with them will be easier. Because the assertive communication style can express their ideas and feelings well, without sidestepping the interests of others.

Convey the message clearly

This can make the message conveyed well received by your colleagues. If you feel you can't communicate well or are usually in a hurry, try to calm down first. This can be done when listening to what the other person is saying.
