16. She was described by rizal as gay, talkative, witty, virginal, attractive, alluring and rizal fell in love with his first romance.


Answer:

Segunda Katigbak– Rizal’s teenage crush.

She was a comely 14-yearold Batangueña from Lipa City. She was also known as “Painsana” but Rizal referred to her in his diary as “K”. She was described by Rizal as gay, talkative, witty, virginal, attractive, alluring, and beguiling. At 16, Rizal fell in love with his “first romance”. Segunda was a boarding student at the Colegio de la Concordia where his sister Olympia and his future fiancée Leonor Rivera also studied. Rizal found out later that Segunda was engaged to be married to her townmate, Manuel Luz.2
