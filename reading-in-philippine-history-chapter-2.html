19. It took Evangeline 1 hour to read 4 2/3 chapters of a novel.At this rate,how many chapters can she read in 3 hours?


1 hour = 4 2/3 chapters
3 hours = ?


Transform 4 2/3 to an improper fraction which is 14/3.
[tex]4 \frac{2}{3} = \frac{14}{3} [/tex]
Then multiply 14/3 and 3 hours.
[tex] \frac{14}{3} \times \frac{3}{1} [/tex]
[tex] = \frac{14 \times 3}{3 \times 1} [/tex]
[tex] = \frac{42}{3} [/tex]
Then simplify by dividing the numerator and the denominator by 3.
[tex] = \frac{42 \div 3}{3 \div 3} [/tex]
The answer is
[tex] = 14[/tex]
3 hours = 14 chapters of a novel

She can read 14 chapters in 3 hours.
