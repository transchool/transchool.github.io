General qualifications

A citizen of the Philippines A person of good moral character Must have passed phychiatricpsycological, physical, mental and dental and drug tests to be administered by the PNP Health ealth Service and Crime Lab Group or by any Napolcom accredited government hospital for the purpose of determining the appointees physical and mental health. Must possess a formal baccalaureate degree from a recognizer learning institution. Must be eligible by accordance with the standards set by the commission. Must not have dishonora lyn discharged from military appointment or an AWOL or dropped from ROLLS from the PNP service or dismissed for cause from any civilian position in the Government. Must have no pending criminal case in any court including the office of the umbudsman or administrative case if he/she is already an employee of the government. Must not have been convicted by final judgement of an offence or crime involving moral turpitude. Must be atleast one meter and sixty-two centimeters in height for male and one meter and fifty-seven centimeters for female. Must weight not more or less 5.0 kg. from the standard weight corresponding to his/her height, age and sex. Must not be less than twenty-one nor more than thirty years of age.

Parola Park

HomeThings to DoHistory & HeritageParola Park

Also known as Presing Park named after the wife of former Mayor Loreto Urieta during his term. This Mountain Park was served as the setting of a historic watchtower and cannon built in 1861 against Muslim pirates and intruders ho used to raid the area. Today, a lighthouse and an old cannon could still be found there. The park offers the best sunset view in town.

25. kailan itinayo ang unang parola sa pilipinas na bunga ng pasig river​


Answer:

The Pasig River Light was the first light station in the Philippines when it was established in 1642.

Answer:

The Pasig River Light was the first light station in the Philippines when it was established in 1642. The first lighthouse tower in the country was erected in the station and first lit in 1846. It was then located on the north jetty at the mouth of Pasig River in San Nicolas, Manila, marking the entrance of the river for vessels cruising Manila Bay, looking to dock on the inland Port of Manila that was then located along the banks of the Pasig River in Binondo and Intramuros.
