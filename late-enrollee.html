19. 3. the total number of enrollees in NFE classes in the district of concepcion in 1495. five years ago the total numbers of enrollees was 1215. how much was the increase in the number of enrollees in five years​


Answer:

To calculate the increase in the number of enrollees in NFE classes in the district of Concepcion over the past five years, you can subtract the number of enrollees from five years ago (1215) from the current number of enrollees (1495):

Increase in enrollees = Current number of enrollees - Number of enrollees five years ago

Increase in enrollees = 1495 - 1215

Increase in enrollees = 280

Therefore, the increase in the number of enrollees in NFE classes in the district of Concepcion over the past five years is 280.
