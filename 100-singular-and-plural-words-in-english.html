9. Which of these words are singular?which are plural


Answer:

Singular means only one. Plural means more than one.

Answer:

Singular words are words that pertain to only one subject. It can be a pronoun, noun or verb. Examples of singular words are cupcake (noun), she (pronoun), and eats (verb).

Sentence: She eats the cupcake.

Plural words are words that refer to two or more subjects. It can also be a pronoun, noun, or verb. Examples of plural words are cupcakes (noun), we (pronoun), and eat (verb).

Sentence: We ate cupcakes last night.

Take note, there are singular words that are written like a plural word. For example: Mathematics, Physics, rabies, checkers, etc.

21. when we use the subject with words neither,either,nor,or or it may take a singular or plural verb when are we to use the singular or plural form of the verb?​


Explanation:

If the pairings "either/or" or "neither/nor" form part of the subject and at least one of the elements is plural, then the verb must be plural too. For example: Neither the lawyer nor the detectives are able to follow the sequence of events. (Here, "lawyer" is singular, but "detectives" is plural.

Answer:
