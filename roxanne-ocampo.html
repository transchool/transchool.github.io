22. Albert is 4 times as old as Roxanne. After 5 years, Albert will be three times as old as Roxanne. Find the present age of Albert.


Answer:

Albert's age now = 40 years old

Step-by-step explanation:

Let x = age of Rox now, so:

4x = age of Al now, then:

5 years from now:

x+5 = age of Rox

4x+5 = age of of Al:

Solve for x:

(4x+5) / (x+5) = 3

(4x+5) = 3(x+5)

(4x+5) = (3x+15)

(4x-3x) = (15-5)

x = 10

Age of Al now = 4x = 4(10) = 40

Checking:

After 5 years:

Age of Rox = 10+5 = 15

Age of Al = 40+5 = 45, which will be 3 times as old as Rox (45/15=3)
