Backstabbing FriendsBackstabbing friends are a different breed of scheming schemer. It's difficult to see how a friend can benefit from backstabbing. She, like a coworker, believes she can gain an advantage in life. It could be by evicting a mutual friend who is closer to you. A friend who tells lies in order to gain an advantage wants to ruin your reputation. Worse, a friend who reveals a secret to others, exposing you to all manner of finger pointing and gossip.When a friend backstabs you, the pain can be excruciating. This is someone you trusted and thought had your best interests in mind, just as you did hers. Discovering a friend's dark side necessitates a fresh look at the friendship.

How to deal with a backstabber?

When dealing with backstabbers, it's critical to maintain your positive self-image and avoid becoming a backstabber yourself. Examine the situation thoroughly. Don't overreact or react too quickly without first considering your options. Take the time to write down what the person is saying as well as what you did.
