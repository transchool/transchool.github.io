10. sino ang Sumulat ng tula na nilapat sa Pambansang awit bilang lyrics nito?​


Answer:

JOSè PALMA

Explanation:

José Palma y Velásquez (Spanish pronunciation: [xoˈse ˈpalma]; June 3, 1876 – February 12, 1903) was a Filipino poet and soldier. He was on the staff of La independencia at the time he wrote "Filipinas", a patriotic poem in Spanish. It was published for the first time in the issue of the first anniversary of La independencia on September 3, 1899. The poem fit the instrumental tune "Marcha Nacional Filipina" by Julián Felipe, and it has since been the basis for every translation of the Philippine National Anthem.

26. ano ang masasabi mo sa paksang ito? Pagbago sa titik o lyrics ng ating pambansang awit


Ang masasabi ko sa paksang iyan ay hindi ako sang-ayon sa pagbago sa titik o liriko ng ating pambansang awit dahil sinisimbolo o sinasalamin nito ang tunay na Pilipinas, ang mga paghihirap ng mga ating ninuno para makamit ang kapayapaan.
