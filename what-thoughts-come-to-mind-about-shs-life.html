25. What thoughts come to mind about SHS life? What feeling does it give you? How do you intend to prepare yourself for that? Pa BRAINLEST KO Kong sino yong MAKASAGOT ng tama


Explanation:

What thoughts come to my mind about Senior High School life?

These are the considerations that come across my mind when thinking about a Senior High School life :

What is the Senior High School Track and strand are best for my abilities and interest?

Among schools offering Senior High School program, which is the best that gives quality education?

How can I avail the Senior High School voucher program of the government?

Are the subjects in Senior High School difficult for me?

What feeling does it give you?  

All the considerations mentioned above give me a feeling of anxiety, excitement, and nervousness. Senior High School is a new chapter in my life as well as it covers the last two years in my high school life. At present, finishing in Senior High School is an important requirement for college admissions. Choosing the right track and strand should be carefully considered. I am also excited to take Senior High School because I can improve communication and social skills, meet new peers, and explore possibilities after graduation both local and international.

How do you prepare yourself for that?

I intend to prepare myself for my Senior High school life by doing the following:

Take an aptitude test in Junior High School to assess my capability.

Ask several schools offering Senior High School about their curricular offerings, take an entrance examination, and if possible visit for a campus tour.

Make inquiries about how to avail of the Senior High School voucher program from DepEd and Private Education Assistance Committee (PEAC).

Interview a friend or relative studying in Senior High School to have an overview of the subjects and experiences they have.
