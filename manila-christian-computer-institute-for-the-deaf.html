23. Lending institutions use what is called the _________ to compute the interest owed when a loan is paid off early.


Lending institutions use what is called the interest rate to compute the interest owed when a loan is paid off early.

Explanation :

Interest rate is one of the important elements in the national economy. The interest rate set when a bank makes a loan to a customer. This interest rate also applies when customers save their money in banks and are also used by lending institutions.

In general, the interest rate is one of the economic variables that is often monitored by economic actors. Interest rates are seen as having a direct impact on economic conditions. Various decisions regarding consumption, saving and investment are closely related to the condition of the interest rate.

The effective interest rate is calculated using the simple formula: r = (1 + i/n)^n - 1. Calculate the effective interest rate using the formula above.

For example, say a loan with a nominal interest rate of 5% compounded monthly. Using the formula, we would get: r = (1 + 0.05/12)^12 - 1, or r = 5.12%.
