11. advantages and disadvantages of local grocery store​


ANSWER:Few advantages that can be listed for grocery store are as follow:

Easy To Reach: Grocery store are located in an area that are within the reach of the customers.

Economic Boost: Stable revenue system causes the grocery store to provide more jobs and opportunities to engage in the earning activities.

Cost of the Store: It doesn't cost too much for opening a grocery store.

Franchise Availability: People have earned a lot by taking a franchise from various company. Also, there are lot of opportunities available in the groceries where you can take franchise.

there are disadvantages of grocery store as well. Few of them are listed below:

Long Operational Hours: Long operational hours are generally required to run a grocery store which is quite challenge able for the one who are owned and operated.

Theft Risk: Often the store are at a great risk of the theft. Also, we have seen many times the thief has looted the ATM's and have damaged their buildings as well. So, there should be some strong security system available for the grocery store.

Maintenance: Large grocery requires a lot of cleaning and maintenance and much efforts are needed from the worker side as well.

Selling Point: Most of the time grocery store sells what people need then what people want. If someone is trying to break a bad habit, then store recommendation is normal for that person.
