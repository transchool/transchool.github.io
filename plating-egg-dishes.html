Ingredients

1 x 240g can SPAM® Classic; cubed

2 fresh chives; chopped if desired

4 large eggs

60mL milk

Directions

•In bowl, whisk together eggs and milk.

•In greased skillet, cook egg mixture over medium-high heat, pulling with spatula to allow liquid to contact skillet surface, until desired doneness.

•Add SPAM® Classic to skillet. Reduce heat; gently stir mixture until SPAM® Classic is heated thoroughly. Garnish with chives.

22. what is the importance of plating and presenting egg dishes or other food products?​


Answer:

Food plating is the process of arranging and decorating food to enhance its presentation. Improving the presentation of a dish adds value to the dining experience, and provides room for a higher mark-up on your food. People eat with their eyes, and creative and thoughtful plating enhances both the look and taste of your food. Focusing on presentation also allows chefs to showcase their creations and demonstrate to guests that they're getting their money's worth.
