2. what are the proper storage of sandwiches?​


Answer:

Keep refrigerated; wrap sandwiches tightly with aluminum foil or plastic wrap or place in plastic bag or airtight sandwich container. For best results when freezing, coat bread completely with a layer of butter or margarine before adding filling; this will help prevent soggy bread when thawed. You should therefore store sandwiches and filled rolls below 80 c and display them in a chilled cabinet.
