29. Which among the following statements is an example of a subsidy? a. When Dole Philippines Inc. follows export rules and restrictions made by the World Trade Organization. b. When Dole Philippines Inc. reports a larger company for attempting a hostile takeover and seeks help from the SEC. C. When Dole Philippines Inc. receives direct money from the government so as to encourage their production of certain goods. d. When Dole Philippines Inc. benefits from a new great tax plan from the government which decreases taxes for large businesses by 10%. e. When Dole Philippines Inc. reports to the government that there is a cartel being formed on the domestic market and requests government intervention.​


Answer:

C. When Dole Philippines Inc. receives direct money from the government so as to encourage their production of certain goods.

Explanation:

A subsidy is a financial assistance or support provided by the government to a business or industry, usually in the form of a direct payment or reduction in taxes. Subsidies are often used as a way to encourage the production of certain goods or services, or to support industries that are considered important to the economy.
