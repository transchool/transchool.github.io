Panagbenga Festival ( transl. Flower Festival) is a month-long annual flower occasion in Baguio. ... The festival also includes street dancing, presented by dancers clad in flower-inspired costumes, that is inspired by the Bendian, an Ibaloi dance of celebration that came from the Cordilleras.

An ode to Baguio's stunning flora, the Panagbenga flower festival is truly a sight to behold.
