Why is it important to understand and follow the basics of first aid?​

Knowledge of first aid promotes a healthy, secure and a safer environment, and instills confidence amongst people, their families, their colleagues and associates. Basic first aid knowledge is very helpful in dealing with trauma situations

It is important to understand since First Aid is an essential knowledge not many carries everywhere. We can never know what will happen to us or to other people, and if in some way an injury happens to us or to other people in our presence then we should find a way to help that person.

Having a basic first aid knowledge means that you'll be confident in your skills and abilities in relation to first aid administration. ... Having this understanding will boost your confidence in a wide range of non-medical day to day situations.

First Aid training gives you the confidence to step in to help when an emergency occurs. Responding straight away, the action you take could save a life or prevent a person suffering permanent disability. Identifying hazards is another benefit of having a knowledge of First Aid.

First aid also equips you to deal with bleeding, burns, breathlessness, bites, shocks stings, splints and fainting so whatever medical situation life throws at you, you can respond effectively.
