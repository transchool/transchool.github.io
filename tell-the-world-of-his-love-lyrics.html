THE MAN WHO CAN'T BE MOVED

Guide Questions:

1. Where did the action happen?

2. When did the action happen?

3. Who is the persona?

4. What are the actions done by the persona?

5. What is the reason behind his actions?

===========

The action happened in a busy city, specifically in a corner where the persona and the other he loves met.The action happened after the breaking up incident between the two pair. The persona continued to stay in that corner in days and cold nights.The persona is the man who is called to be "The Man who can't be Moved". It is the role the singer adopted in order to show what are his actions during the time he stays in the corner.The actions done by the persona is staying in the corner and not leaving the place, camping in a sleeping bag, and asking the people who passes by about the girl he loves.The reason behind his actions is love. The persona wanted to stay in the corner and have the determination to wait because he is hoping that one day the girl comes to that place. The reason is because he is still in love with the girl he met at that corner.
