3. based on the lesson, how important are the aquatic recreational facilities to your family and the community as a whole?


The Importance of Aquatic Recreational Facilities  

Aquatic recreational facilities refers to the facilities where an individual or a community can do aquatic activities such as swimming. And based on the lesson, aquatic recreational facilities pose various importance not only to the health of a person but also to the community as a whole.


Here are some of the importance of aquatic recreational facilities:

DEVELOPMENT OF AQUATIC SKILLS - Every individual needs to develop and hone their skills. Those individuals who are into the development of their aquatic skills will be the ones who will benefit from these aquatic recreational activities. On the other hand,  those who just want to have an exciting recreational activity will also benefit.

FOR A HEALTH LIFESTYLE - Aquatic activities are really good for someone's health. Thus, if there is are aquatic recreational facilities, members of the community will be able to constantly do aquatic activities beneficial to achieving a perfect healthy lifestyle.

OPENS UP OPPORTUNITY - Aquatic recreational facilities open up job opportunities for local people or for the members of the community.

In conclusion, it is highly encouraged that a community especially those in strategic locations will have an aquatic recreational facility in order to bring positive results to the members of the community and to the community as a whole.
