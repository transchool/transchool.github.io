22. a bronze xylophone consist of 10-14 tuned metal bars is called​


Answer:

Gendèr

Explanation:

A gendèr is a type of metallophone used in Balinese and Javanese gamelan music. It consists of 10 to 14 tuned metal bars suspended over a tuned resonator of bamboo or metal, which are tapped with a mallet made of wooden disks (Bali) or a padded wooden disk (Java).

30. Finger cymbals that are made of thick and heavy bronze


Answer: Chhing finger cymbals

Explanation:

Made of thick bronze, Chhing finger cymbals are bowl-shaped, and about two inches in diameter. The player strikes them together, one in each hand. For the closed sound, chhepp, the player damps them. For the open sound, chhing, the player lets them ring.
