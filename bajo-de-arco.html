5. Classify the following instruments. (idiophone,membrophone,chordophone,Aerophone) Guitar Gong Kudyapi Bajo de Arco Bungkaka


Answer:

Guitar - Chordophone

Gong - Membrophone

Kudyapi - Chordophone

Bajo de Arco - Chordophone

Bungkaka - Idiophone

Answer:

1. Guitar - chordophone

2. Gong - idiophone

3. Kudyapi - chordophone

4. Bajo de Arco -  chordophone

5. Bungkaka - idiophone

Explanation:

Guitar is a stringed instruments and it is played by either plucking or strumming. It is a chordophone.

Gong is a circular metal plate-like percussion instrument with or without a knob or boss at the center. It is played by way of striking it with a mallet or with the bare palms. It is an idiophone.

Kudyapi is a Philippine two-stringed, fretted boat-lute. It is four to six feet long with nine frets made of hardened beeswax. It is played by plucking of strumming its strings. It is a chordophone.

Bajo de Arco is the biggest among the string family. It is a chordophone.

Bungkaka is an instrument of the ethnolinguistic groups of the Philippines . It is made from bamboo with a slit. It is also called bamboo buzzer. The instrument is played by striking the edge of the slit of the bamboo with the palm of the player and let it buzz or vibrate. It is an idiophone.
