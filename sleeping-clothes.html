16. Why is it discouraged to have more pillows, fluffy blankets, and other cloth items on the crib while the baby sleeps?​


It is discouraged to have more pillows, fluffy blankets, and other cloth items on the crib while the baby sleeps because it might cause suffocation to the baby. It is recommended to use a firm, flat crib mattress, and to place a sleeping bag on the floor to protect the baby from suffocation.
